Hands-On Training: Developing Multiplatform Mobile Apps On Adobe AIR

duminică, 7 august 2011

Mihai Corlan , Adobe Developer Evangelist, will conduct a 3 hours hands-on training opened only for Timisoara Mobile Development Group members on Adobe platform usage for development of multi/cross-platform mobile apps, capable to run on Android, iOS or Blackberry PlayBook.

The training is mostly addressed to beginners, and no previous Flex or ActionScript are required.

The formal part of the event will be hosted by Timisoara Software Business Incubator, Sunday August 7th, between 15.00 and 18.00. Afterwards, participants will be invited to freely socialize and approach tech issues together with Mihai, over a beer, in some geeky place in Timisoara City Center.

In order to get the most possible from this training, the participants are requested to install the Flash Builder 4.5 prior to the event. It can be downloaded from http://www.adobe.com/ ... with a 60 days trial period. Additionally, students can get it totally free from the same location :).

Those that cannot attend this Sunday training but would like to  learn more about the subject, are invited to join the 8th Timisoara Mobile Development Group event Monday, August 8th, 18:30.
