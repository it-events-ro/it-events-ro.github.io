Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup #6

luni, 20 iunie 2011

Due to a cluster of reasons we are (quite late, sorry) announcing the re-scheduling of the #TiMo #6 event for Monday, June 20th, same time (18:30), same place (City Business Center, C Building).

This edition will include two presentations:

An Android, mobile, augmented reality application developed over the Layar platform by the Timisoara-based company Realmedia, owner of www.imobiliare.ro. The application was recently launched and is briefly presented on www.androider.ro.

The second presentation to be included in the program will be a mobile e-health bunch of Windows Mobile 7 apps, developed by a trio of "Politehnica" University of Timisoara students led by Horia Jurcut.

In the end of the program, Radu Ticiu will briefly share his recent and positive experiences in Helsinki with the Mobile Monday movement. A Skype intervention from a key person from Mobile Monday is under arrangement, too.

You are all invited to continue the mobile-focused discussions after the formal part of the meetup and have a beer or a coffee at 700 Coffee & Lounge.
