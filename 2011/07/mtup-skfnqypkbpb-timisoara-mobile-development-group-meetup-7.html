Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup #7

luni, 11 iulie 2011

The July edition of the #TiMo meetings is getting closer. We will welcome you next Monday with a program containing presentations of two new mobile apps launched this week by fellow Timisoara programmers and a discussion on the post launch evolution of the promising Instamap.

http://photos3.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/6/0/3/7/event_37944631.jpegThe first app to be presented is Monitive , a (currently only) Android-based web site monitoring tool, developed by Lucian Daniliuc. Monitive permits users to have a mobile access to detailed stats for each monitored service, including uptime, response times, incidents and last checks.

The second presentation will bring insights on Adi Cuzman 's process of development of Falling Marbles , an Android game with nice usage of physics engine and on his choices on different stores selling approach.

http://photos3.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/6/7/1/7/event_37946391.jpegIn the end of the formal part of the event we'll find out from Raul Andrisan about Instamap 's last month's evolution in technological, sales and PR terms.
