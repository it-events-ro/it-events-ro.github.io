Learn all about Startupbootcamp with Alex Farcet

marți, 5 iulie 2011

Startupbootcamp is a European startup accelerator network, affiliated with Techstars from US. Take the opportunity to find out what Startupbootcamp is really all about, directly from the source. Live video streaming from Copenhagen/Denmark to Bucharest Hubb, with Alex Farcet , the Startupbootcamp big daddy.


Co-founder at Startupbootcamp, organizer, recruiter, fundraiser and facilitator at StartupWeekend, Alex is going to let us in with many details about what a startup accelerator is, what it can do for you and why you should apply to such an amazing experience.


After Alex’s speech, all of us will be invited to participate to a “Questions and Answers” round, where you can find the answers to all the questions that have been bothering you for a while and that no one has answered yet.


Ciprian Borodescu, Co-Founder at Webcrumbz will host this event, as an encouragement for participation, since he is the proud survivor of such a business accelerator.


Content & Schedule of the event:
18.30 - 19.00 - Welcoming of the attendees/Check-in


19.00 - 19.05 - Presentation of the speaker, introductions, a small word from CiprianBorodescu, Co-Founder at Webcrumbz


19.05 - 19.50 – Videoconference and Q&A with Alex Farcet about the importance of abusiness accelerator like Startupbootcamp.


We are looking forward to meeting all of you!

Free access for Cool and Mini Cool Members of Bucharest Hubb.
