Meeting Rada Mihalcea, Professor, North Texas Univ., Natural Language Processing

marți, 5 iulie 2011

Rada Mihalcea, Associate Professor of Computer Science, Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, University of North Texas will join our meetup and tell us about her research experience in Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Information Retrieval.

http://www.cse.unt.edu/~rada/research.html

This Meetup is organized by the Semantic Meetup Group in Cluj at BT Cafe, str. Baritiu nr. 1, Cluj-Napoca and will be broadcasted live in Targu Mures, Petru Maior University, Room L25.

Interactive Q&A support will be provided.

Link: http://www.meetup.com/Targu-Mures-Semantic-Web/events/21799331/
