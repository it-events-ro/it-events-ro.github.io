"Mobile Industry - The Future Is Here" Conference within TiMo Joins MoMo Events

luni, 14 noiembrie 2011

This mid November, Timisoara </a><a href="http://www.meetup.com/TiMoDev">Mobile </a><a href="http://www.meetup.com/TiMoDev">Development </a><a href="http://www.meetup.com/TiMoDev">Group re-invents itself by joining MobileMonday ™, an open community platform of mobile industry visionaries, developers and influential individuals fostering brand neutral cooperation and cross-border peer-to-peer business opportunities through live networking events to demo products, share ideas and discuss trends from both local and global markets.

Mobile Monday Timisoara inaugural day will be marked by a conference focused on technical and business topics covered by foreign and Romanian specialists. The conference will be opened to interested participants from Timisoara and neighboring regions in Romania, Serbia or Hungary.

The preliminary list of speakers and themes of the conferece (more entries will be possible added) is as follows:

Philipp Kandall , Skobbler , Cluj-Napoca, RO, Mobile Navigation

Patrick Bosteels , TCS </a><a href="http://www.tcsdigitalworld.com/">Digital </a><a href="http://www.tcsdigitalworld.com/">World , Bruxelles, BE, Proximity Mobile Business

Dan Romescu , Augmentise , Berlin, DE, Business Models with Augmented Reality

Antonio Eram , MobilePay.com ,  Bucharest, RO, G et out more from mobile payments. mobilPay

Roman Savvin , Kyivstar , Kiev, UA, Facts and Figures on Mobile Business

Claudiu Suma , ETA - AI , Timisoara, RO, Mobile Business Vertical

Dani Barbulescu , Alcatel - Lucent , Timisoara, RO, Mobilize Home Media Sharing

Tom Desmet , Kooaba , Zurich, CH, Mobile Visual Search

For registering for the conference please use this Amiando page . Please note that there is a 90 RON participation fee for attending the conference and the party following. The participation fee will be collected at entrance, where you'll have the invoice and the receipt issued.

The conference will be followed by a formal official launching of Mobile Monday Timisoara, that will include the opening of a poster exhibition: "Being Mobile in Timisoara" and a live Internet connection with Mobile Monday Germany Congress.

The end of the day will be dedicated to a party in one of the Timisoara clubs to be announced soon.
