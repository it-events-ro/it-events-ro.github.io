Go USA!Roadshow How to Succeed in Business in the USA as a Romanian Entrepreneur

luni, 21 martie 2011

Because we know there is a lot of brilliant Romanian entrepreneurs willing to conquer the world, that is move to America and do business there, ladies and gentlemen, we present to you Go USA! How to Succeed in Business in the USA as a Romanian Entrepreneur?

The GO USA! is one of the Romanian American Chamber of Commerce most important trade and networking events, providing an effective platform for trade and commerce between the U.S. and Romania. This event enables small, medium, and large Romanian companies, as well as individual entrepreneurs, to connect and develop new business in the United States.

And it will take place, right next to you, at Bucharest Hubb.

This half-day event is packed with information you need to understand and overcome your US business growth obstacles. Been in business for years? Or just starting out? No matter where you are in your business life cycle, innovative strategies to penetrate the US market are always needed. Learn from an array of American experts who understand the challenges you face. With expert panels, roundtables and presentations as diverse as your business needs, you receive immediate solutions to establish a successful US business. Don’t waste time making the same mistakes – listen as other Romanian entrepreneurs share their stories and learn directly from them.

Not sure what you need? Ask all your questions here. The only bad question is the one you don’t ask! Develop a new network of resources to call on next week or next year when you need more answers.

Who should attends?

• Aspiring entrepreneurs

• Entrepreneurs leading start-ups, fast growing ventures, and spinoffs

• Potential franchisees

• Future and recent graduate students

• Franchisors, licensors, and agents

• Franchise consultants and suppliers

• Professional firms

• Banks and other sources of development funding

• Venture capitalists and angel investors

• Growing Romanian enterprises ready to expand into the US

• Entrepreneurial ventures in search of US partners

• Individual investors

• Importers

• Exporters

• Distributors

• Anyone interested in opening an office in America

Topics covered:

• Opening Offices/Commencing Operations in the USA

• Distributing in America

• Marketing in America

• Raising American Capital

• Partnering with U.S. Companies

• Finding Products in America to Import and Distribute in Romania

• U.S. Visa Options and Strategy

• International Tax Planning and Minimizing Strategies

• First-Hand, Case-Study Experience – presented by Romanian business?executives who have penetrated the market, raised capital and established strategic?partnerships in America.

• Incubator, Soft Landing and Turnkey Options and Opportunities – to become?established with minimal risk and upfront expense.

Networking and private meetings with speakers available:

In-person networking opportunities, conference materials, publicity, and media kits will be available. The conference organizers will also arrange private meetings for interested individuals with panelists.
