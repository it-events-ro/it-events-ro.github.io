Update - NetTuesday - Modern Revolutions Begin Online

marți, 12 aprilie 2011

Update : we'll have Szakats Istvan joining Oleg in the discussion about modern activism. Istvan is co-founder of AltArt Foundation and meber of the Group for Social Action. Artist, curator and cultural manager, he prefers to be referred to as "cultural contributor". He has generated numerous artistic and cultural projects with a social stake and he is an evangelist for urban movements.

Hi friends,

We are extremely excited to introduce this NetTuesday, as we have an amazing guest joining - Oleg Kozlovsky . He is co-founder of  “Solidarnost”  and „Oborona”,  two very renowned  movements for democracy in Russia. His speciality? Using online to mobilize offline reaction. He is also that type of a blogger that gets featured in The Washington Post, so we highly reccomend you this read: http://olegkozlovsky.wordpress.com/ .

I’m looking at you, crazy civil society members! We know you all dream of bigger or a smaller revolutions. Why not start with a FaceBook account or a blog? Let’s meet Oleg this Tuesday and have a serious, yet highly enjoyable discussion about modern activism.

See you there,

Net Tuesday Team

P.S.: We thank our dear friends from Ratiu Center for Democracy for giving us this great opportunity to have Oleg at a NetSquared event.
