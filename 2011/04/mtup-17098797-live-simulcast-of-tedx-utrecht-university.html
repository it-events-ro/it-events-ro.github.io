Live Simulcast of TEDx Utrecht University

luni, 4 aprilie 2011

We find the right people to inspire us at such events as TED and TEDx, correct?

On April 4th we have the chance to watch live during the TEDx Utrecht University, in Romania, at Bucharest Hubb, amazing talks about Building a sustainable future .

In a finite world where resources are scarce and the population is expanding we need to come together and think about building a sustainable future. Join us on April 4th, 2011, to engage in an event with ideas worth spreading. Come see how diverse disciplines get together to share their thoughts on what tomorrow should look like.

Among speakers: Marc Cornelissen (Arctic Explorer); Cesare Peeren (Architect basing his designs on the concept of recyclicity); Katja Gruijters (Food Artist and Designer); Tim Steinweg will talk aout the human and environmental story behind our mobile phones, laptops, iPads; Paul Heistein (former globetrotter); Mark Edwards (photoprapher).

See here detailed info about all the speakers.

The event starts at 11.30 AM (GMT+2) and lasts until 6 PM (GMT+2).

Considering it's Monday, it will be a walk-in simulcast, that is (busy) people who can't stay during the entire event can walk in and see parts of the simulcast (morning/afternoon/one single TEDxUtrechtUniversity Talk).
