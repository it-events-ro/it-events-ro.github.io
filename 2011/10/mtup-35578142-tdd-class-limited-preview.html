TDD Class Limited Preview

joi, 6 octombrie 2011

In the past few months, I've been working hard at creating a TDD class for Mosaic Works. The time has come to put it to the test, and I need your help to make it better.

I am therefore offering a 2 days TDD class on 6-7 October 2011. The class is completely free but with very limited attendance: only 6 people. You can expect to learn a lot about the topic during this class. However, the way the topics are presented will not be perfect and your feedback will help me fine tune it.

Join this class if you want to learn TDD and to help improve the class. To attend the class, you need to have programming experience in Java or C# and be interested in the topic and preferably have little experience with TDD.

After registering, you will receive an email with further instructions.

Thank you and see you at the class!
