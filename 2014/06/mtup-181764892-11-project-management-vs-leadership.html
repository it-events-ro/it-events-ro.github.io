#11 Project Management vs Leadership

joi, 5 iunie 2014

Hi Everyone,


The topic of this meeting is Leadership vs Project Management. For a Project Manager, what does it take to be the leader? Is it allways the project manager the real leader of the team? If not, how should both of them work together in order to get things done?

Let us find the answer together based on your own experience.

If you want to participate, please and join this meetup.

If later something comes up and you cannot participate, just take a minute and actualize your answer.


The meeting will be on 5th of June, 18:30 at Briks, Horia Street.
