Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 16 iunie 2014

After a couple of no-app presentation TiMo events we are returning to the regular format in a two presentation event.

As a first announced presentation within this mid-June edition, we'll have a great opportunity to have some deep insights on one of the already recognized apps developed by a group of great Timisoara professionals. 7Out is an ad-hoc event generation tool developed by the prolific team consisting of Antonia Goga , Horia Jurcut and Stefan Filip . Their team was recently designated as the Romanian winner of the Best Designer Startup category of Central European Startup Awards 2014 while their app was proclaimed as the mobile app of the day on May 31st by FWA . Only these and it becomes clear you should meet them.

The second app to be presented is TipExpert - World Cup Edition, a mobile application to be used by football and betting passionate people, in order to make predictions for the games played in the 2014 Brasil Football World Cup.

Developed by a team coordinated by Andrei Markovits ( Ana Markovits - backend & Android development, Florin Moisa - iOS development, Alpar Kusztos - design) , the app has versions for both Android and iOS powered devices. As it is already downloadable from the Play Store we are looking forward to have it available on the Apple App Store. Andrei will approach in his talk both the tech and biz aspects of building and launching an app that is so confined to stressful, impossible to control milestones, but that can definitely benefit by the popularity of such a globally relevant sport event!

As usually we'll offer participant beverages and snacks, so please be prepared with the 15 RON contribution!
