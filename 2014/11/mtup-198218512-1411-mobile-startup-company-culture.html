1411 Mobile Startup | Company Culture

joi, 27 noiembrie 2014

For the first time our monthly event will cover two topics:

Building a startup for your mobile app

Everybody teaches you how to build a mobile app. Alexandru Iliescu, cofounder of Mondly Languages will join us to showcase tips and tricks about how to turn it into a successful global business.

Engineers as the core of company culture

At rewards.ro (alpha) we believe that a company culture that puts the engineer at its core, is one that will achieve the best results in customer satisfaction. The only way the team wins is by focusing on making each of its members succeed on all aspects of ones life through all types of rewards.

Such a company is a magnet for new talent that will grow and join the elite of valuable engineers that are appreciated way beyond the limits of the organization.

While juniors are interested in an eco-system where they can grow and outperform themselves, the A-players are more interested into solving crucial problems and being recognized for their contribution by users they come in contact with.

You are invited to a discussion about the tool that will change the way you reward your team's engagement and boost their morale to outperform themselves.
