Timisoara Mobile Development Group at HackTM

vineri, 17 octombrie 2014

Everywhere, October is the month for larger tech events season opening.

We are participating with Timisoara Startup Hub, Banat IT and a big number of other tech communities in the city and an impressive list of sponsors (including Google, Elster, Digital Education, Haufe Lexware, 3Pillar Global, New Haircut, AudioNow, Continental, Movidius, Casa-Automata.com, Microsoft, Autoliv, Atigeo, Avaelgo), to the organizing of HackTM .

With[masked] participants expected under the same roof, this supersized and defocused hackathon aims to set a regional record for the South Eastern Europe biggest hackathon.

In its defocused approach, HackTM includes a number of 7 tracks :

• mobile technologies

• web technologies

• open-source

• game development

• hardware, embedded & robotics, home automation, wearables, IOT

• open data platforms & city projects

• kids coding

We are inviting all Timisoara Mobile Development Group to join HackTM, to enjoy a longer exposure to technology than our regular 3 hours meetups, to form teams with other passionate devs, to receive support from tought mentors, to code and to pitch your freshly baked apps, impressing the jury and to win one of the mobile technology track's recognitions for:

• the best iOS app

• the best Android app

• the best Windows Phone app

• the best Blackberry app

• the best cross-platform app

• the best app developed by an individual participant

• the app with the best UI/UX

• the best consumer app

• the best business-dedicated app.


An overall track winning app will finally be proclaimed, awarded and included in the competition for The HackTM Great Prize consisting of, yes you guessed, some awesome tech stuff.

For more details and registration go to HackTM web page or Eventbrite ticketing page .
