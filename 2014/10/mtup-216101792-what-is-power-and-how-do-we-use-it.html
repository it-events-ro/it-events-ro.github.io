What is power and how do we use it?

joi, 30 octombrie 2014

In the first meeting of the group we agreed that the best approach for the community to guide the conversations is by extracting topics from the flow of dialogue and make each voice important by using democratic voting.

The voting was done on https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/7N3HPC7 and the most popular subject was "What is power and how do we use it?"

We've written a more personal perspective on this topic on the group's blog: http://thehumansidegroup.com/2014/10/28/power-seeking/

When Andrei and myself talked about this subject, the dialogue followed some axis:

- from self to others

- from subordinates to superiors

- from influence over peers to control over processes

This Thursday we are looking forward to leave the confinement of such simple analysis through dialogue inside the community. See you there!
