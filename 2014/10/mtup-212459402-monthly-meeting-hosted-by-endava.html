Monthly Meeting Hosted by Endava

joi, 30 octombrie 2014

PMI Romania invites all its members to the second Monthly Meeting this year in Cluj-Napoca on 30th October 2014. Non-members are accepted under some conditions, please read on the bottom of the page.

Together with Endava Cluj, we challenge you again, this time with the folowing subject: “Study case: outsourcing company being a trusted partner and getting involved in client's product roadmap”

As you are already used to by now, after the presentation we shall to a panel debate that aims to examine more thoroughly the importance of the topic mentioned above.

Agenda:
18:30 – 19:00 Registration & Welcome Coffee
19:00 – 19:15 PMI Presentation
19:15 – 19:30 Presentation Endava
19:30 – 20:00 Presentation PM topic
20:00 – 20:30 Panel Discussion
20:30 Networking


We are waiting for you on October 30, 2014 starting with 18:30 at Endava location in United Business Center Iulius, Str. Al. Vaida Voivod no. 53-55, Cluj-Napoca. Fifty (50) seats are available for this meeting.

If you have a PMP, PMI-SP, PMI-RMP or PMI-ACP certification you can claim two PDUs by attending this event, after receiving the event code from PMI Romania. You can participate if you are not a member PMI Romania, provided you have not participated in other meetings monthly PMI Romania this year. This way we give access to people interested in becoming members of our organization and want to know the benefits from this affiliation. Please request further information about our organization addressing your PMI Romania's board members present at this meeting.

Each member can come accompanied by a non-member PMI Romania. However, it is necessary to send an e-mail containing the full name of the guest to [masked].
