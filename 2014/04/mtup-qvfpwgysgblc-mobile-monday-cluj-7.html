Mobile Monday Cluj #7

luni, 28 aprilie 2014

Hi all,

In this meetup, we’ll discuss the future of image processing and how technology drives change in this domain. Whether we’re speaking of computer vision, gesture recognition, image enhancement and facial recognition these are all areas of interest for mobile developers, as we are heading towards a world that operates with more visuals with each passing day. If we’re speaking of the general rate of adoption of your average Joe, it is definitely and evidently increasing.  At least in what photos are concerned, the estimated number for 2014 is around 800 billion.

Our proposal for the forthcoming event is to generate discussion threads on the above and other topic related ideas.

The trigger of our discussion will be the presentation held by Cristian Messel.

Cristian is a free software developer and he’s presentation will evolve around:

-->Detection vs Recognition

-->OpenCV, Integral Images, Viola-Jones, Predator

-->Face Detection API

-->Rat Detection to help cure Alzheimer's Disease

About Cristian and what he’s working on:

--> http://json.northpole.ro/

--> https://github.com/fmoga/servustalk

See you on Monday!
