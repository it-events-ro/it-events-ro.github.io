1st Edition of Rails Girls Timisoara

vineri, 30 mai 2014

Rails Girls comes to Timişoara! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the chocolate world of Ruby on Rails.

Rails Girls Timisoara is part of the Rails for Girls global initiative that organizes 12-hour Ruby-on-Rails workshops for absolute beginners in the world of programming and web development in locations around the world.

Interested? Applications for participants are now open via our dedicated web page .

We are also looking for coaches - if you think you'd be a good fit, drop us an email at [masked] and we'll get back to you shortly with more details.

Also make sure to keep an eye on our Facebook page and Twitter account - we will start posting regular updates there very soon.

Our partners & sponsors to date (the list is fast expanding):

UnifiedPost , UP-nxt , GitHub , Timisoara Startup Hub .

More about Rail Girls Timisoara

Rails Girls Timisoara aims to support women programmers in becoming more familiar with Ruby and Rails. Join us in Timisoara for two days of coding and fun - don't forget to bring along your laptop, your curiosity and your sense of adventure!
