MoMo #10 - Android L preview + Mobile App Insights + iBeacons

luni, 28 iulie 2014

Hello everybody!


If it's Monday, it's Mobile Monday. For this session, our speakers are going to be:

· Ciprian Grigor ,Technical Lead Android @ 3Pillar Global

· Vlad Vidican , CTO @ Jigglist.

· Marius Mornea , Business Developer @ OnyxBeacon.

Ciprian will talk about Android L preview and about the Google I/O Conference that he attended in San Francisco 3 weeks ago.

Vlad will talk about Jigglist - a mobile communication app for top recommendations - and how it evolved from a web based blogging platform.

Marius presentation will include a brief overview of the current state of the iBeacons market and an open discussion regarding:

- beacons market in Cluj

- Google alternatives in the micro/ indoor location and context

- Beacons and Wearables (Android Wear)

At the end, there will be a trial session with Cardboard VR and Smart Watch .

See you on Monday!
