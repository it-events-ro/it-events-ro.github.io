Mobile Monday Cluj #12 - Mobile Security & DriveAssist App

luni, 29 septembrie 2014

Hello everybody!

This new meetup of mobile developers brings about an interesting subject, that we've been long expecting. We're going to have onboard Sorin Moldovan , who is going to develop on the subject of Mobile Security and how to enhance security on our devices as users and how to enhance security for our apps, as developers, having in mind the safety of our clients.

Next up we'll have Razvan Itu , a proud startuper, fond of image analysis and recognition. Razvan is currently involved in developing advanced driver assistance systems using mobile technologies and computer vision. The main project he is working on is a new startup called DriveAssist that represents an augmented Android application meant to assist drivers on public roads and highways.

Razvan discovered his passion for software development during high school. He began developing simple web sites for personal use. Later on he found a new interest, mobile development. Nowadays, Razvan has a bachelors degree in computer science, and is studying for a masters degree in artificial intelligence and computer vision.

See you on Monday!
