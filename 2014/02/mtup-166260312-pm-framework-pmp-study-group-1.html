PM Framework - PMP Study Group #1

joi, 20 februarie 2014

Do you know what is EEF, but OPA ? Those who are PMP certified should know.

EEF stands for Enterprise Environmental Factors and OPA for Organizational Process Assets and together with Simona Bonghez from Colours in Projects we will launch the monthly Cluj PMP Study Group.

At the subject project management framework we will clear out the mystery with EEF and OPA and we will present you the rest of the 10 themes schedule in the 2014 study groups.

The study groups are organized in a manner which will help you to prepare for the PMP certification exam.

For this edition the access is free of charge being supported by Colours in Projects and ClujHUB.

See you soon !

ps. after the study group we will continue over beers and snacks so please join also the next meetup scheduled for the 20th of March.
