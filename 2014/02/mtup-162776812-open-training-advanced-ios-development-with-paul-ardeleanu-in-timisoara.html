Open training: Advanced iOS Development with Paul Ardeleanu in Timisoara

luni, 10 februarie 2014

UPDATE 1: Early bird period extended

Please be aware, we are also organizing an Open Training: Introduction to iOS on February 7th-8th.

Timisoara Mobile Development Group and Tritium Consulting SRL are organizing the first Advanced iOS open training in the region.

The training is authored and delivered by Paul Ardeleanu , a reputed Romanian-origin, London-based, iOS developer and software engineer with a passion for mobile utilities. Paul is regularly delivering iOS Development training events with one of the major specialized organizations in London. Paul is also regularly speaking at iPhone user group meetups at London's Apple store and is one of the early predictors of the end of computers as we knew them, with the touch interfaces going to rule the world. Paul is also organizer of or speaker to relevant iOS development conferences in UK and beyond.


The one-day training will take place in the City Business Development Center.

During the Advanced iOS Development Training , you will expand on your iOS programming skills and learn how to get the best out of the iOS hardware.

<b style="font-size : 16px">

<b style="font-size : 16px"> The agenda of the training will include the following topics


* Code Design Patterns

* Memory Management

* Blocks

* Consuming Web services

* Testing and Optimisation

* iTunes Connect - security (encryption, digital signatures, etc.)

* How to publish on the App Store


<b style="font-size : 16px"> Training Prerequisites


To get the most out of their participation to the Advanced iOS development training, those interested should have iOS programming knowledge and, of course, a fair understanding of object oriented programming. You will also need to be a registered iPhone developer.

We are expecting you to bring your own Intel-based Mac to this training. If you wish to publish your app to Apple Stores, you will need to be a registered Apple Developers (this paid registration process can be completed during the training course).

Training Methodology

The training methodology relies on presentations, discussions, practical exercises but mostly on hands-on programming sessions.
