Monthly Meetup #13

miercuri, 8 mai 2013

Hello everyone,

This edition of Tabara de Testare will be a treat for all those of you who want to find out more about testing on mobile devices. Specifically, manual testing on mobile phones.

The meeting will start with a brief introduction into the world of mobile phones, their evolution and the phases that testing went through for these devices as a result of all the technological innovations.

The program will continue with discussions on specific areas, particularly localization and client acceptance testing.

The meeting will end with one hour workshop that will explore some of the aspects discussed earlier and will contribute to gaining some insight on how to approach mobile phone testing.

The application that will be tested will be a surprise. You need not worry about anything, just bring your smartphones with you, regardless of their make.

Speakers: Ana-Maria Mociofan, Dorian Almasan, Razvan Vuscan

See you on the 8th of May!

P.S. Don't forget about the contribution for the Cluj Cowork location which is 10 lei .

(Please note that the meetup venue can change!)

Thank you,

.Iuliana
