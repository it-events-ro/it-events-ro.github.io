Timisoara Startup Weekend - Discounts for TiMo Members

vineri, 20 septembrie 2013

There's a good news for tech people in Timisoara for the beginning of this Autumn: we are organizing the first local edition of the globally implemented Startup Weekend concept.

So you should get ready to spend one awesome weekend together with other local and foreign tech, business and design guys, staring on Friday, Sep 20th afternoon by generating, ranking and selecting ICT project ideas, by creating and staffing teams to implement those fantastic ideas with the close and direct support from world class mentors and coaches, experienced in building from scratch and growing up multi-million EUR revenue generating apps, and finally pitching them on Sunday, September 22nd evening to a competent jury made of entrepreneurs, business angels and VCs, tech and business gurus.

You'll have the unique opportunity to meet, between others, the likes of internationally acclaimed entrepreneurs like Bill Liao , Vidar Andersen , Patrick Bosteels , Philipp Kandal .

The Startup Weekend concept is pretty suitable for a weekend-long intensive development process centered on mobile apps. You'll have a unique opportunity to create in a very motivating, competitive and supportive environment, with your working desk in the middle of the global reach launch pad represented by the SW world-wide community.

As bringing in the people from the mobile community is a must, the organizers are offering a special discount of 20% from the regular price of EUR 65 to the members of Timisoara Mobile Development Group. Please send a message to the TiMo organizer ([masked]) in order to express your interest to benefit of this offer and for requesting the discount code for your registration .


For any additional information needed, follow event's blog and/or please use the comment field of this event.


Enjoy the rest of the Summer and join us for an outstanding tech and entrepreneurial beginning of Autumn!
