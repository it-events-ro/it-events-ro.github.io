Test Automation: From Record/Playback to Framework, by Roxana Cracana

miercuri, 4 decembrie 2013

Business pressure becomes higher and higher to decrease time-to-market for new product releases. Continuous delivery means also continuous testing, so automating everything is the new trend in software industry. In this context, there is the need for test automation frameworks that are flexible, reusable, easy to use and maintain for thousands of tests executed every day.

Presentation will start with a brief walk through of various types of test automation frameworks with its issues and benefits. It will continue with a detailed test automation framework architecture, suitable especially for UI functional testing, that is easy to integrate as part of the software testing process and further into the process of continuous delivery. There will be mentioned the focus points of the framework design, some of the lessons learned during implementation of a flexible architecture, encountered issues and possible solutions.

This session is addressed to testers with automation experience.  Also testers with less or no experience in automation are more than welcome, as any tester can become at one point a test automation framework user or creator.
