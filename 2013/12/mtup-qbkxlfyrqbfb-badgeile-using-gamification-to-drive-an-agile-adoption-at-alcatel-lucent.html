Badgeile -- Using gamification to drive an agile adoption at Alcatel-Lucent

marți, 3 decembrie 2013

For this event we'll have a special guest from Alcatel-Lucent. Cosmin Lupu will be sharing their story of using gamification techniques and creating a friendly competitive environment for adopting agile.

Here's his description:

While I have heard of Scrum/Agile "badge challenges" on some occasions, they were either theoretical, or just basic models presented."Badgeile" is a complex game with a coverage growing to hundreds of employees - we have put it in place at Alcatel-Lucent Romania.It can help "selling" Agile to your organization.

This presentation is a detailed description of this game concept, rules and evolution, in case you want to replicate such gamification experiment on your side.
