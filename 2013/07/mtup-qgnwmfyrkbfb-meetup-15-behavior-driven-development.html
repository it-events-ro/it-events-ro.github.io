Meetup #15 = Behavior Driven Development

miercuri, 3 iulie 2013

Hello everyone,

Our 15th meetup is focused on Behavior Driven Development (BDD).

There will be an introduction to BDD, followed by examples of behavior driven tests.

All these will be integrated in the bigger picture of a project to support understanding of the notions and generate discussions.

The tech stack will consists of:

- Ruby

- Gherkin

- Cucumber

- Capybara

The presentation will be held by Gabi Kis.

Interesting enough for you?

Then we'll see you on the 3rd of July!

P.S. Don't forget about the contribution for the Cluj Cowork location which is 10 lei .

(Please note that the meetup venue can change!)

Thanks,

.Iuliana
