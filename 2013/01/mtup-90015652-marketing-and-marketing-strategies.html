Marketing and Marketing Strategies

miercuri, 30 ianuarie 2013

Andrei started a new discussion...
"I know we're just at the beginning, talking about business models and whatever, but I would like to ask ... when are we going to get to marketing? I'd like to hear some people talk about how to market your product in the IT world, how they've approached it and how it should be done.

Besides advertising through Google Adwords, Facebook, Admob for mobile, etc, what are the other ways of doing it? Or, even using these advertising systems, which ones are the best? Also, how much money should be allocated by a start-up, to marketing and advertising?

I'm talking, of course, mostly about B2C products, which I'm guessing are the ones who are in need of good marketing, a lot more than B2B. (or, I might be wrong)

So, maybe on the second or third meetup from now, if someone with experience could talk about these things, that would be great. Anyone backing me up? :)"
I am...Let's discuss it in January.
