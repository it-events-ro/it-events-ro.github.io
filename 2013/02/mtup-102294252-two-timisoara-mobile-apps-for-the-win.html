Two Timisoara mobile apps for the win!

sâmbătă, 2 februarie 2013

During this period, two of our frequently presenting teams are engaged in two different but interesting competitions and definitely need your support for becoming THE winners:

Cobalt Sign (co-owned by Oana and Arthur Csertus ) compete to be one of the 20 winners of the Startup Weekend's "Windows Startup Challenge" with a new app they presented at MobileAppCamp in Skopje last December.

As described by the authors, the app, Weightographic, "is a simple and beautiful app for weight tracking and healthy habit building for Windows tablets and phones, addressed to the overweight 55% of the population in North America and Europe."

No matter if you are in or outside the 55%, you can see a Weightographic presentation video and vote for the app once every 24h here .

SlickFlick (developed by Maria Constantinescu , Raul Andrisan and Walter Fettich ) is nominated for The Next Web Romanian Startup Awards 2013 in the Best mobile app category. As you already know, SlickFlick is a visual story telling app.

If you get on the competition's voting page , please also select the other two Timisoara nominees 123contactform (nominated for the Best web app ) and Adrian Gheara (123contactform's business angel, nominated for the Best investor category).

Let's show our tech & entrepreneurial ecosystem's cohesion and help our peers to bring good news to Timisoara!

Please RSVP YES to this virtual event if you have voted for the two teams! Thanks!
