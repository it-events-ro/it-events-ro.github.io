Timisoara Mobile Development Group February Meetup

luni, 18 februarie 2013

The program of the February edition of Timisoara Mobile Development Group will include a set of presentations including one of the app of the February 2-3 Mobile and Tablet development Hackathon co-organized by TiMo and CodeCamp and two of very freshly launched Android apps by Timisoara-based teams.

.

http://photos2.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/a/2/a/event_203402602.jpegThe high-school students team formed by Aurel Faur , Bogdan Stanga , Dani Dobre , Paul Cazan will talk about a time and location-aware automated silent profile changer for Android, they developed during the 1 and a half day Mobile and Tablet Development Hackaton earlier this month ( photos here ).

The second presentation will be a feminine one: Lavinia Basaraba and her team mates, Lavinia Bratis , Oana Balaceanu and Mirona Tartan will mix us with their photo manipulation app that can help you define your outfit style. Download Mix Me Beta from Google Play and recreate yourself!

The third presentation will put the audience in the gaming mode: Oana Bratu , Mircea Rila , Adrian Pasarica will talk about and demonstrate their Spot the Number game, recently launched on the Google Play version and currently in advanced testing phase of the iOS version.

Stay tuned for more info as a forth point in the event's agenda could be announced soon!

You are kindly asked to bring with you the 15 RON (if possible the exact sum) to cover the cost of beverage and snacks we are offering during the registration period and the mid-event break.
