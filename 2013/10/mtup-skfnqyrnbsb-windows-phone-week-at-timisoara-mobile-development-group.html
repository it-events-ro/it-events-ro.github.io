Windows Phone Week at Timisoara Mobile Development Group

luni, 7 octombrie 2013

Windows Phone Week is a series of events created for the developer community by Windows Phone Developer MVPs. The events are organized in 14 countries (Romania, US, India, Ukraine, Italy, Brazil, France, Croatia, Spain, Columbia, Indonezia, etc), encouraging developers from around the world to explore app and gaming development in WP.

The Timisoara implementation of the week is coordinated by Catalin Gheorghiu , Microsoft MVP and Oana Csertus , both Nokia Developer Champions .

Together with Catalin and Oana we've included in the event's agenda a set of presentations on various aspects related to WP apps split in two sessions:
