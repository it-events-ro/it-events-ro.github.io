Special edition: black boxes testing – literally!

sâmbătă, 19 octombrie 2013

On their way to Eurostar , some little black boxes suddenly decided to stop by in Iasi to be tested.

For this edition, our guests from TdT Cluj , Oana and Adina , planned some very interesting puzzle games to practice our pattern finding skills. We will do a practical testing exercise and will learn by interacting and collaborating.

Bring your detective mindset, as you’ll need to find the patterns that lurk inside the programs!

Very important notes for the attendees:

This event is a limited seating session! This time we are even more confined due to the rules of the game! In case you cannot make it to the event, please change your RSVP answer to "No" ASAP, so that the first person in the waiting list is (automatically) moved in the Attendees list and is ahead of time notified. Posting a comment does not help!

Changing our RSVP to "NO" one day before the event is preferable to not changing it at all and not showing up or changing it one hour before the event. By updating our RSVP in time we show respect to the persons on the Waiting List and to our community.

Thank you in advance for your attention to this matter!
