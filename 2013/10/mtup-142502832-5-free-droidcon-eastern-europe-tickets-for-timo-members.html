5 Free Droidcon Eastern Europe Tickets for TiMo Members

luni, 7 octombrie 2013

Our friend Dan Romescu is offering to 5 interested members of TiMoDev group the possibility to attend the Droidcon Eastern Europe Bucharest with an 100% discount on the participation fee (worth 125 EUR). Additionally, if you are a student, the organizers will also offer you free accommodation.

Droidcon Eastern Europe is not only the biggest regional event dedicated to Android developers, but also the place where you have the opportunity to take part at some first runs on the Romanian market.

Here are 5 reasons why you shouldn’t miss Droidcon Eastern Europe, on the 7-8th October, in Bucharest:


• Test the Google Glasses in premiere and meet the Romanian team of developers who wrote the first application for the famous glasses;

• Be part, live, at the competition where the most important 10 mobile Android applications of the year will be selected

• Find out in just 2 days which are the most important news and trends in apps development, monetization strategies, security for mobile apps, design and so on.

• You can test if your application has a good design and learn how to be successful in Google Play by attending Design Thinking Lab.

• Meet leaders in the area of application development and extend your network.

If you are interested, please RSVP to this meetup.com event and also send me an e-mail at [masked].
