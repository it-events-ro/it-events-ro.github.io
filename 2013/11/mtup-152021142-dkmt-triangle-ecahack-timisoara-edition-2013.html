DKMT Triangle ecaHack - Timisoara Edition 2013

sâmbătă, 30 noiembrie 2013

We invite all the hacks enthusiasts (UX/UI , DESIGNERS, DEVELOPERS, OPEN MINDED PEOPLE) to participate to the last series of ecaHacks on 2013.

The ecaHack 2013, a hacking marathon launched by our fellow member Dan Romescu, that covered locations in Eastern and Central Europe and Central Asia, will conclude in Banat-DKMT* area with 3 ecaHacks scheduled in the Timisoara - Szeged - Novi-Sad triangle.

We'll start in Timisoara, November 30th-December 1st, continue December 7th-8th in Szeged and Novi-Sad in January 2014 (TBC).

The philosophy of the event is a cross-platform or platform neutral one, with all the platform enthusiasts being welcome to attend.

Additionally, we'll bring in for hackers' delight some devices that are still in very early market stage. Sony SmartWatch 2, the Android PC (APC board) or the Epson Moverio wearable display will be here for you to use in your hacks.

There will be plenty of prizes to "fight" for, provided by ALCATEL ONE TOUCH FR, the global ecaHack sponsor!

* for those unaware, DKMT is a HU-RO-SRB euroregion comprising areas centered by the major cities of Szeged, Timisoara, Novi-Sad, an extremely resourceful region for the tech field
