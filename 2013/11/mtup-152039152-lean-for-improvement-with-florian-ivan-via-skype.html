Lean for improvement, with Florian Ivan (via Skype)

vineri, 29 noiembrie 2013

Agile has become synonym with change. And every change has as ultimate purpose the improvement. Everybody wants to improve things, but few succeed! And even fewer are capable of explaining how they did it.Lean offers a framework that proved its efficiency in the last 70 years or so. And yet, few use it correctly because it’s not obvious. It’s not that much about rules and recipes as it is about values, principles and a lot of discipline. Join Florian for a short intro on how lean thinking can improve the way you look at your projects and eventually come up with ideas to get the job done faster, better and cheaper.

Florian Ivan, Managing Partner, Rolf Consulting Germany,  is a project management consultant and trainer with a strong focus on Agile methodologies. His prior work experience includes managing programs for Microsoft Corporation and overseeing the development of a business intelligence start-up from creation to acquisition. He holds a master’s degree in business administration and a post-graduate degree in corporate communications. Among his certifications: PMP, PMI-ACP, CSM, Prince2 Practitioner, MCTS, MVP.
