The next projects of Startup Lab Community

miercuri, 28 august 2013

Hi everybody,

I want to invite you Wednesday, 28/08, 17h, to discuss about the projects that we will launch in Startup Lab Community. These projects concern the following events/facts:

·  Mures Startup Weekend- next edition

·  TED Mures- first edition

·  Co-working space- for actual and for future entrepreneurs

·  Startup Lab Meetups- regularly meetings in order to promote entrepreneurship, innovation and new technologies ( the format is the same with the previous startup lab meetings)

·  Open Connect- meetings where entrepreneurs present their idea in front of VC/business angels

·  Startup Lab website administration.

The main focus is to find out the leaders and the members of each of these projects.  Each leader and his team will decide the planning and he will manage all the aspects related to the project.

Thank you!

Have a great day!
