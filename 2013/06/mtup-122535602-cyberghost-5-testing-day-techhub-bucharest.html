CyberGhost 5 Testing Day @ Techhub Bucharest

vineri, 14 iunie 2013

On the 14th of June, the CyberGhost team welcomes you to test the newest version of CyberGhost VPN and to enjoy a nice day together with the tech community at TechHub Bucharest.

Who is CyberGhost?

One of the best known VPN providers worldwide, CyberGhost is focused on vital matters like online privacy, data security and bypassing internet censorship. At the very core of the service lays a team of young and enthusiastic techies determined to stand up for what they believe in and make a difference for the better.

What’s going to happen?

You will test a prototype of our future product, CyberGhost 5.0, meet our topnotch team and benefit from mentorship. Don’t worry if you don’t know anything about the product or never used a similar one before. We need your fresh minds and critical eyes in order to achieve the highest standard possible.

One of our Ghostie Research Expert will be joining you for an hour where he or she will try to learn as much as possible from your CyberGhost User Experience. You will receive a laptop and an “Action List” with stuff you will try to achieve in the application. You will have to achieve them by yourself during the trial testing time, without any help from the Ghostie Research Expert. Afterwards you will be able to receive all the information you need from us. No matter if this is related to CyberGhost or not.

Why is it so cool?

You’ll get to use the Techhub working space all day for free, meet people with similar interests and goals and get a substantial dose of knowledge directly from an amazing team of professionals with many years of experience, and tons of great ideas.
