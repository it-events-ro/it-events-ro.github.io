Net Tuesday, ep. 4

marți, 26 octombrie 2010

Hello guys,

Let's have another Tuesday meeting this week at Verde Cafe. I have a new cookie recipe that i would just love to test on you, it's very much about chocolate. . .

At the last meeting we have observed that some people want to meet earlier, around 6, while others cannot arrive earlier than 8.30. To make everybody happy, we decided to have a more practical session and the first round of beers until 8.30, and then we'll go on debating the topic of the meeting, ideally with a nice guest.

For the practical outcome session, everybody is more than welcome to put things on the agenda-specific topics that one is interested in resolving. I'll update the agenda and kindly ask/bribe the awesome IT crowd to come and help. I, for one, would like to know more about tools that make long distance communication possible. I wanna master project management tools such as Doodle, that come handy when you're, let's say, 215 km away from your boss, test the best solution of recording a long-distance video interview, and find out about other cool stuff that couldn't ever cross my mind.
So, i declare the list open, and wait for it to be filled in, as well as the super-nice IT volunteer/s to offer to help a damsel in distress :)

See you soon,

Ruxandra
