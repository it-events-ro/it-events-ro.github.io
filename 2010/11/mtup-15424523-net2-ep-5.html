marți, 16 noiembrie 2010

Hei people,

Let's have a Net2 on the 16th. All sorts of crazy happening: I'm making the Dobos cake (Marius, prepare that spoon :)), Chris is descending from his ivory tower and will be joining us, the cats at Verde Cafe are turning gay, so it's gonna be such a show! :)
I'll be waiting for the NGO people to be putting things on the agenda for the practical 6 to 8 session (it wouldn't hurt if the guys could have admin access), as for the funny discussion afterwords, I'm thinking of "Fundraising campaigns powered by new media" as the subject. I would love to announce a speaker at this point, but the dude is so very busy, he might show up or not, so...

UPDATE: Back with good news, as promised: our guest confirmed his presence. We'll be joined by Toader Paun, new media guy for Europa FM and now for Romania Libera. He also handled all the social campaigns in which Europa FM was a partner, and I speak from experience when I say he rocks the house!

We'll be talking about new media empowerment applied to social campaigns and we're gonna share some awesome case studies, including the campaign for which Europa FM and Habitat for Humanity (yes, that's right, our friend Alex was a part of this too) recently won Silver Award for Excellence at the Romanian PR Awards

So see you soon,

Ruki
