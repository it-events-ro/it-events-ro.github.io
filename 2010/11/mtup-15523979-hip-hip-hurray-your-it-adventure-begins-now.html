We at TechSoup are very excited about our partnership with IT Spark; it enables us to reach a wider public and produce a bigger impact on civil society. Our collaboration goes beyond simply providing IT support opportunities to NGOs in Romania; it opens a wide variety of options centered around the social benefits of Microsoft applications and provides us with the chance to pilot concepts and gather an exceptional, creative community like IT Spark around initiatives that empower civil society.

About IT Spark : IT Spark is a technical community, useful to those in the IT-Pro entry and mid-level area: students or beginners in IT, technical and non-technical staff from SMB/NGOs and end-users/consumers that are either just getting started, or want to learn more about this field. Our partnership with TechSoup enables us to interact more easily with NGOs, in order to offer practical and feasible solutions to their problems.
