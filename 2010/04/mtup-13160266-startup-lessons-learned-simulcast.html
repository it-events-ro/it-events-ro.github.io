Startup Lessons Learned Simulcast

vineri, 23 aprilie 2010

Startup Lessons Learned is the first event designed to unite those interested in what it takes to succeed in building a lean startup.

The live event will take place in San Francisco, California. In order to globally diffuse the this unique event, the organizers decided to partner with local entities to host simulcasts of the event.

Timisoara Software Business Incubator was accepted as one of the 8 European locations so far to host a simulcast of the event. The local time for the starting of the event is Friday, April 23rd, 6 PM while the end time is Saturday, April 24th, 4 AM.

The goal for this event is to give practitioners and students of the lean startup methodology the opportunity to hear insights from leaders in embracing and deploying the core principles of the lean startup methodology. The day-long event will feature a mix of panels and talks focused on the key challenges and issues that technical and market-facing people at startups need to understand in order to succeed in building successful lean startups.
