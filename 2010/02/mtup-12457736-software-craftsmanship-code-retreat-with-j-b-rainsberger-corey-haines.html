Software craftsmanship - Code retreat with J. B. Rainsberger & Corey Haines

sâmbătă, 13 februarie 2010

Code retreat number 7 (Romania)

Great news: we'll have in Bucharest, week February 8-13, two of of the most active and well known software craftsmen: J.B. and Corey . On Saturday February 13, J.B. and Corey will run a code retreat - one full day, as usual. More details about this type of event: /www.agileworks.ro/software-craftsmanship/

What you have to bring with you: a laptop -with a working development environment-, your pair for programming (which of course it have to be registered at this meetup) & your programming-mood :)

The code retreat is basically a community free event and we do our best to keep it that way. MosaicWorks will sponsor a part of the event expenses, but we still need:
* some contribution/sponsorship for the food&beverage (around 80 ron/participant);

Thank you in advance for your contribution to the AgileWorks community!
Thank you all that contributed already! & Thank you Netop company for hosting us!
