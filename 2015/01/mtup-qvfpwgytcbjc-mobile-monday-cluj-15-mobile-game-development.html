Mobile Monday Cluj #15: Mobile game development

luni, 26 ianuarie 2015

We're Tractor, Set, GO !, an independent games developing studio from Cluj-Napoca. For the next Mobile Monday Cluj event we'll go through the challenges of building our flagship game , Gelluloid Pro .

We'll cover technical topics such as working with Cocos 2d-x , improving performance and minimizing memory usage . We'll also talk about our scripted tutorials and the way we balance out players, scoring and achievements.

Finally, we'll end our presentation with some reflections on the overall process of building the game and a Q&A session. Join us for a good time and possibly some laughs.

About the presenters:

Cristian Cristea is an associate owner of TSG and he works as a lead developer and game designer. He's been working on games for ages past and will continue to do so for ages to come.

Horea Trîncă is also an associate owner of TSG and he works as a technical artist and game designer. He started having fun with 3D graphics in 3ds max around 15 years ago and co-founded graphics studio Tractor Media. In the meantime he studied programming and ended up with a master's degree in computer science at UBB.

Tractor Set Go is a game studio committed to make awesome games that are just a little bit different.  :)

See you on Monday!


Registration here on Meetup is mandatory for this event! Arrival is between 6:15 and 6:30.
