Tim.Net Meetup No 11

marți, 28 aprilie 2015

With fresh energy after the far too short Easter vacation, Tim.Net is back in town.

In order to further complete the enthusiasm, we’re hosting this edition in a new venue: Haufe-Lexware Romania offices.

And so that we won’t get too serious after a leisure weekend, we approach an entertainment related topic: music apps.

Ionut Antiu from Style Jukebox , a successful hi-fi cloud music player will share with us his know-how presenting 'Scaling to 100k users using Azure Cloud-services'

Robert Iagar is the second speaker of the Tim.Net Meetup #11. He is a software developer from Intelligent Software Systems and his presentation is entitled 'Clicker! A game powered by Azure Mobile Services'

See you at Haufe-Lexware Romania 's place in the 28th. ‪#‎timNet
