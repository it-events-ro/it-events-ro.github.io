UI/UX Cluj Meetup #12 - Talks with Steve Arnold - co-founder at emberlight

marți, 10 februarie 2015

Hi guys and girls!

Ready for our first meetup for 2015?

This year we're starting a new trend for our evenings! Virtual hangouts with high profile UI/UX professionals around the globe.

Agenda:

18:30 - review of 2014 and news for 2015

19:00 - Steve Arnold

Steve Arnold is Co-Founder and Chief Design Officer at emberlight , makers of a plug and play adapter that turns any light into a smart light. He is a passionate problem solver who has spent the past two decades designing brand-defining product experiences.

Steve was the designer behind the recent launch of Twitter Fabric. Prior to Twitter, Steve built product at Google where he focused on simplifying complex consumer and business products. Steve also spent a number of years at Autodesk where he was the design lead for multiple key initiatives including AutoCAD Mac and the product which became Autodesk Homestyler.

Steve will join us from San Francisco on a very, very interesting discussion for this community.

Registration here on Meetup is mandatory for this event! Arrival is between 6:15 and 6:30.

In Cluj-Napoca there is a lot of potential for growth in the software sector in more strategic (and better paid areas as well), such as User Experience and User Interaction Design. From this perspective, we are forwarding you this invitation of joining us and collaborating within the UI/UX community. This invitation is addressed to individuals and companies as well.

Due to the fact that each company has one, two ot three UI/UX specialists we invite you to connect and thus create a growth environment for the professionals in this area. In this way we are contributing to the overall development and augmentation of specialised talent in the Cluj-Napoca area.

We can't wait to meet you and learn from one another!
