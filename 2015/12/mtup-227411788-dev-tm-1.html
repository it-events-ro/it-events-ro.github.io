/DEV/TM #1

vineri, 18 decembrie 2015

We would like to invite you to the first cross-community meetup for Timisoara hackers. Whether you are interested in web or mobile development, cloud computing, big data, DevOps practices, trending or highly used programming languages, research papers and more join us at /DEV/TM and get in touch with the tech ecosystem in Timisoara.

Agenda and speakers:

Alexandru Sisu - "Spark and Machine Learning"
Rujinschi Remus - "Cluster Management Best Practices - Mesos."
Flavius-Radu Demian - "C# everywhere - Building Cross-Platform Apps with Xamarin and MvvmCross."
Alessandro Pilotti - "Windows Nano Server + Windows / Hyper-V Containers + Docker."
Cristian Vat - "Timisoara Wireless Survey."

The order of the speakers will be announced shortly before the event. Each presentation will last roughly 30-40 minutes, with breaks in between sessions. Please also RSVP on Facebook at the page of the event, in order to help us better prepare the logistics:
https://www.facebook.com/events/1653601578243069/

We mean to hold this event as a bi-anual tradition. The speakers are top notch so be sure not to miss it! :)
