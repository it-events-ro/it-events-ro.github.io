Security Aspects in the Android Ecosystem

marți, 20 octombrie 2015

With its October edition, InfoSec TM is organizing a borderline event with relevance for the members of Timisoara Mobile Development Group and other Google ecosystem interest groups, focusing on security aspects in the android OS ecosystem.


Alin Barbatei , malware researcher in the Timisoara office of Bitdefender, one of the leading security solution providers, probably the best known Romanian products on the global market, will introduce and detail topics like:

- basic concepts about Android OS

- Introduction to underground world of android malware

- Persistent threats, repackaging malware

- Why, how much, how, what and with what tools can we do about it

Alin will be accompanied in his presentation by Marius Tivadar , team leader of the malware research team, one of the devoted leaders of the InfoSec TM community. The presentation is designed as a co-exploration of these topics, offering all the participants to get a personal involvement to the discussions, planned to span over 2 hours.

Most active 5 participants will receive printed copies of The Secrets to App Success on Google Play , a Google publication.
