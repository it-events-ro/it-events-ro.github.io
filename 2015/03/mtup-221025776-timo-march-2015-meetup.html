TiMo March 2015 Meetup

luni, 16 martie 2015

There was a longer than acceptable interruption in our long history of monthly gatherings of the Timisoara mobile passionate developers.

Time to repair this has come: we are inviting you to join on Monday, March 16, a fantastic lineup:

Adrian Cuzman , one of the TiMo veterans presenting his MTG Magic Scanner , an Android application that can scan MTG ( Magic the Gathering ) cards and display their online price!

Madalin Gageanu , approaching the topic of material design and preparing lots of guidelines and tips for aligning your apps with the material design principles

Ionut Antiu , planning to unveil the way he and his team scaled their Style Jukebox app to deal with 120.000+ demanding mobile users.

Additionally, we'll have one of the Timisoara mobile entrepreneurs just returned from Mobile World Congress in Barcelona sharing his conclusions about this major industry event. Details on who exactly will speak about MWC will follow shortly.

We'll make available beverages and snacks for people attending so please be ready to contribute a 15 RON participation fee, to cover the expenses.

So, who is in for this treat?

PS: as usual, the event will start with a 30 mins networking segment, so the first presentation will start about 7.00 PM
