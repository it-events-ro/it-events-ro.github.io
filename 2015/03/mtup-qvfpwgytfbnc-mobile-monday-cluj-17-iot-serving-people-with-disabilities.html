Mobile Monday Cluj #17 - IoT serving people with disabilities

luni, 30 martie 2015

Hi guys and girls!

In this edition of Mobile Monday, we are going to talk about the Internet of Things (IoT) and the benefits this approach to technology brings for people with disabilites.

We're going to hear from two startup owners that use IoT in order to make the surrounding world more easier to understand, navigate and more full of flavour.

They are:

• Marian Goia - Sensewave , Romania


• Slavi Richmart Slavev - Blitab , Austria


Both their products are completely innovative and they solve important problems that people with disabilities face .

Sensewave is a device for hearing impared people and Blitab is a device helping blind and visually impaired people .


Curious to find out more? We are waiting for you!


Registration here on Meetup is mandatory for this event! Arrival is between 6:15 and 6:30.
