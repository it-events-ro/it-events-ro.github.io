Introduction to soldering

marți, 24 martie 2015

--EN--
Short description: Introduction to soldering We will learn the basics of soldering with a soldering pen and hot air station, removing and placing THT (through hole technology) and SMT (surface mount technology) components, various tehniques for the above, tip maintanance and cleaning and other tips and tricks.


Difficulty level : Introduction (Absolute beginner)

Necessary knowledge: None


Necessary equipment: soldering pens, hot air rework station, flux, solder (we got it covered, but bring your own if you have)

Provided equipment: soldering pens, hot air rework station, flux, solder, copper wicks, "victim" PCBs and components


Course duration: 1h30m - 2h


Organizer: George Marusteru

--RO--
Scurta descriere: Introducere in lipituri pe placa (si nu numai). Vom invata bazele lipiturilor pe placa cu ledcon si statie de lipit pe aer cald, montarea si demontarea componentelor THT si SMT, diferite tehnici pentru cele de sus, curatarea si mentenanta varfurilor de ledcon si alte trucuri.


Nivel de dificultate: Introducere (Incepator / incepator absolut)

Cunostinte necesare: Nici una

Echipament necesar: Ledcon, cositur, flux (avem toate, dar daca aveti puteti sa va aduceti propriul echipament)

Echipament oferit: ledconuri, statie de lipit pe aer cald, cositor, flux, mesa cupru, PCBuri si componente "victima".

Durata: 1 ora 30 minute - 2 ore


Organizator: George Marusteru
