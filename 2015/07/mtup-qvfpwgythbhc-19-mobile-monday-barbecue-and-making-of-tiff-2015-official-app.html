#19 Mobile Monday Barbecue and Making of TIFF 2015 official app

luni, 13 iulie 2015

The Transilvania International Film Festival, aka TIFF , is the biggest movie festival in Romania. Its 2015 edition was a great success, and part of this success was the smooth running app that helped participants schedule their movie going experience, view the agenda, find out more about the movies and share what you're up to on the social networks.

The TIFF APP 2015 was powered by Greppy Systems .

The app runs on iOS and Android. It has a Pooshwoosh push notification function used for showing images, services for scheduling and for adding items to favorites.

The most important characteristics of the application are the following:

- it shows the complete agenda with info about the movies (synopsis, trailer, images, screening cinemas and times)

- it shows the entire festival agenda

- it has a news and events sections

- it gives you the possibility to plan your schedule

- one can share the events you attend on Facebook, Twitter and other social media platform along with all the details of the event (date, hour, map with GPS coordinates)

Greppy Systems , the developer of the TIFF App, is an IT company that has been on the market since 2010, offering full stack services on platforms like Net, Asp.Net, PHP, Drupal, Zend, iOS, Android, Xamarin, Cocos.

They have partnered with AOL (America OnLine), Funtomic, 5minmedia, OkapiStudio, Engageya, Porticor, NowBuzzing, ClassOwl, Visual.ly, Connatix and many others.

The team is made up of 30 young and enthusiastic people that love working in a startup-like environment and collaborating with startups. They have plans to bring 5 to 10 more people into the team by the end of this year.

The speaker of this Mobile Monday meetup will be Marius Jumolea.

Marius Jumolea: Marius shifted from outsourcing to product in 2013 when he became the Co-Founder in Connatix & GM of Connatix Romania, platform that brings together publishers and advertisers.

Before that, he was the General Manager of Visually Romania as well as Founder & General Manager of Greppy Systems, agency serving clients in Israel, France and the US and he was involved in creating R&D teams for different big companies (AOL Videos – 5minMedia, Funtomic-Kizi).

He started his career as a software developer working on large scale projects. Besides this, he is an active investor in different startups, having in his portfolio teams from Romania and the US.
