Technologies and processes in IT products and services development

joi, 17 decembrie 2009

During this session we'll have the opportunity to communicate with Maria Diaconu of Mosaic Works on topics related to the technological side of building up an IT business.

During this session the discussions will tackle mainly the following issues:


how to structure and manage your development team?
what kind of project management approaches are most suitable?
how to smartly select technologies?
why mock up your product/service and then build successively more detailed/featured versions
release only when perfect&over or launch early and improve?
why to think scalable from the beginning?
what benefits arise from usage of modular arhitectures?
is it important to think portable?
what is the reason for developing mobile ready apps?
why thinking your project ready for as many platforms as possible?
why and how to crowd-source development, criticism and testing?
is it possible to think “green”, your application? (how to lower resource usage on both ends: server and client)


Those coming from a non-IT background should not be afraid, while Maria will use a very common language and will introduce highly understandable examples.

I am looking forward to learn together with you this apparently complicated staff!

Radu
