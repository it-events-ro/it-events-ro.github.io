Transylvania Semantic WEB Meetup

vineri, 11 iunie 2010

We hope to have the first introductory meetup at the location of Recognos Romania in Cluj.
The intention is to meet with all the interested parties and to establish the way how we will meet.
The meetup will be held Friday, June 11th 6:00 PM at the location of Recognos Romania, 21 Victor Babes St.
400012 Cluj-Napoca, Romania.

George
