AgileWorks: All about Scrum, with our special guest Kenny Rubin

miercuri, 30 iunie 2010

Kenny Rubin, former Managing Director of the Scrum Alliance & Certified ScrumTrainer is our special guest for this event. Kenny Rubin comes with a great experience and expertise in Scrum and is willing to share it with the Timisoara Agile community.

Language for this is event will be exclusively English, so members from abroad are greatly encouraged to participate.

The event is hosted, as usual, by Timisoara Software Business Incubator (as a new participant, you may find it on this map ).

This is a great opportunity to find out about Scrum's secrets, features and changes over time right from the people that started the Scrum movement around the world and stood up to change the way software was being developed.

The group members representing Sevenbit Solutions will offer participants snacks and drinks, in order to celebrate their company's incubation period graduation.

The event is free of charge.
