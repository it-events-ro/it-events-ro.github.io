How to Hack PR for Your Startup (Free Online Event)

marți, 2 august 2016

PR is one of the most cost effective ways a startup can begin building thought leadership, brand recognition, and a customer base. Unfortunately, many entrepreneurs don't know where to get started or how to deal with journalists - so they hire an expensive PR firm, or ignore PR entirely.

On this free, interactive webinar, two startup PR experts with decades of experience will guide you on how to start leveraging PR at your startup.

This interactive Q&A webinar will be hosted by Adeo Ressi (Founder & CEO of the Founder Institute, 9X entrepreneur and investor), and feature special guest Conrad Egusa.

Conrad is the CEO of Publicize ( http://publicize.co ), a startup aiming to change the way companies approach PR. He has been featured in publications including The Financial Times and Bloomberg, and has been a guest contributor for TechCrunch, VentureBeat and TheNextWeb. Conrad is also a Global Mentor at 500 Startups, The Founder Institute, ANDI (Asociación Nacional de Empresarios de Colombia), and the Spanish accelerator Zarpamos.

During this event, Adeo and Conrad will discuss:

- How to properly strategize your PR efforts
- How to grow yourself as a thought leader
- How to get your company featured - all without the help of a big PR firm.
- How to write an effective press release
- The tips and tricks of dealing with journalists

We hope you will join us!
