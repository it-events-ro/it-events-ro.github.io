PMI Romania Chapter Monthly Meeting - Cluj, August 11 - hosted by 3Pillar Global

joi, 11 august 2016

PMI Romania Chapter has the pleasure of inviting you to a new Monthly Meeting in Cluj, on August 11th 2016, hosted by 3Pillar Global.
Meeting Topic: PM Summer Stories
Agenda
18:15 – 18:45 Registration / Snacks / Networking
18:45 – 19:00 PMI Presentation – Benefits of PMI membership – Cristina Has
19:00 – 19:10 Host company presentation: 3Pillar Global – Dan Suciu
19:10 – 19:40 Presentation PM Summer Stories – Cristian Baciu
Daily life situations can teach us a lot about our future behaviour as project managers. We will share various stories focusing on communication patterns and risks evaluation, how you can act as a consultant and help clients in making the best decisions for their companies and projects.
19:40 – 20:00 Break / Snacks / Networking
20:00 – 20:45 Panel Discussion
20:45 – Networking

Panel Members:
- Cristian Baciu – over 12 years’ experience in the software industry and over 4 years in entrepreneurship and consulting. Currently Senior Engineering Manager at 3Pillar Global. Areas of expertise: project and programme management, business development and risk management.
- Dan Suciu – Director of Global Learning & Development at 3Pillar Global, lecturer at Babes Bolyai University, Mathematics and Informatics Faculty. Over 12 years’ experience in managing software projects of varying size and complexity. Areas of expertise: software project management, Agile methodologies.
- Vlad Panut, PMP – over 9 years in the software industry, currently Unit Manager at Fortech. Areas of expertise: project and programme management and business analysis.
Moderator: Cristina Has, PMP – over 5 years’ experience managing products, projects, programs and portfolios

Location: The Office - Building A, Ground Floor Events Room
