19:00 - 19:15 - Welcome!

19:15 - 19:45 - Daniel Ciocîrlan - A Gentle Intro to Adiabatic Quantum Computation

By now you know that quantum computers behave a little differently than classical ones because of the spooky quantum mechanics.

And those were the conventional quantum computers, based on quantum gates. Adiabatic quantum computers are different, yet again.

We'll talk about how adiabatic computers can solve problems by themselves. And we'll discuss how D-Wave implemented these principles in their D-Wave 1,2 and 2X machines. Finally, we'll solve a real NP-complete problem for the D-Wave.The environment is simulated, but the code is real!
