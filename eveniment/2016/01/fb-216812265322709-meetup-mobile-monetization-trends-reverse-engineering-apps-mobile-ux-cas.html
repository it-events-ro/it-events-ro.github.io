Meetup: Mobile Monetization Trends, Reverse Engineering Apps & Mobile UX case study

marți, 19 ianuarie 2016

Hey all, We are happy to announce our first meetup organized together with our friends from MobileAcademy.ro and TechHub Bucharest on January 19th 2016! The special flavor of this meetup is the mix of Business, Tech & UX Design presentations related to Mobile. We're big fans on interdisciplinary learning and we believe that the best product teams have people that understand each other's areas, so they work better together. Agenda: 18:30 - 19:00 Welcome participants & networking 19:00 - 19:10 Intro 19:10 - 20:40 LIVE STREAMING Meetup: Mobile Monetization Trends, Reverse Engineering Apps & Mobile UX case study Presentations + Q&A 1. Mobile UX case study: Let's Do It, Romania! by Tudor Juravlea - Digital Experience Manager 20:40 - 20:50 Overview Mobile Bootcamp Simplon Romania 20:50 - 21:30 Networking Thanks to Auras Mihai Geambasu & Bülent Duagi from MobileAcademy.ro we will be streaming the presentations from TechHub Bucharest. After that we will have an overview of the Mobile Bootcamp coming up at Simplon in Cluj and beers for the small talks after that. As we're big fans on interdisciplinary learning, we believe that the best product teams have people that understand each other's areas, so they work better together.
