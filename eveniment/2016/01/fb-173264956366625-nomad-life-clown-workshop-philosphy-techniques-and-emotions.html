Talks: Nomad life. - 17:30h. - 20:30h. Clown workshop: Phylosophies, techniques and emotions. You must know: - Spectators aren't allowed, just only participants. - Not limit age (14yo, 30yo, 80yo...). But the games we'll play aren't created to teach children (all games are innocent, of course, but the technique to teach children is different). But it's not a problem if you come with your children. - I will speak in English. - Disabilities aren't a problem. - If you're sad, lonely or you have a cronic illness, come in! You'll feel better.
