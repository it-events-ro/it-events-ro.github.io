RTC 2016 Party [by Frequentis]

joi, 19 mai 2016

#RTC2016's party is brought to you by our partner, Frequentis, and it is addressed to every attendee! Well... THE PARTY is a must this year! In order to break the ice, the first shots are on us. Your access to the party is guaranteed by your badge. So, don't forget about it. Note to yourself: This party is only for 19th of May, also known as Day 1 of #RTC2016. One more reason to be with us at this party: we'll have a girl band! 3 o'clock is having a concert for you! Don't miss the atmosphere. See you on the dance floor!
