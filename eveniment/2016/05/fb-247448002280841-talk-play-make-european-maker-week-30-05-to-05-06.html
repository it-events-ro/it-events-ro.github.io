Talk / Play / Make // European Maker Week /// 30.05 to 05.06

luni, 30 mai 2016

During one week, from 30.05 to 05.06 Cluj Makers will host a series of workshops, debates and projections related to the Maker Movement. Here's what we're planning: ///////////////////////////////////////// Monday 18:00 - Opening Event - Creative industries & Maker movement - talk & networking event hosted by Fabrica de Pensule & ARIES - Asociatia Romana pentru Industria Electronica si Software Tuesday 16:30 - Launching Little Geeks - Robotics for kids Wednesday 18:00 - Maker Cafe - 3D Printing workshop 19:00 - Plastic Recycling - talk Thursday 18:30 - Make a Drone - workshop Friday 18:30 - Introduction to Arduino Class Saturday 15:00 - Open Doors - Repair Cafe Sunday 14:00 - Makers Documentary - projection 16:00 - Barbeque Party //////////////////////////////////////////// This events are part of the European Maker Week, an initiative promoted by European Commission. It's a celebration of makers and innovators all over Europe. The workshops and meetings will take place at Cluj Makers on Traian Street 40-42. The entrance is free based on reservation on the website http://events.europeanmakerweek.eu/events/view/476
