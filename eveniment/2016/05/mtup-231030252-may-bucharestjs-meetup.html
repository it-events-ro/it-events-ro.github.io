May BucharestJS Meetup

miercuri, 25 mai 2016

We're proud to announce that this month BucharestJS has joined Bucharest Technology Week - a movement that reunites tech enthusiasts and leaders for one week of gatherings across the city. After similar events in London, New York and Berlin to name a few, this event puts Bucharest on the international tech map.

Here are the exciting topics our speakers will cover at May BucharestJS Meetup:

• JS Design Patterns and Reactive Programing by Adrian Badarau, Web Developer @Avangate


• Creating App-like Experiences with Progressive Web Apps by Alexandru Bularca, Web Tools Team Lead at EveryMatrix

Don't miss this one and feel free to invite 1-2 friends that might be interested in joining the community. Make sure to find us on Facebook ( https://www.facebook.com/groups/BucharestJS ) and Twitter ( http://www.twitter.com/bucharestjs ).
