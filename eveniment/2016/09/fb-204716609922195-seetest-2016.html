SEETEST 2016

joi, 15 septembrie 2016

After the great success of SEETEST conference in Sofia, Bulgaria we are pleased to present to you the Fifth South East European Software Testing Conference – SEETEST 2016, which will take place in Bucharest, Romania on September 15-16, 2016. SEETEST 2016 is organized by SEETB, Quality House Ltd. and ANIS. On September 15th and 16th, leading experts will come to the Romanian capital to share the latest news, practical and research insights, experiences and advances in software quality and testing. Use your chance to meet the experts and VIPs in the software quality business – during the Conference or at the Social Event. As the tradition goes, the conference program will be divided into two days – one day of tutorials (15 September) and one day of keynote speeches and presentations (16 September). At the exhibition, which will be running both days, international companies working in the software quality field will demonstrate their tools and services. See you in Bucharest!
