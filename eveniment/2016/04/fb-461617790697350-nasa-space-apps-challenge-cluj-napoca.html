NASA Space Apps Challenge Cluj-Napoca

sâmbătă, 23 aprilie 2016

Welcome to the largest hackathon ever held! NASA's Space Apps is an international hackathon that occurs over 48 hours in 193 cities from 72 countries around the world. Although the competition reached its fifth edition this year, for the first time it's being held in Romania. Startup Transilvania Entrepreneurship and Inovation Center was selected as NASA's local collaborator to organize the event in Cluj-Napoca, the only location in Romania where it takes place. The event is supported by Cluj Hub, Tech Academy, SprintPoint Techcelerator and the Romanian Space Agency (ROSA). We expect 100 participants. Please use this link to register: https://2016.spaceappschallenge.org/locations/cluj-napoca-romania. Also, this is our official page for the competition, where you will find useful information about the six themes, the challenges, the judging criteria and the schedule. Join us to share ideas and engage with NASA's open data to address real-world problems, on Earth and in space. Work alone or with a team to solve challenges that could help change the world. https://2016.spaceappschallenge.org/
