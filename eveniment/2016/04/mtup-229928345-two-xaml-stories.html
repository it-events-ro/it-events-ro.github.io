Two XAML Stories

miercuri, 13 aprilie 2016

XAML entered our .NET lives in several ways and, like it or not, we made many decisions on how and when to use it. It was key to our Silverlight projects and now we use it also for the Universal Windows Platform.

Moving from Silverlight to a more “pure web” HTML5 application model, or building the next app for any Windows 10 device has real life challenges.

We want to show you how we did it in 2 examples. From the real life, no marketing, no snake oil.

Join us for the following stories:

* Romulus Crisan will show how he actually build a first mobile app with UWP

* Zoltan Balasz will talk about what it really takes to replace Silverlight with HTML technologies


Last, but not least, Radu Vunvulea & Tudor Damian will share with us the novelties that this edition of ITCamp brings.

***

Building my first mobile app with UWP ( language: Romanian)

Nowadays all sorts of devices are popping up into our lives and being a developer I’m intrigued by the idea of customizing or building apps for this type of devices. As you all know perfectly our time is limited and since I make my living out of Microsoft technology until recently the only real option to tinker with this devices was to learn a new technology. Personally I loved the idea of learning something completely new but my boss and my family decide against it. Last year Microsoft launched the new Universal Windows Platform and I thought that this is my “breakout” ticket since I already know most of the technology, or at least I thought I knew it … .

So in this session I want to share my experience on building my first mobile application using Microsoft Universal Windows Platform. To be more specific I will talk about tools, data binding and layouts.


About the speaker:

Romulus is a Language Platform Evangelist at SDL. He has experience in Software Development, Enterprise Architecture, Information Exchange, LOB and Digital Media applications working for global enterprises.

Learn more about Romulus from his blog: http://romuluscrisan.com/blog/index.html

Replacing Silverlight with HTML technologies (30-45 min, language: Romanian)

Browser plug-ins are on their way out. In this presentation we will talk about different HTML application types (SPA and traditional apps), JavaScript frameworks that we’ve evaluated - advantages and disadvantages of it. Focusing on our needs we will show what framework we’ve arrived at, with practical examples. This won’t be a deep dive, but it might help you to choose the right framework / library for your next project.


About the speaker:

Zoltan is a Senior Developer at SDL, building products used by over[masked] users worldwide both from the desktop and on servers, both individual persons and international companies. Hard earned code battle badges enable him to be pragmatic and he will share what we have looked at, what were the issues we faced and what we arrived at when it comes to Silverlight replacement in a real project
