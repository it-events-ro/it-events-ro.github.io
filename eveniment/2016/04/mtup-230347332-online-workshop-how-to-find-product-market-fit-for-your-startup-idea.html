Online Workshop: How to Find Product-Market Fit for Your Startup Idea

marți, 19 aprilie 2016

This is an interactive, online event that you can participate in from your computer. To join and get the access link, you must register at https://fifitwest.splashthat.com .

----

With so much uncertainty, and so many questions to answer, validating your startup ideas can be overwhelming. Justin Wilcox, one of Founder Institute's most popular and highly rated mentors, has spent the last 18 months solving this problem.

Combining the best of Customer Development, Design Thinking, Lean Startup, and Growth Hacking, Justin's "FOCUS Framework" ( http://j.mp/_F0CUS ) lays out an actionable path to validating your startup ideas, and achieving "Product-Market Fit."

On this interactive online workshop, Justin will guide you through a step-by-step process to outline your ideas, test your riskiest assumptions, and run valid startup experiments to find your Product-Market Fit.

You'll leave the workshop with a clear, actionable, next step - and a practical strategy for finding your Product-Market Fit.
