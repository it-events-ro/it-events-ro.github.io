Product Tank meetup at VeInteractive. Do you talk Product?

miercuri, 27 aprilie 2016

#8 #ProductTankCluj | Join us April 27, 6:30 pm. This time we make it Interactive, Ve Interactive. :) It’s an event by Product Managers, for Product Managers, to discuss Product Management! To attend, please sign up on the meetup page. Our guest speakers will be: Cristian Cazan - Development Director Romania @VeInteractive Toma Matei - Head Of Commercial, Eastern Europe @ VeInteractive Ana Oarga - Product Manager @ VeInteractive Camelia Gui - Product Manager @ VeInteractive Agenda: 1. Networking & Snacks 2. Speakers 3. Roundtable discussion 4. Further Networking & Snacks Food and drinks will be served and we need your RSVP also for logistics. P.S. If you are interested in hosting/sponsoring future events, please hit the ‘Message’ button. We are inviting everyone in this community of product enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, product owners and innovators in Cluj-Napoca and Transylvania to join a discussion on what are the latest developments, challenges and opportunities in innovative product development and management. Ve Interactive is a world-leading technology company whose platform has become an essential component for any online business to acquire, engage and convert customers. Formed in 2009, Ve has gone on to become National Champion representing the United Kingdom in the 2013/4 European Business Awards, Digital Champion in the Growing Business Awards 2014, Gold medal winner for Software Company of the Year in the Stevie International Business Awards 2013, winner of a Data Strategy Award 2014 and a European Business Award for Best Practice 2014. Under the vision and steerage of its co founder & CEO, the award-winning serial entrepreneur David J. Brown, the company has since gone on to become one of the most exciting and proactive technology companies in the e-commerce space.
