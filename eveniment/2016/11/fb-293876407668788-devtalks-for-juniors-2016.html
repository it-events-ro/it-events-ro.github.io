DevTalks for Juniors 2016

sâmbătă, 12 noiembrie 2016

DevTalks for Juniors is the small sister event of DevTalks Romania. Located in Bucharest, we propose you a practical approach to the junior's learning process. On 12 November we will meet for a one day event designed to reach top juniors motivated to continuously learn and invest in their personal and professional development. This is an amazing event designed for showcasing powerful technologies and how they impact our IT Community and this will be the most fun and thrilling event of the Autumn. With a dynamic and ﬂexible Agenda that includes conferences and seminars, one week before DevHacks, we aim to build great know-how for dealing with current school or working challenges. 200 juniors with no more than 2 years experiences in the IT&C ﬁeld 2 large tracks covering Embedded & IoT and Web & Mobile 2 workshop rooms that will emphasize the best out of current technologies such as Java, .NET, PHP, C++/C# and much more 3 internationals that will participate in shaping the future of the IT Community. Your involvement should include: - Instruments that a junior can access on developing and evaluating his professionals skills; - Knowledge and information about current & new technologies; - Inspiration from successful leaders and support in their learning process; Join our forces to shape the next IT Community generation!
