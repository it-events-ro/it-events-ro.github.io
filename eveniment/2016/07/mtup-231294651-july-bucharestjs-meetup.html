July BucharestJS Meetup

miercuri, 27 iulie 2016

This is going to be a different kind of meeting: we're going to talk about JS communities around the world in general and how we, as JS developers, can interact and collaborate not only at the local level but across communities, thus having a global impact.

This is a " NO JS CODE " meetup and everybody that wants to get involved and contribute to a growing JS community is welcome to attend.

We're going to have a short interactive presentation on Global JS Communities delivered by Ciprian Borodescu, Organizer at BucharestJS.

Don't miss this one and feel free to invite 1-2 friends that might be interested in joining the community. Make sure to find us on Facebook ( https://www.facebook.com/groups/BucharestJS ) and Twitter ( http://www.twitter.com/bucharestjs ).

Link: http://www.meetup.com/BucharestJS/events/231294651/
