Open Day @ClujHUB

vineri, 1 iulie 2016

Are you a freelancer and working from home doesn’t work for you anymore? Do you own a small company or startup and you are looking to find new clients and to grow your income? Or maybe you are an employee who wants to try a new setup, more neutral and casual, for those planning meetings? If you find yourself in one of the situations above the answer you’ve been looking for is ClujHUB – the coworking space in the business district of Cluj. You are invited to the ClujHUB Open Day on Friday July 1st, from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm. Please join us to view the facilities, meet our team, enjoy some refreshments and a barbecue in the garden at lunch time (1:00 pm). Come and claim your discount voucher. You get 50% off on the first month subscription (offer available until September 30, 2016) or, two monts subscription at the price of one if you bring a friend for the both of you. It is proven that coworking improves your work life balance, helps you expand your personal and professional network, helps you find new clients and makes you more productive. But don’t take our word for it, come and see for yourself! Join us for a day, enjoy the amenities ClujHUB has to offer, meet our resident hubsters and our team! Be a happy hubster for a day! Please help us provide you an excellent experience by registering here: https://en.xing-events.com/open-day-cluj-hub.html
