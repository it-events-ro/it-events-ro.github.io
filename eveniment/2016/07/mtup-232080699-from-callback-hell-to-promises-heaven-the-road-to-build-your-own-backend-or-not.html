From callback hell to promises heaven (EN, Intermediate-Advanced level)

This presentation aims to shed some light on the topic of using promises in our codebase and what are the advantages of doing that. Callback-driven design is not without its shortcomings and understanding those, means we can make better and more informed decisions about the paradigms we should adopt throughout our code.

"Callback hell" as we know it is more than just indentation. It's about inversion of control (IOC) and conceding to 3rd party code the decision of HOW to continue the flow of your applications. This is one of the most important drawbacks that promises solve in an elegant way and by providing us with an easy to use API. Although not the silver bullet to the async issues, promises are definitely a step forward. Using them will result in a cleaner codebase and happier developers.
