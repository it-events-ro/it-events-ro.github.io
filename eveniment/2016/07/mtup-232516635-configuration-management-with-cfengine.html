Configuration Management with CFEngine

marți, 26 iulie 2016

Curious about CFEngine or just want to join us for a discussion about daily life as a DevOps? Join us Tuesday, 26 July for another meetup! Same time, same location. See you soon ;-)

Agenda:

18:30 - Registration and welcome coffee

19:00 - "Configuration Management with CFEngine" by Daniel Stan - Discover how CFEngine, one of the oldest configuration management tools around (it was created in 1993, long before DevOps was a thing!), can be used, how it compares to other similar solutions, what benefits it can bring in our daily jobs, as well as the ugly sides of it.

19:45 - Coffee break and networking

20:00 - "Challenges, expectations and requirements" - Open discussion about the challenges we're facing at our jobs, what are the expectations of our peers and what are the current job requirements, both from the technical and social perspective.

20:30 - Beerfest
