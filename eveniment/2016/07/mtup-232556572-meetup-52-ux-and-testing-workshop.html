Meetup #52 - UX and Testing Workshop

miercuri, 20 iulie 2016

Hello everyone,

Usability is a complex domain, it's not about testing an interface, is about making that interface a familiar place for user. Usability is like an umbrella, including a lot of other domains (as design theory, neuroscience, psychology etc.) which will help testers to better understand the user. So, let's find out how to understand the user and make the Internet a better place for spending our time and doing our activities.

In this interactive workshop held by Andreea Popescu we'll do user group exercises, so we will work in teams, to discover the foundation of usability that we can apply to our projects, further remains to adapt the audit/testing to your specific project.


In this workshop you will discover:

• What is usability and why is necessary for a software project;

• How an usability audit will be influenced by cultural background?

• Which are the basic questions to put when you start an usability audit?

Notes:

- each team will need a laptop or two!

- please show up at 7 PM to ensure a smooth and proper start of the event

- please mention if you do not wish to be photographed or have your picture posted online


Thank you!
