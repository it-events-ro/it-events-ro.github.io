Freelancers Community Meetup - Accounting and legal aspects

joi, 7 iulie 2016

About the speaker:

Hello Freelancers,

Because we had an amazing experience last time and received such a positive feedback from you, we are looking forward for the next meeting. The topics on the agenda are of general interest: the accounting and legal aspects of a freelancer's activity.

Please join us for a very dynamic and interesting edition and make sure to prepare your questions in advance for the two guest speakers!

Agenda:

Accounting aspects to be considered by any freelancer

About the speaker:

Adrian Beşleagă (Operations Manager at A&I Consulting) is guided by the principle that there is a positive solution to any problem. He likes to work with people and he is motivated by the prospect of learning new things. Adrian enjoys a good challenge and testing his skills.


http://www.aiconsulting.ro/

Legal aspects to be considered by any freelancers

About the speaker:

Horea Gorde (Attorney at Law) has almost 10 years of experience in civil law cases (including relationships between professionals / traders, debt collection, insolvency proceedings, employment, insurance, real estate, intellectual property, tax law, non-banking financial institutions, procurement etc.).

Worried that you might not make it in time for the meetup? Fear not, you are our guest and can work from the HUB the entire day, for free.
