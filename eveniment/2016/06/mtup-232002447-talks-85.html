Talks #85

miercuri, 22 iunie 2016

We don’t know about you guys, but we love learning new things from old speakers.

We think most of you are already familiar with Mihai Maruseac but for those of you who haven’t met him yet, here are a few words: he just came back to Romania from Boston, where he is a Research Assistant and he has been attending and supporting Softbinator Events since 2013.

Our second speaker is Radu Amarie, Co-Founder & CTO of Softbinator. When he doesn’t sleep, he sure has something to do with programming.

We believe that it will be a great edition and we hope that you join us.

Here’s the agenda for Talks #85:

19:00 - 19:15  - Welcome!

19:15 - 19:45 - Mihai Maruseac → Reinforcement Learning. Learning from Adversarial Environment.

How does a child learn? The simplest answer to this question is that he learns based on results/rewards/punishments generated by procedures that he does. Inspired by this response, AI appeared in Reinforcement Learning (Learning through Rewards), a topic closely related to Monte Carlo algorithms that allowed the creation of an AI Backgammon.

19:45 - 19:50 - Break

19:50 - 20:20 -  Radu-Sebastian Amarie → Still using MySQL? Maybe you should reconsider - A short overview over graph databases - Neo4j in particular.

20:20  – Q&A - Networking! :D

Price ticket: IT&C knowledge, tech enthusiasm, beer fan and a big amount of positive energy!
