Gaming Night

vineri, 3 iunie 2016

Join us for an amazing Gaming Night, organized by Tech Academy and hosted by ClujHUB, on June 3, starting with 07:00 pm. We're talking real business: LAN Party, console (PlayStation, XBOX, Wii), virtual reality devices, board games, mobile games, poker table. During the evening we set apart a 2 hours time slot (from 9:00 pm to 11:00 pm) for a Game jam consisting of 4 moderated talks. The games are provided by our friends at Thron Games Iulius Mall and cutia.ro. The evening will be completed by a beer & barbecue party in ClujHUB's garden. Please help us organize an awesome event by registering here: https://en.xing-events.com/Gaming-night-Cluj-HUB Life is a game, play it!
