Call to arms

joi, 16 iunie 2016

Hello clu js ers!

We started Cluj JavaScripters as JS meetup aimed to bring people with similar interests together and we've been doing regular meetups for some time, but we want to aim higher. Our goal is to really become a major community, to bring people from different companies and with different levels of expertise together so that the learning experience is enhanced for everyone.

Which should be the next steps for our community? Would it be hard to put Cluj on the map as a JavaScript hub? We would like to do that, but we need your help. We need to get everyone involved. We have big plans for the community, but let's start small.


We've been thinking for the past weeks to try and gather ideas for future meetups from everyone in the community and we want to meet this time and simply propose topics/ideas that can be scheduled in the coming months. We would like you to really get involved here and propose any topics which are of interest to you or which you would like to share with the community. Together with this process, we will try to brainstorm ideas about the future of JavaScript in Cluj and the impact that we can have on both locally and internationally.
