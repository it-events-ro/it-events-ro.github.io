Freelacers Community Kick Off Meeting

joi, 9 iunie 2016

We created this meetup in order to resume the monthly meetings we used to organize at ClujHUB, as a platform for the freelance community to grow a friendly and professional network, bringing together freelancers from different industries and resuming. The environment is intended for feedback and collaboration, so come with something to share. Whether you’re stuck on a problem, want to celebrate a recent success, or just need to be inspired by other freelancers, this is a great space to connect. So let's meet, network, have a snack, a cup of coffee/tea and find more creative ways for members to interact and finally share your experience as freelancers. About the speakers: Roxana Nasoi is Upwork Mobilizer, Founder at SERPlified and Co-Founder at RemOath, You can find more about her here: https://about.me/roxanasoi Codin Pop is co-founder of Creative Monkeyz (http://creativemonkeyz.com/) and, together with Ramona Cordoș, the creative behind RObotzi. Worried that you might not make it in time for the meetup? Fear not, you are our guest and can work from the HUB the entire day, for free.
