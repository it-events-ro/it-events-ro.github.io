hello.js launching event

joi, 18 februarie 2016

We are really excited to announce that our launching event will be hosted on 18 & 19 February 2016, in Cluj-Napoca, at Grnd Hotel Italia. Save the date, prepare your talk & start thinking about the awesome IoT or Robotics project you're going to showcase at this event.


Check out all the event updates on www.hellojs.org


Join the very first ‪#‎hellojs‬ line-up of speakers & demo teams! Demo teams? Yes, you read it right, this is going to be fun!

Featured  Tom Dale (helped create Ember.JS, co-founder @ Tilde.Inc), Gautam Arora (Tech Manager @ Condé Nast), Emil Tamas (Co-founder @ Moqups) Zahra Jabini (Bustle.com) and Andrei Rusu (Nightwatch.js) at our launching event!

What’s in all about?

The conference features 2 full days of program, the first day is planned for talks, and on the 2nd day we say hello(.js) to the brave teams who’re going to showcase their work during the Demo Day.

Talks, teams & projects are going to be announced on February 1st, 2016!

For details and updates, please visit our website Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn or Meetup pages. Let the helloJS frenzy start!
