OpenStack Bucharest Meetup 2016.1 - first meeting this year!

joi, 25 februarie 2016

Cloudbase Solutions and Skylab Startup Development would like to invite you to the first OpenStack Meetup in Bucharest in 2016.

We'd like to have a regular OpenStack meetup in Bucharest and we hope to build a strong community around OpenStack, so please help us spread the word and extend this invitation to any and all OpenStack enthusiasts out there, developers and OpenStack users alike.

We have 3 interesting topics in store for you, ranging from OpenStack in education, software defined networking and interoperability with Windows.

Full Agenda: 18:30 - 18:40 Welcoming the guests. Short networking session before the event starts.

18:40 - 19:10 Eduard Luchian (Skylab Startup Development): "OpenStack from NFV to SDN"

19:10 - 19:20 Coffee Break 1

19:20 - 19:50 Razvan Dobre (UPB, Adobe): "OpenStack in education"

19:50 - 20:00 Coffee Break 2

20:00 - 20:30 Sergiu Strat (Cloudbase Solutions): "OpenStack and Windows"

Coffee and snacks are on us. See you all at the event!
