Daniel Tamas , Idea Studios Transylgamia Jellycs

Daniel T ămaș - Entrepreneur, Founder of Idea Studios, Transylgamia and Jellycs, monetization expert, frenchie lover

An entrepreneur for the past 9 years, Daniel Tamas is behind three of the most interesting names in the local gaming industry: Idea Studios - one of the largest independent gaming studios in Romania, mobile game development and publisher company Transylgamia and gaming marketing company Jellycs. Together with his business partners, he has turned his passion for game production and development into a success model and is now part of a team that creates games for millions of people all around the world. A key secret? Curiosity: Daniel is constantly on the look-out for new technologies and creative ways to deliver top results for advertisers and relevant content for users.

Daniel regularly shares his experience and the lessons learnt as an entrepreneur on his blog http://danieltamas.ro . He’s also very happy to connect with you on LinkedIn and Facebook .

What is Startup Grind ?

Startup Grind is a global startup community designed to educate, inspire, and connect entrepreneurs.
