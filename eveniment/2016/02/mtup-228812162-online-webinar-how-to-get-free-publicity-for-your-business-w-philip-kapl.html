Publicity can provide any business with a massive advantage.. but how can you get it for free as a startup? Join this webinar to learn how to generate buzz for your company, how to work with reporters, and the latest strategies for getting publicity, from two serial entrepreneurs who have both been featured on some of the world's largest publications (for free).

This webinar will be hosted by Adeo Ressi (Founder & CEO of the Founder Institute), and feature special guest Philip Kaplan.

Philip has been a serial entrepreneur since the 90s, creating massive press for himself while starting companies like Adbrite (one of the world’s pioneering online ad networks), Tiny Letter (a free newsletter service acquired by MailChimp), and F**ked Company (a massively popular parody site that chronicled the dot.com crash in the early 2000s). Currently, he is the Founder & CEO of DistroKid (a service that musicians use to get their music into iTunes, Spotify, and other digital music stores).

On this webinar you will learn:

- How to generate buzz for yourself, or your business/ startup

- How to work effectively with reporters

- The latest strategies for getting free publicity

We hope you will join us!
