Business Model Canvas Intro-Workshop by SprintPoint Techcelerator

sâmbătă, 6 februarie 2016

Startup Europe Week has been created to include all the european regions in a startup focused program. Many global initiatives have been created to celebrate entrepreneurship. Typically, such events follow the Silicon Valley format (keynote motivational speeches, networking sessions etc). But Europe is different and requires something more when it comes to starting a business. Regional diversity and local policy making play a big role in shaping European startups. European regions are key players, from helping entrepreneurs to incorporate a company, to providing grants or taxes breaks. Come and join us for our Business Model Canvas Intro-Workshop organised by SprintPoint Techcelerator & Startup Transilvania, You will learn about the nine building blocks on the map, discuss, and learn business design to help you on your next startup. Work with fellow innovators and entrepreneurial enthusiasts to dissect established companies before evaluating your own ventures. Hear from our accelerator examples about the necessary business thinking needed in order to succeed. Admission is FREE for registered participants of Startup Europe Week in Cluj. Light refreshments will be provided by our ClujHUB hosts. Please register through this event: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/business-model-canvas-intro-workshop-by-sprintpoint-techcelerator-tickets-21379334164 Our facilitator is Mark Filipas, SprintPoint Techcelerator Coordinator, being assisted by Tudor Pasc, events coordinator at Startup Transilvania. The main organizer is Startup Europe Week, who provides all the guidelines and support for the event, support and marketing materials.
