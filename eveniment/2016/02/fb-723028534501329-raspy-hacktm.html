Raspy HackTM

sâmbătă, 20 februarie 2016

Haters gonna hate, makers gonna make! ...this time with Raspberry Pi and Windows 10 IoT Core and Azure. We are inviting at least 30 of the IoT passionate people to join us in a one day hands-on event that will have in focus the development of innovative projects that will involve at least one piece of Raspberry Pi and will use Window 10 IoT Core or Azure IoT Hub or the Azure IoT suite . Additionally to this compulsory elements, participants are encouraged to pour in the tech magic hat any other of their own collection of pieces of hardware, software, sensorics, actuators, etc. needed to produce the most "wow" projects. We are encouraging participants to create teams of maximum 3 members that will compete for winning the Timisoara Golden Raspberry Trophy and, of course, some tech awards that we'll announce as the event is getting closer. We'll embed with each of the registered teams one of the Timisoara Metropolitan Area CoderDojos mavericks to contribute to respective team's project and also to learn. The event benefits by the gratious help of Microsoft. The organizers will offer the food and the beverages needed to fuel the making enthusiasm.
