Launching of Innovation Labs Programme 2016 in Sibiu

luni, 29 februarie 2016

Passionate about technology? Join us at the launching event of Innovation Labs 2016 programme in Sibiu! The event is an introduction to the Innovation Labs programme and a preparation for the Hackathon that will be held in Sibiu on 12 - 13 March. At the launching event on the 29th, you will meet the mentors, find out more information about what a hackathon is and how to prepare for it. With the programme’s presentation, we also organize an innovative ideas contest - Idea Jam! 1) You will form teams on the spot 2) Develop an innovative idea of a technological product 3) All teams will present their ideas 4) The team with the best presentation will win prizes offered by Sony and Fitbit Flex More about Innovation Labs on our website: www.innovationlabs.ro or facebook: www.fb.com/ilabsro . ▬ Agenda: 1. Presentation and registrations launch for the Innovation Labs 2016 Sibiu Hackathon 2. Innovation ideas contest - Idea Jam ▬ Contest Schedule: - During the first hour there will be a presentation and a mentoring session on ideas held by the Innovation Labs team and the programme’s mentors - Forming teams for the Idea Jam - In the ideas contest, 2-4 members teams will each present an innovation idea of a technological product - In the end, one representative of each team will present a pitch in English of maximum 3 minutes. The pitch needs to include the following topics: the innovative idea, the problem which it addresses, the proposed solution, the target audience, the team - The winner will be the team with the most interesting pitch Idea Jam duration: approximately 2 hours. ▬ Prizes: - 1st prize: Sony MDR-V150 Headsets for the entire team - 1 prize in the tombola - fitness tracker Fitbit Flex Extras: pizza & drinks ________ The jury will be formed of the Innovation Labs Sibiu team and programme mentors. They will vote based on the following criteria: * clarity in defining the problem * clarity in defining the solution * clarity in defining the differentiator * the market * the team (how well does it fit with the product’s requirements)
