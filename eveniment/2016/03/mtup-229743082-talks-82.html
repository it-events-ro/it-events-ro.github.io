We know you’ve been asking about this and we’re sorry for our mysterious and unexpected disappearance but we’re back! Let’s warm up this cold spring with a new & hot edition of Talks by Softbinator :D Don’t forget to RSVP!


Here’s the agenda for Talks #82:


19:00 - 19:15  - Welcome!


19:15 - 19:45 - Adrian Oprea → There's garbage in our code

Let’s face it, you’ve written your code well, you have a style guide and you even wrote tests. Still, your code seems to work against you and insists on not performing well. If this sounds like you, let’s talk about the beast that eats into your application performance. Instead of trying to fight it, we’re going to try to understand it, and see how it makes its decisions. The beast we’ll be talking about is the garbage collector and we’re going to learn how it works and when/why it decides to take its toll on you.


19:45 - 19:50 - Break


19:50 - 20:20 - Victor Drâmbă → Hotword detection study based on Google's TensorFlow


Or how I learned a new programming language - Python, and the basic notions of Machine Learning using TensorFlow from Google.

- Why hotword? What it is and why use it. "Ok Google", "Hey Siri", "Alexa"
- What is machine learning. Common examples and tutorials freely available on the internet.
- Feature extraction
- Choosing an algorithm - the structure of a Neural Network.
- Training the net
- Using the algorithm in production without TensorFlow.


20:20  – Q&A - Networking! :D
