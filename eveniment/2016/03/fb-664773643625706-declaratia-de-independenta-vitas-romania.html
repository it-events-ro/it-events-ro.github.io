Declaratia de independenta - Vitas Romania

luni, 21 martie 2016

Timisoara Startup Hub and Vitas IFN Romania are joining forces once again in sustaining startups! Timisoara Startup Hub and Vitas Romania are organizing an event dedicated to young Timisoara Area entrepreneurs and entrepreneur-wannabes focusing on finance sources for startups. During the 2-3 hours event, participants will learn about financing sources for a startup, how to create and sustain a business plan and the importace of financial calculations for starting businesses. Aditionally, Vitas Romania will present the 2nd edition of the Declaration of Independence business plan contest, endowed with a 20.000 RON prize and opened to startups younger than 6 months or to teams just aiming to register a new company. All participants at the event can participate into a short Business Plan workshop, awarded with a surprise prize (it's no surprize actually – it's food for the mind!). The program of the evening will include: 18.30 - Participants’ registration 19.00 - Event opening What's New in 2016 regarding startup financing - Radu Ticiu Declaratia de independenta Business Plan Competition by Vitas Romania - Adrian Garlonta 19.30 Break & Networking 20.00 - Using Business Canvas for Business Planning Workshop 20.45 Networking 21.15 Raffle winner announcement 21.30 Conclusion of the event The participation to the event is free, but in order to secure your place you have to register here: http://bit.ly/vitasreg.
