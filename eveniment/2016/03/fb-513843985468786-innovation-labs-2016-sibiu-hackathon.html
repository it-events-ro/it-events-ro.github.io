Innovation Labs 2016 Sibiu Hackathon

sâmbătă, 12 martie 2016

Welcome to the largest tech startup competition and mentorship program in Romania! Pitch your idea in 1 minute in front of the public. Inspire the audience, find team members and go on to build your prototype during the 24 hour Hackathon - with mentorship and lots of coffee. At the end, pitch your prototype in front of the jury, renowned IT professionals, business gurus, tech-angels – and keep your fingers crossed for the selection. Join us now and take your first step as an entrepreneur in the Innovation Labs Hackathon, on the 12th of March! Register now on (Limited number of tickets available): https://www.amiando.com/InnovationLabs2016_Sibiu.html Find out more on our website: http://www.innovationlabs.ro/ The Innovation Labs 2016 program in Sibiu is organized by TechLounge and Fundatia Comunitara Sibiu in academic partnership with Lucian Blaga University of Sibiu. The programme is implemented in partnership with Orange, Carrefour and The Romanian-American Foundation.
