Spring Meetup - Ladies Taking The Stage

marți, 22 martie 2016

BucharestJS, Frontend Bucharest and JS Girls present Spring Meetup - where the Ladies take the stage. This is one of the many special editions aimed at encouraging more and more people to come and take the stage at local development meetups.

1. Face recognition in JS by Diana Miron

Diana is both a frontend dev and illustrator. Her main focus is to teach kids coding and to encourage girls to take a path on JS programming.

2. OpenData.js by Maria Niță

Maria does devops, loves open source and is passionate about numbers and letters.

3. Flexbox, the trusted toolbox by Raluca Stan

Raluca is a frontend developer who currently works with React and enjoys writing ES6.

Don't miss this one and feel free to invite 1-2 friends that might be interested in joining the meetup. Make sure to find us on Facebook ( https://www.facebook.com/groups/BucharestJS ) and Twitter ( http://www.twitter.com/bucharestjs ).
