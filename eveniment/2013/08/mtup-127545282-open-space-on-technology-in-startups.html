Open Space on Technology in Startups

sâmbătă, 17 august 2013

Why?
Find a meeting place for founders , technical people , investors and other interested people.
Discuss and present your achievements and ideas.
Shout your problems to find solutions from other experienced people.

You will have fun , meet other people interested in domains like startups, business, technical startups, learn new things from each other in an informal atmosphere.

How?
Open Space was started in the 1980s by Harrison Owen, who used to organize conferences in a traditional format. He decided not to create an agenda, but invite people, and everyone would create the agenda on the spot.
During Open Space gatherings the agenda is created by the audience by writing discussion ideas on paper and sticking them on the wall. This wall is called The Market Place , as in people go there to buy ideas they want to discuss.

In this spirit we invite you to join the Open Space . Please think before the event what you would like to talk about and we will have an awesome time!

It is very important for us to share the knowledge we gathered, so please prepare to tweet , write blog posts , write articles , take pictures , or do anything crazy for this purpose.

You will experience new ideas, concepts and meet interesting people with same interests.

Format
30 min Intro, describe the Open Space concept.
30 min The audience creates the Market Place
Self-organize to set-up the room: chairs, tables, flip-charts, projector(s), etc.
Discuss the ideas on the Market Place during 45 minute sessions
Retrospective at the end of the day: share your findings.

This event will be in English if we have non-Romanian speakers.

This event will be facilitated by Adi Bolboaca

Remember to think about what you would like to discuss, learn or share.

Prepare to be amazed!
