Keeping the Startup Weekend Spirit Alive!

joi, 18 aprilie 2013

The second edition of Startup Weekend Cluj brought a lot of new people in the Startup Community! So, we want to invite you to an informal meeting for sharing insights, ideas and updates between participants of both events organized in Cluj Napoca. Mainly we will talk about: What did the StartupWeekend event mean for you? Which startup ideas presented at the event are going further in the startup world? And most important we will NETWORK. Food and drinks are on us See you there!
