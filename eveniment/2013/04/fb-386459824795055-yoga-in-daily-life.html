Yoga in Daily Life

vineri, 5 aprilie 2013

To participate sign up here: http://timisoara.incubator107.com/atelier/Breasla%20Mi%C8%99c%C4%83torilor/780 What: The workshop will share various practically applicable 'Yogic Capsules' which can help us to relax, rejuvenate and reconnect to our inner self. Various yogic practices will be taught which can positively transform our life without making big compromises to our usual lifestyles. Yoga is undoubtedly one of the few systems of 'Holistic Wellness' which has passed the acid test of time and has offered multiple solutions to manage the thick and thins of our day to day life. In todays world when stress has almost become 'a natural state of being' and we are squeezed against time, its even harder to introduce new habits or make major lifestyle changes to achieve external or internal equilibrium. With whom: I, Sn Mangaldharma (better known as Avi), have been practicing Yoga since my early childhood. I was blessed to graduate from Bihar School of Yoga (which is one of the India's oldest Yoga schools) and did my Yoga teacher's training from Sivananda Ashram, Kerala, India. In the past years I have conducted wellness workshops for different governmental and non-governmental organisations and offered yoga classes for citizens of different nationalities during my stay in Hong Kong, Macau, Philippines, China, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Mauritius, India & Romania. I also have diploma in Ayurvedic Massage,Thai Foot Reflexology, Reiki & First Aid. With what/ pre-workshop • Comfortable clothes to be able to make some easy movements i.e sit in crossed legged position. • Avoid eating a heavy meal 2 hrs prior to the workshop. • Bring a Yoga mat or a camping mat. • Remember – It’s not physical flexibility that is the prerequisite for Yoga, it’s the mental flexibility. Workshop participation: 10 ron, half for the trainer and the other half for the project.
