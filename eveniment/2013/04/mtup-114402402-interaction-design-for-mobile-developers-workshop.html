Interaction Design for Mobile Developers Workshop

sâmbătă, 20 aprilie 2013

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Please make sure that you are also filling in the registration form here . </a>

What:

a half-day hands-on workshop conducted by Raul Adonis Raduca

When:

Saturday, April 20th,[masked] PM

Where:

Timisoara Startup Hub, City Business Centre, D Building, 10th C. Brediceanu St, Timisoara ( <a href="http://goo.gl/maps/RSwAZ">map )

Participants:

current mobile app developers and people considering to tackle this field, starting from high-school students getting their first steps in programming for Android to seasoned software professionals considering extending their competence areas towards the highly demanded mobile development, being it iOS or Blackberry.

IMPORTANT NOTE: A number of up to 20 participants, in the order they are registering on this event-page, could attend

Details:

Through this workshop we are aiming to bring into discussion and then practice the principles of interaction design and the role and importance of an user centered design approach to create successful mobile apps that sell.

Adonis will use an event format that will rely mostly on practical work in pairs, having as starting point most probably the real case of an app dedicated to business persons. The main tools to be use by participants will be mechanical pencils, paper and eraser, rather than graphical tablet or laptops.

Following the workshop, those interested will be encouraged to continue working on the case study app, and compete for a prize offered by the owner of the app.

Needed tools:

- mechanical pencil
- blue liner
- marker (highlighter)
- eraser
- paper

Organized by:

Timisoara Startup Hub, with the support of Timisoara Mobile Development Group and UX/UI Timisoara

Cost:

This is a free event. We are only encouraging participants to bring a voluntary contribution in an amount on their choice to have covered the costs of refreshments, snacks, stationary used.
