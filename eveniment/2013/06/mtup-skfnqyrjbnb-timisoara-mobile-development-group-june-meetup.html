Timisoara Mobile Development Group June Meetup

luni, 17 iunie 2013

****************************************

LAST UPDATE:

The event will also include a presentation done by Dan Bugariu on the first Timisoara City Projects endeavour: the "Administrator de bloc" , a project will need some mobile development expertise to be added to the existing team.

****************************************

The TiMo June edition will be structured around a series of project presented by teams of local young students:

VDTruck 3D a truck driving game, developed for Android so far, recently unveiled at Google I/O Extended – Timisoara by Andrei Manescu .

HChecked - the health app that will represent Romania to the global final of Imagine Cup 2013, developed by MedScientists, a team from "Politehnica" University of Timisoara team ( Roxana Padurariu , Diana Lascu , Giuliano de Sabata , Andrei Avramescu ).

</a>

Space Collector , a mobile and tablet game, best described <a href="http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/space-collector">here , on Indiegogo, where Razvan Avram and Adrian Maroiu , the two developers are looking to crowdfund the needed publishing licences in order to launch their product.

The evening will be closed by Raoul Badarau (high-school student at Electrotimis Technical College) and his TTNS - Timisoara Transportation Network Simulator team. They are running a project aiming and starting to succeed in putting on a map, real time, the Timisoara buses, trams and trolley buses, in both web and mobile versions.

In order to keep the spirits up we'll offer to participants beverages, snacks, cookies. Please be ready to contribute with the usual 15 RON.
