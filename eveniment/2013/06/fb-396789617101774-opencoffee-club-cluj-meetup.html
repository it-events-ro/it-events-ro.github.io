OpenCoffee Club Cluj Meetup

miercuri, 19 iunie 2013

OpenCoffee is an open and regular meeting place where entrepreneurs, developers, investors and startup enthusiasts can meet with each other in an informal setting. Agenda 18:30 - Arriving at the location - We really want to start at 19:00, so please arrive on time. 19:00 - Hello and get to know each other - Hellos and a few words about each attendee 19:10 - News - We’ll talk about new product launches, news about local startups, legal information regarding startups, developer toolkits releases, best practices about using x ( x could mean almost everything) 19:30 - Pitch Time - During this time some of you could pitch their idea or product, ask general and specific questions, ask for feedback and help, ask and give recommendations and talk about job opportunities (even startups need to hire). Also during pitch time we’ll have guest speakers from time to time. By the way, you can suggest us some speakers, and we’ll make whatever it takes to bring them. 20:00 - Networking - This will be the perfect time for follow-up discussions, because you’ll get some topics in the first hour. PS Mark, Larry and Sergey aren’t available this year.
