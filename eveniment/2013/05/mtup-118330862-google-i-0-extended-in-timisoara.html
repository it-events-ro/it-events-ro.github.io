Google I/0 Extended in Timisoara

miercuri, 15 mai 2013

NOTE: Joining this event only on Meetup is not enough. For a full registration please also use this form .


A group of Timisoara Google technologies users including Oana & Arthur Csertus and Silviu Vert challenged the Startup Hub with the idea of hosting a local gig under the Google I/O Extended brand. Such an initiative cannot be resisted, by no means.

So, all those interested in being connected to San Francisco's Moscone Center for the Google I/O 2013 event alongside other local geeks, will be able to spend the evenings and most of the nights of the May 15-17 interval in Timisoara Startup Hub, watching the live streaming sessions of various presentations on Android, Chrome, Google+, Google Maps, YouTube, Google Glass, Google Ads, Google Wallet and also discussing and (if courageous enough) hands-on testing the technologies presented.

Follow the links bellow in order to find out more details about Google I/O 2013 and for registration. Participation is free, but we are encouraging you to contribute by bringing in the goodies listed here.

Looking forward to meet you in Timisoara Startup Hub in one of the only two Google I/O Extended events to take place in Romania, besides the one organized by the University Politehnica of Bucharest.
