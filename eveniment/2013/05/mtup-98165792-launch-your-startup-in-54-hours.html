Launch Your Startup in 54 Hours

vineri, 24 mai 2013

Guys, we are happy to let you know that it's official, Startup Weekend is coming to Targu Mures. We've changed the date to 24-26 May, so make sure you cancel everything for that weekend !

We are pretty proud that we managed to get some amazing mentors that will guide and help you through the weekend!

The Startup Lab is very special for us and we would really, really love to have you at the event and to show this, we have prepared some special tickets for you guys! Just go to our website and then on the Eventbrite page and look for the "Enter promotional code" and insert: "STARTUPLAB@SWMURES2013". The code takes 30% of the price. If you register soon, you can take advantage of our cheaper "Early Bird" tickets, which are on sale until the 31 of March.

Find out more at mures.startupweekend.org and we're looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Startup Weekends are weekend-long, hands-on experiences where developers,designers, marketers, product managers and startup enthusiasts come together to share ideas, form teams, build products, and launch startups.This is time and the place to find out if your startup ideas are viable.You need also either technical or design backgrounds or business backgrounds.On Friday, attendees will pitch their best ideas to inspire others to join their team.Over Saturday and Sunday teams focus on customer development, validating their ideas, practicing LEAN Startup Methodologies and building a minimal viable product.On Sunday evening teams demo their prototypes and receive valuable feedback from a panel of experts.At midnight we will celebrate at one of the best locations in Transylvania.
