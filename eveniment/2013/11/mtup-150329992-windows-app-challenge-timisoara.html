Windows App Challenge Timisoara

sâmbătă, 16 noiembrie 2013

Timisoara Startup Hub and Timisoara Mobile Development Group are supporting a local implementation of the all-Romania Windows App Challenge, a hackathon focused on Windows 8 and Windows Phone development to take place in 5 cities (Timisoara, Bucharest, Cluj-Napoca, Iasi, Sibiu) this November 16-17 .


During the event a set of learning opportunities will be scheduled, referring to design, development and selling apps through the Windows Store. The event will involve mobile development experts and practitioners and Microsoft representatives that will help participating teams to advance their project.

Within the event program, various Windows 8 and WP seminaries, workshops, 1-to-1 discussion with participating mentors will be scheduled in order to get the needed feedback.


You will benefit of all the needed life-support input around the clock (food, beverages, all night working space access) in order to develop the winning app, that will compete for the Asus Vivotab tablet, the Xbox 360 and other surprise awards put on stake by the organizers.
