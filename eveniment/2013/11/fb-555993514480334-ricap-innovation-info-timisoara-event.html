RICAP Innovation Info Timisoara Event

miercuri, 6 noiembrie 2013

We both know you are the innovator. We also know that you and your team have developed a new, innovative technology. You are confident that it can go global. The good news is that now we can tell you that there is support available. RICAP (Romanian Innovation Commercialization Assistance Program) will be in Timisoara to let you know how to access it. RICAP is a program aiming to connect entrepreneurs that developed innovative technologies to a global network of potential partners and investors, offering support towards international commercialization through mentoring and facilitation of partnerships. Within the first stage of the program, a number of 15 innovative technology projects generated either from the industrial or academic environments will be selected in order to receive support during the December 2013-April 2014. Two most promising technology innovator teams will travel to the US West Coast to meet face-to-face investors, advisers, commercialization partners. You are invited to meet RICAP experts and learn about this outstanding opportunity in the RICAP Innovation Info Timisoara Event on Wednesday, November 6th, starting at 15:30. During the event you’ll have the opportunity to discuss with Dragos Pislaru, RICAP Program Director and General Manager of Gea Strategy & Consulting (most probably the best Romanian think-tank and consultancy organization in several fields, including innovation, cluster development, regional economic development, etc) about innovation commercialization, RICAP offer to innovative entrepreneurs and the program application process. The event, planned for a duration of 2 hours, will take place in City Business Centre, D Building, Mezzanine, 10th, C. Brediceanu St, Timisoara. Find more details about RICAP visiting www.ricap.ro. The first application period for accessing the RICAP support is opened until November 15th, 2013. RICAP is a project developed by Romanian Center for Innovatino in Local Development and Larta Insitute, headquartered in Los Angeles, SUA, implemented with Gea Strategy & Consulting involvement and financed by the Romanian-American Foundation. The Timisoara local event is co-organized by RICAP, Timisoara Startup Hub, City Business Centre and Timis Chamber of Commerce, Industry and Agriculture. Looking forward to see you soon!
