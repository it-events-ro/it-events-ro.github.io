TEDxBucharest, 2013 – Make it Happen – Saturday, the 09th of November

sâmbătă, 9 noiembrie 2013

TEDxBucharest 2013 is about to happen! On the 9th of November, the 5th edition will gather more than 15 speakers and divergent thinkers who will inspire us by sharing their experiences and innovative ideas. Furthermore, great performances will keep us entertained. Come and listen to their amazing stories on journalism, entrepreneurship, law, extreme sports and traveling, technology and design. You’ll have the chance to personally meet influencers that make amazing things happen every day and build great connections during the networking breaks. We will cater your every need – energy boosters, delicious food and cookies and great socializing spaces. Join us and let’s "Make it Happen" together at the 5th edition of TEDxBucharest. Register now and spread the word about this event: http://www.tedxbucharest.ro/register/ AGENDA! She was involved in a horrific accident that almost got her paralyzed just to get back on her feet and in search of new adventures. YANNA BUHRIER - Human rights activist and award-winning freelance journalist based in Sofia, Bulgaria, also the Campaign Director of the Bulgarian Helsinki Committee. She is a TED Fellow and was named one of the 50 people who will change the world in WIRED's Smart List 2012. DRAGOS DUBINA – Mountain sports and climbing instructor that will share his experience on his expeditions in the Indonesian Archipelago and New Guinea, leading us into the world of hidden indigenous tribes. Performance: MOONLIGHT BREAKFAST – Jazzy vibes and powerful lyrics from a „retro-electro” world; PAUL RADU - Stanford Knight Fellow, one of the most renowned investigation journalists on organized crime. He holds trainings for journalists both in the Balkans and Eastern Europe, promoting preventive and "open-source" journalism. LUCIAN DESPOIU - Political consultant, founder and general manager of Kondiment - the first digital company in Romania. He will explain how voters can and should influence political parties in the new digital era. DORIN STEFAN - Founder of the Romanian Architecture Society, this award winning architect has many international projects in his portfolio, one of which is the Taiwan Tower - a building with an imposing height of more than 300 meters. GEORGE POPESCU - Founder and CEO of Boston Technologies, a provider of trading platforms for brokerage firms that has grown into a global business with offices in Boston, New York, Paris and Tokyo. VLAD ANDREI - Researcher in experiential education, co-founder of Ikedoo, where children learn by doing, through their own experience, having minimum rules and boundaries. Performance: SILENT STRIKE - Electronic music composer - a journey through sounds and beats. SABINA ALISTAR – PhD student at Stanford University, Sabina develops and applies research tools in order to shape health care policies, with an emphasis on resource allocation for controlling HIV. BERTI BARBERA A Romanian musical performer, actor and columnist, who will offer us an unique perspective on rhythm and how it can be used in a surprising manner to help us overcome our flaws. SIMINA TANASESCU - Professor in the Department of Public Law at the Faculty of Law and French - Romanian College of European Studies. She will shed light on what she calls “practices transplant” and its repercussions on the domains in which it is used. Register now: http://www.tedxbucharest.ro/register/
