Lean Start Up Workshop in Targu Mures

joi, 4 iulie 2013

We will introduce the concept of Lean vs traditional strategic planning; analyze the concepts of value and waste while introducing the concept of Validated Learning. We will go through the new definitions that should matter to a start up, explain the idea of testing the key business assumptions and Pivoting.
We will continue with the understanding of the key hypotheses to be tested, and how to uncover the customers archetype. We will then explain the Value Proposition Canvas and the customer validation up to the Minimum Viable Product.
We will talk about key indicators and the concept of funnel, as well as that of Split testing, which is followed by the analysis of the Lean Canvas (Ash Murya) and the Business Model Canvas (Osterwalder).
From this point on we will present the importance of Communities and Co-creation of value with customers and supporters. We will analyse the importance of crowd-sourcing and more in general the power of openness: which represent a completely new paradigm in the way projects are shaped and implemented.

After this we begin with the 2nd part of the workshop: which is currently done on paper but will be soon available through the online platform that we are developing.
Participants are given a 22 question to structure their business model.We will introduce the necessary theoretical knowledge that will enable projects to better understand and answer to each question.The participants will be given 1 minute per question (we will chronometer the time) as the goal is to write key words and not a romance :)
Participants without a project will join an existing project and work together to answer all questions.

The last part of the workshop is grounded on open collaboration: the participants form groups of 4 people and each individual is given 10/15 minutes to present his business model to the rest of the group, benefitting from everyone's feedback to draw the most effective strategy to validate key assumptions in the easiest, cheapest and fastest way.
As mentors we will go from table to table in order to stream the collaborative process towards the creation of solutions for the challenges of each start up.
In case that enough time is left, the participants will pitch their idea in front of everyone: they will be given 2 minutes.
