Does your testing hurt? Please direct your emergencies to the testing clinic!

marți, 9 iulie 2013

Tuesday, July 9th , we are gathering at Endava head office for an emergency TdT Iași session.

Please bring with you any testing, managing, scripting, technical problem as we, the other attendees, are ready to put it through anamnesis and give you a prescription! Like in any (medical) examination, we ensure that anything you say will remain confidential and will not be communicated to any outside party.

Timeline :
18:15 – 18:30 – Attendees arrival (in Milky Way Conf Room, 4th floor. Ana will be expecting you at the ground floor and will help you with the guest access pass)

18:30 – 19:45 – Test clinic cases

19:45 – 20:00 – Feedback and closing of the TdT edition

Very important note for the attendees:

This event is a limited seating session! In case you cannot make it to the event, please change your RSVP answer to No so that the first person in the waiting list be moved in the Attendees list. Thank you in advance for your attention to this matter!
