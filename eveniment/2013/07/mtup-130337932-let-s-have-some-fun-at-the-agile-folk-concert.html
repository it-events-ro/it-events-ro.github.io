Let's Have Some Fun at The Agile Folk Concert

joi, 25 iulie 2013

Since it's summer and holiday season, I prepared you a surprise: a meetup just for fun.

Join us in Mojo, get some beers (or lemonades), talk to your fellow community members and listen to a great folk concert. Marius Matache, a really fun guy with a great voice - http://www.mariusmatache.ro/ - agreed to do this concert for free for our community.

Let's know each other better and have some fun together!
