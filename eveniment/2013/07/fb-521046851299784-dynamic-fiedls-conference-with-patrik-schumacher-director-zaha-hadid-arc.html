DYNAMIC FIEDLS Conference with PATRIK SCHUMACHER, Director Zaha Hadid Architects

luni, 29 iulie 2013

DYNAMIC FIELDS Conference, 29 July 2013, 09:00 – 18:00 “Parametric Semiology – Architecture as interface of communication” Special Guest: PATRIK SCHUMACHER, Director ZAHA HADID ARCHITECTS Location: ROMEXPO, C1 Pavilion Organizer: Parametrica [digi fab school] We invite you to a day of innovation where you will meet on the world’s most famous architects, Patrik Schumacher, who is going to introduce us into the world of parametric design and its significance. The conference closes the Dynamic Fields project by presenting the work of the 14 Day Workshop taking place in UNARTE between 16 and 28 July. Patrik Schumacher is one of the most important figures in architecture at the moment and certainly a controversial character. Director of one of the leading architecture firms in the world, Zaha Hadid Architects and founder of the research laboratory AA Design Lab in London, Patrik is one of the architects who have the ability to influence public opinion. The architectural studies conducted at the University of Stuttgart and London (Southbank), in addition to completing a philosophy studies at both Bonn and London universities, Patrik proposes a series of lectures on how architecture should impact on the environment and on people.
