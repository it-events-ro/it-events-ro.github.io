Timisoara Agile Software Meetup - Scrum at Imobiliare.ro

marți, 12 februarie 2013

Imobiliare.ro is Romania's no. #1 real-estate portal, based in Timisoara and using Scrum for development since the Timisoara Agile Community exists, back in 2009.

Although some people suspect they're 4 to 6 people working on a site, Imobiliare.ro is backed up by 43 people, spread across 3 floors and 8 departments, not to mention an extra sales workplace in Bucharest.

Come join us and see how Imobiliare.ro's online development team builds new features for the people looking to buy or rent a home.

Learn about the tools, workflows and practices that make Romania's no. #1 real-estate portal.

We'll host this meetup in the very room where the development team spends its day, and we'll be happy to get feedback on everything that is going on there.

Meetup agenda:

18:30 - participants reception & welcome 18:40 - participants introduction 18:55 - about Imobiliare.ro as a business 19:10 - Imobiliare.ro under the hood: a few numbers 19:20 - the tools & processes 20:20 - questions & answers
