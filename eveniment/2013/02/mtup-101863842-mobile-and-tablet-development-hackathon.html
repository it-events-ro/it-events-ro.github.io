Mobile and Tablet Development Hackathon

sâmbătă, 2 februarie 2013

Timisoara Mobile Development Group co-organizes together with Codecamp a mobile phone and tablet hackathon opened for Windows 8, Windows Phone, iOS și Android developers or people interested to get closer to this technologies.

The two days event's program will include a first, introductory, more theoretical part to cover the Saturday AM hours, build as a two-track conference.

One track will be dedicated to mobile development ( Raul Ciotescu Cross Platform development on iOS with Objecive C and Phonegap, HTML5 and JavaScript , respectively TBD: Windows Phone 8 Development ) while the second track will tackle Windows 8 development topics ( Radu Vunvulea : Building and testing Windows 8 Modern UI applications using C++, C# and JavaScript, respectively Emil Craciun : Enhance Windows 8 Store applications with Charms).

The PM half of Saturday and the whole day on Sunday will be dedicated to coding and development of small (or big, why not) apps on participants' choice from both technology or project goal points or view in the creativity enhancing environment of the Timisoara Startup Hub . The organizers will encourage ad-hoc formation of teams but any already existing teams are warmly invited to participate with their current projects.

Teams will be assisted by knowledgeable mentors and will be able to set up the length of their own working schedule in the Startup Hub in the Saturday evening, even full night sprints being possible.

The projects developed will be demonstrated on Saturday, starting 5PM.

Given the active involvement of a group of kind sponsoring companies, including Haufe-Lexware , Cloudbase Solutions , Telerik and Microsoft , all the costs will be covered, so there will be no participation fee.

For registration , besides RSVP-ing on this meetup page, please go on event's Eventbrite.com page .
