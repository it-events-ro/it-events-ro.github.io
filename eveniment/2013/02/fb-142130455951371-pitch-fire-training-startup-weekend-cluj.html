Pitch fire training - Startup Weekend Cluj

miercuri, 20 februarie 2013

Want to blow everyone's socks off at the next Startup Weekend Cluj friday night pitch fire? Startup Weekend, with the support of OpenCoffee Club Cluj (http://www.facebook.com/opencoffeecluj) and Incubator 107 Cluj (http://www.facebook.com/Incubator107Cluj), is giving you the opportunity to learn the secrets of the perfect pitch from the best! The workshop will be held by Philipp Kandal, Co-Founder & CTO of skobbler and great supporter of startups. Topics covered during the Pitch fire training will include: - What to include in a 60 seconds pitch? - Guidance and feedback on delivery skills - Overcoming nerves - How to maximise the networking opportunity at the event. If you want to increase your chances of getting your idea selected on Friday night, come and practice with us next Wednesday! PS: Non Startup Weekend attendees are free to come if they want to improve their pitching skills too!
