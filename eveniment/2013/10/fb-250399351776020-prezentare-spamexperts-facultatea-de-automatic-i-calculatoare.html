Prezentare SpamExperts @ Facultatea de Automatică și Calculatoare

marți, 29 octombrie 2013

Hello, ROSEdu vă invită marți, 29 Octombrie între orele 14-16, la o prezentare susținută de către Dreas van Donselaar, CTO la SpamExperts[0]. Prezentarea va fi despre serviciile SpamExperts in general : Incoming Email Filtering, Outgoing Email Filtering, Email Archiving. Prezentarea va fi ținută in EG106. Cele mai bune întrebări de la sfârșit vor primi câte un tricou din partea SpamExperts. Pentru o aproximare cat mai bună, te rugăm să completezi acest form [1]. SpamExperts specializes in inbound email filtering (for spam and virus protection), outbound email filtering (for blacklist prevention), and Email Archiving (for backup and compliance). We are the preferred supplier of Email-security for webhosting providers, data-centers, Telcos, and Enterprises around the world. This presentation will provide you a technical insight on how our services work in general, and will elaborate further on the product development side. You will also have the opportunity to learn how SpamExperts has grown Dreas van Donselaar is a co-founder of SpamExperts, the Amsterdam based leading provider of professional email security solutions. He currently holds the CTO responsibilities in the company. Dreas is also the founder of the Mailserver Whitelist Foundation, a not-for-profit and free registration database for mail servers using an IPv6 Internet address. A self-starter since his University years, Dreas set-up, grew and profitably sold two internet services businesses, before assuming his role in SpamExperts. Recognized expert in the web hosting and information security technologies, Dreas has participated as a speaker at some of the larger industry events, including HostingCon and the EU Spam Symposium. Dreas holds a Masters degree in Information Management and a Bachelors degree in International Business from the Maastricht University. An accomplished entrepreneur, he is also an active supporter of animal protection causes through local politics.
