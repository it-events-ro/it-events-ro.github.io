Ineta-Europe: Kathleen Dollard Balkan Tour. Timisoara Stage

luni, 14 octombrie 2013

.NET goddess Kathleen Dollard is doing a tour in South Eastern Europe, and is returning to Timisoara after a couple of years. Timisoara Startup Hub together with @RONUA Timisoara is kindly inviting all those interesting in programming, generally and .NET, specially, to a two hours feast with high quality tech presentations. Kathleen will approach two main themes: 1. Modern Tracing " It's a talk I'm giving at DevReach. Everything you know about tracing has changed with ETW support in .NET 4.5. I am really passionate about this and anxious to share with you!" 2. VS Tips and Tricks "This is a talk I’ll be presenting at DevIntersection. It includes VS 2013. I will gladly balance this talk with old and new VS features depending on the audience." We are also foreseeing a significant Q&A section to this event and, why not, an informal extension with some beers in a place we'll announce during the event. For those who haven't met Kathleen during her previous visit to Timisoara, here are some bio info: Kathleen Dollard is .NET Coach for Crystal MEF Lab. She has been a Microsoft MVP since 1998 and has given hundreds of speeches around the world. She has worked extensively with compositional architectures in relation to specific development platforms. She’s interested in making core .NET technologies available to all coders to enable them to write better software faster. Kathleen is also a long-time advocate of generative techniques and is the author of Code Generation in Microsoft .NET (from Apress). She has published numerous articles on a range of .NET technologies. Kathleen is active in the Northern Colorado .NET SIG, the Denver Visual Studio User Group, and the Northern Colorado Architect's Group.
