Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 16 decembrie 2013

This last of 2013 editions of the Timisoara Mobile Development Group events will include a series of presentations on new apps recently launched by local teams and a talk on mobile development education by a special guest from Skopje, Macedonia.

The program includes the following entries:

Oana and Arthur Csertus of Cobalt Sign developed the Vocea Romaniei Windows Phone app and will share the experience of working with a major media trust.

http://cdn.marketplaceimages.windowsphone.com/v8/images/54b5bb1f-724a-4101-a1ad-77a0721a2a9e?imageType=ws_icon_large

Zoran Popovici expanded his passion for comics from starting a specialized magazine, Terapie de Basm and comes to present Super Erou , a specialized mobile publishing platform, developed by a large team with members from Timisoara and Bucharest.

https://lh5.ggpht.com/Xdqah5YPQ1VD9zP72TIFfHUfBxpkhFXIrrOrSnrnqUEqn0h2sI-V3TgGkXqDOvOM4w=w300-rw

We'll also have a 30 mins talk and facilitated discussion by Martin Ancevski , an excellent young mobile developer, from Codewell (a mobile development agency from Skopje, Macedonia), specialized in iOS and Android who will challenge the audience on continuous mobile development skills and knowledge improvement.

After a short break, Antonia Goga , Bogdan Gaza , Stefan Filip and Horia Jurcut will showcase the product they've developed in order to won this November Develop to Connect , a major hackathon organized by Google Romania in Bucharest. Humming Box , is a project to offer new ways to experience music in public places controlling it with your mobile phone.

The last presentation will be "fired" by VROID , a team formed by Horia Porav , Valentina Musat , Sorin Butnariu , Mihai Bujanca , that progresses towards creation of a new generation of FPS (first person shooter) game controller that combines Virtual Reality with gesture controllers. Their first prototype, using a pair of Epson BT-100 VR glasses, an Android phone, a Raspberry Pi and a toy gun, developed two weeks ago, brought them the victory in the 2013 ecaHack Timisoara event.

You are asked to contribute the usual 15 RON for enjoying snacks and beverages during the event.
