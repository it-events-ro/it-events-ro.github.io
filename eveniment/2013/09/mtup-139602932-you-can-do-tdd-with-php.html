You can do TDD with PHP!

marți, 17 septembrie 2013

Let's meet and share our experience and opinions about how to do TDD, unit testing and other types of testing in PHP. Because there were no previous meeting for this group, I suggest this to be in the style of open discussions on the proposed theme so that we have a better chance to get to know each-other. Of course we will talk in Romanian ;) but I suppose English is also acceptable if people not speaking Romanian want to attend.

Please someone from DXB confirm there is a space for the meeting, if not maybe we can do it at StartupHub (what do you thing Radu?).

Being the first meeting of this group, feel free to come regardless of the seniority or level on PHP knowledge you have. We will meet and discuss and let the discussion flow so that everyone is happy. I don't want this to be very formal. Just meet, get to know each other, and talk on a very interesting subject.
