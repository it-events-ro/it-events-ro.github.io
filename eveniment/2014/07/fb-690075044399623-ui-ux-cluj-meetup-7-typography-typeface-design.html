UI/UX Cluj Meetup#7 - Typography & Typeface Design

marți, 8 iulie 2014

Hi there! We're kicking off this month with a new UI/UX meetup and with a subject close to all our hearts and eyes especially. It's a theme we've been long waiting for and it's fascinating because it finds itself so much in the concept and creation area. Next week we're going to discuss: TYPOGRAPHY. 95% of the information on the web is written language. It is only logical to say that a web designer should get good training in the main discipline of shaping written information, in other words: Typography. Daniel Muntean and Iulian Corunga are going to be in the spotlight this time. Daniel Muntean has been a Graphic Designer for more than six years, mostly doing freelance work and trying new job positions from time to time. Since 2013, he’s an UX Designer at Yonder, challenging himself into this curious and exciting field. When designing interfaces gets tedious, he’s in his studio producing Electronic Music or taking photographs of playful shadows. After years of working with Typography, Daniel has decided to create one himself, and took the opportunity of the BA Diploma at UAD to do so. He is going to share that experience with us, and provide an in-depth view of the process of Designing a Typeface from scratch. For all Typography enthusiasts out there, it’s going to be fun! :) www.danielmuntean.com You can register here: http://www.meetup.com/UXUICluj/events/177040832/. ------------------ In Cluj-Napoca there is a lot of potential for growth in the software sector in more strategic (and better paid areas as well), such as User Experience and User Interaction Design. From this perspective, we are forwarding you this invitation of joining us and collaborating within the UI/UX community. This invitation is addressed to individuals and companies as well. Due to the fact that each company has one, two ot three UI/UX specialists we invite you to connect and thus create a growth environment for the professionals in this area. In this way we are contributing to the overall development and augmentation of specialised talent in the Cluj-Napoca area.
