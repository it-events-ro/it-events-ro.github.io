Product Tank Cluj

joi, 31 iulie 2014

Product Tank Cluj is a monthly meetup to exchange ideas and experiences about product management. For this specific event we are scheduling a hangout with Product Tank Bucharest where are announced: - Ciprian Gavriliu Product Designer & UX Consultant at http://www.triplemagicinteractive.com/ which will present the redesign from Stride CRM and - Bogdan Lucaciu which will present about how he tests features in paralel. - Moderator: Director of Product Vladimir Oane @ Hootsuite, Past: uberVU and How To Web More details also here: https://www.facebook.com/groups/producttankromania. In general our events feature peer to peer talks and advice on designing and building great web and mobile products, including topics such as design, agile processes, business models, using metrics, user experience, and more. We also always build in time for valuable networking over some beers (costs associated with your donation). Our events are public, but we ask everyone to provide a little information about themselves when they register so that we can build a good community. Is recommended the participation for entrepreneurs with a strong focus on products or managers from companies interested in developing future tech products. Until we meet you could read this article http://www.romanpichler.com/blog/new-product-development-with-lean-startup-and-scrum/. If you are a person involved in product developemnt register here: http://www.meetup.com/CoFoundersLab-Cluj/events/197699542/.
