PMI Monthly Meeting hosted by AROBS Cluj-Napoca

marți, 9 decembrie 2014

It is time for a new Monthly Meeting in Cluj.
This time we will be meeting at AROBS which will present us Challenges of Project Management in Product Development. As you are already used to by now, after the presentation we challenge you to a panel debate that aims to examine more thoroughly the importance of the topic mentioned above. Agenda:
18:00 – 18:30 Registration & Welcome Coffee
18:30 – 18:40 PMI Presentation
18:40 – 19:00 Presentation AROBS
19:00 – 19:30 Presentation PM topic
19:30 – 19:45 Break
19:45 – 20:15 Panel Discussion
20:15 onwards Networking We will be waiting for you on 9th Decembrie 2014, at AROBS (Cluj Business Center, Str. Henry Barbusse Nr. 44-46, Cluj), starting with 18:00. Fifty (50) seats are available for this meeting.

You can register at: http://pmi.ro/articole/229/pmi-monthly-meeting-gazduit-de-arobs-cluj-napoca/ If you have a PMP, PMI-SP, PMI-RMP or PMI-ACP certification you can claim two PDUs by attending this event, after receiving the event code from PMI Romania. You can participate if you are not a member PMI Romania, provided you have not participated in other meetings monthly PMI Romania this year. This way we give access to people interested in becoming members of our organization and want to know the benefits from this affiliation. Please request further information about our organization addressing your PMI Romania's board members present at this meeting. Each member can come accompanied by a non-member PMI Romania.
