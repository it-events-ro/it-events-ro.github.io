Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 20 ianuarie 2014

January edition of the Timisoara Mobile Development Group gathering will feature


Oana and Arthur Csertus , talking about their Vocea Romaniei Windows Phone app and will share the experience of working with a major media trust.


Marcel Barbulescu COO/CTO, Director at AudioNow.com presenting how mobile devices can offer you specialized radio experience worldwide at close-to-zero costs and what are the promises that emerge from his company's patents in fine syncing of video and sound from different sources.

Catalin Gheorghiu will unveil latest features in Windows embedded Handheld 8 and how they can be used in B2B apps.

Madalin Gageanu and Alin Meceanu will jointly deliver a key note in a dual approach (Madalin - live in Timisoara, while Alin - via Skype) an interesting presentation on how they are "incubating" thelong-range future mobile apps ideas within a global operator's "special task force".

This should be a very valuable, practical lesson to attend by all those developing their apps ideas.


As usual, the doors opening time is 6.30 PM, the event will actually start at 7.00 PM.

Don't forget to bring in the 15 RON participation fee for covering the costs of beverages, snacks and cookies we'll provide!
