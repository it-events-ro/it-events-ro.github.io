#8 Ethics and issue escalation

joi, 20 martie 2014

Hello Everyone,

This meeting is about ethics and issue escalation . More exactly, we all have people that are higher than us in company hierarchy. They based their knowledge and responses in our input. When things does not go according the plan we need to manage the situation by sending accurate information to the ones impacted.

We would like to discuss the process of solving these situations. Also we need some actions to prevent happening in the future. Having an impediment spread from your team, repeatedly, will discredit you and your team.

• 7.30 - 7.45 People arriving and introduction for new members. Get our engine started with some fresh drinks.

• 7.45 - 8.15 Andrei, our PMI Cluj Branch volunteer, will present the evening theme and we will start debating on this.

• 8.15 - 8.25 We can eat or just make a break for fresh air.

• 8.25 - 9.00 We will continue debating the subject

• 9.00 - 9.10 short break

• 9.10 - 9.30 We will talk about next meeting: you can vote or propose the next meeting theme. You can also ask for more information about PMI Cluj Brench.

If you want to participate, please and join this meetup.If later something comes up and you cannot participate, just take a minute and actualize your answer.
