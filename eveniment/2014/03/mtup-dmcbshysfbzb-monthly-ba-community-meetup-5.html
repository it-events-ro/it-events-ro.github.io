Monthly BA Community Meetup #5

marți, 25 martie 2014

Dear colleagues,

Delivering successful projects is significantly influenced by the interpersonal skills of the Business Analyst. Successful Business Analysts are able to employ well - developed interpersonal skills in their interactions with project team members and stakeholders to achieve superior project outcomes.

BA Community Cluj offers a range of interpersonal skills workshops & trainings designed to enable you as professionals to achieve excellence in areas such as leadership, planning and communicating change, problem solving skills, time management, communication, negotiations, decision making and emotional intelligence.

Therefore, we have the pleasure to invite you at the monthly Meetup of the Cluj's Business Analysis Community with the following topic: Planning and Communicating Change .

More details can be found below:

Key topics:

Planning for effective communication to support a project or other initiative. Performing a change readiness assessment prior to implementing change. Approaches to better understanding and managing the constraints and enablers of change in a particular organization or group. Who should attend: Business Analysts, project leaders and others responsible for managing communication during a project. Managers and others introducing change into an organization. Change managers responsible for planning organizational change. Trainers, implementation managers and process analysis responsible for ensuring an operational team successfully adopts a new system or process. Project office leaders, quality assurance experts and others looking to deploy a new approach or methodology. Prerequisites: There are no specific prerequisites for this workshop. Methodologies & Tools:

http://www.personalstrengths.ro/ce-este-sdi http://www.personalstrengths.ro/alte-instrumente Learning Outcomes:

Create a clear goal for change or communication initiatives. Create a basic communication strategy. Clarify and focus a core message so that it is relevant to different stakeholder groups. Perform a change readiness assessment on a group. Discuss approaches to measuring the progress and impact of change. Build a plan for both initiating change end for rolling out communications. Leverage the enablers and manage the constraints that exist when initiating change. Identify the obstacles that will hinder the successful implementation of change. Speaker:
