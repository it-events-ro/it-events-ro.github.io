Timisoara Startups: Be Present at Timisoara Business Days 2014

miercuri, 2 aprilie 2014

We are partnering with the organizers of the Timisoara 2014 edition of the Business Days events to offer opportunities to local entrepreneurs to make significant use of the their participation. As a startup, there are several ways to get exposure, feedback, and connections during the event: - participate with a stand in the business networking and exhibition area in a space specially designated for startups, offering your product and service to be tested by participants and obtaining immediate, direct feedback - get introduction support by organizers to experts, investor, potential clients - live-pitch your business to interested participants The above mentioned services are available in three different packages, available to up to 10 startups, so be fast in claiming yours! Besides this special offer, as an IT&C startup you may consider participation to a special industry satellite event, scheduled for April 2nd, 2:30 PM, featuring Radu Georgescu and other relevant speakers, focused on product development, respectively a presentation on IBM's Global Entrepreneur. For additional information do not hesitate and get in touch with us using the following e-mail address radu.ticiu@gmail.com.
