1st ever Project Tango Hackathon

vineri, 25 aprilie 2014

UPDATE 1 : We are asking all those interested to attend to perform a more formal registration following THIS LINK .

UPDATE 2 : Alcatel OneTouch is sponsoring the hackathon, offering as prizes for the best projects some of their best smart phones. The Alcatel OneTouch 8008D Scribe HD s will make the competition more vivid, for sure!

UPDATE 3: Asociatia Studentilor in Stiinte Exacte din Universitatea Timisoara ( ASSEUT ) are facilitating the participation of hackers from outside Timisoara offering to those interested accommodation in the Vest University of Timisoara campus!

Timisoara Mobile Development Group, Timisoara Google Developer Group and Timisoara Startup Hub are building an unique opportunity for mobile technology passionates!

In the first community event of its kind, globally, we are bringing in one single place the following exquisite ingredients:

• 10-15 pieces of the mobile devices equipped with Project Tango sensors, apps and capabilities

• 10-15 teams created by  ambitious and knowledgeable members, ready to deal with the cutting edge Project Tango

• 4-5 experts from Timisoara and abroad that participated to the development of the Project Tango

• 4-5 mentors that can guide you in exploring various app concepts based on usage of the Project Tango technologies

• a time frame of about 48 hours to squeeze your tech creativity, to work in teams in order to build the killing app that will become the standard for the next generation of mobile phones

• snacks and beverages, relaxation area

Remember that the goal of Project Tango is "to give mobile devices a human scale understanding of space and motion“ and be part of this endeavour, from the early beginning even if you are not one of the 200 lucky recipients of a Project Tango dev kit, worldwide.

Keep an eye on this page in order to get additional information soon!
