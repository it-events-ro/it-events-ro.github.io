#10 People management

joi, 24 aprilie 2014

Hi Everyone,

This meeting is about People Management. It is not a new topic but according to the collected feedback, there are a lot of things to be said.

This time, we would like to discuss about how to handle delicate situations, how to manage people with difficult personalities in the always challenging context of a project and still build a strong performing team. What can we do to predict and prevent problems.

If you want to participate, please and join this meetup. If later something comes up and you cannot participate, just take a minute and actualize your answer.

The meeting will be on 24th of April, 19:30 at Marty Boulevard, Horia Street.

If you have any questions or suggestions you can write Raluca. ( [masked])
