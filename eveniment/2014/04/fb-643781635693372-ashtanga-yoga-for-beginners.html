Ashtanga Yoga for Beginners

miercuri, 2 aprilie 2014

Participarea se face cu înscriere aici: http://timisoara.incubator107.ro/atelier/Breasla%20Mi%C8%99c%C4%83torilor/1176 CE? We will start with breathing and meditation exercises and then go on with the Primary Series of Ashtanga Yoga. This workshop can support you to find some rest within yourself and exercise your inner and outer stability and attention. Ashtanga Yoga can be referred to as a kind of meditation that lets you focus on your body and feel that all is one. Practiced regularly it can have great effects on your inner and outer being. Your personal effects you will find within yourself. You are welcome to come each time and experience something new, and surely also a step forwards in the experience of your practice. CU CINE? Pia Brodtrager is presently working with incubator107 all over Romania, as part of her European Voluntary Service. Pia has been practicing Yoga since 2010. She has experienced Hatha Yoga for 2 years in Vienna/Austria. Afterwards she started to practice Ashtanga Yoga in Leeds / UK. A journey to an inner and outer awareness began. This led her to the practice of Reiki (energy therapy) in Vienna, where she completed her second degree in September 2013. Besides her spiritual practice Pia has continuously been working with methods that can be sumarised as Theatre for Social Change. With incubator107 she is now fond of sharing her spiritual practice and knowledge.
