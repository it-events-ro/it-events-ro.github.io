Squirrly Hackathon

sâmbătă, 12 aprilie 2014

You don’t have to build your own high growth tech startup to be part of one. Join the Squirrly Hackathon and see what’s up with growing a startup. Be part of a startup environment of building awesome things for the web for 24 hours, on the 12th of April. Coding, Free Beer and Pizza will be included. Prizes Included + Get Promoted + Hall of Fame - You won’t write meaningless code, that will end up in the utter void of time and space. - Your code can be shipped to over 40,000 people (that we already have using our software) and then 100,000 more new people that are expected to come by the end of the year. - Special Prizes will be awarded. - Hall Of Fame on the Squirrly Blog and also on our Facebook page. - We’ll promote the most engaged hackers at the Squirrly hackathon to our clients for freelance jobs on SEO, web dev, etc. They’re looking for such services, and it would be a great match (plus great Moolah Moolah $$ passed around) - the German company, Social Trademarks, will be glad to hire one of the best coders involved in the hackathon. Darth Squirrly said it: It is your destiny. Join me Who should come? If you’re a php, MySQL, jQuery, JavaScript guy / gal, then most probably you’re strong in the Coding Force and the Squirrly Hackathon will need you.
