Getting your Startups in Media

miercuri, 16 aprilie 2014

This edition of GIST TechConnect is titled Attracting Media Attention for Your Venture -- think like a journalist and attract the right media for your venture. Guests: Mark Roberge, Chief Revenue Officer, Hubspot Dragos Ilinca, Co-Founder, uberVU Shaherose Charania, Co-Founder & CEO, Women 2.0 Kim Azzarelli, Senior VP, Women in the World GIST TechConnect Moderator: Ovidiu Bujorean, Senior Manager, CRDF Global GIST TechConnect is a series of interactive videoconferences that brings together great entrepreneurial minds to spark the creation of new technology ventures. Following the videoconference we'll continue with a discussion involving the participants about specificities for their startups. More details about the speakers and the GIST TechConnect project can be found here: http://gist.crdfglobal.org/about-gist/events/2014/04/10/gist-techconnect-attracting-media-attention-for-your-venture.
