Present your business in one minute

vineri, 4 aprilie 2014

Our first event delivered in English, with an awesome trainer from abroad, Anna Heijker. In this interactive session we will go through all the steps of creating an impactful presentation: crafting and delivering it and how to use it in different situations. You will be practicing in small groups several times. In this training we will create the perfect one-minute presentation for your business, which will help you to present your startup or freelance business when you are in front of an audience. Or when you meet investors, prospective clients or partners and other stakeholders. After this training you will: • know how to create a positive first impression and to engage your listeners • have your own, clear structured one-minute business presentation ready • have practised your presentation and have received feedback • have further developed business communication muscle *Anna Heijker is a communications specialist from The Netherlands. After travelling the world by herself for a year she came to Romania and started her own business. She delivered trainings on one-minute pitching and pitching to investors at e.g. the Netherlands-Romanian Chamber of Commerce, The HUB Bucharest and Incubator107. Anna holds a master’s degree in Dutch language and culture and specialized during her studies in public speaking and how to create strong messages. For more information about Anna have a look at her website (www.annaheijker.com) or LinkedIn profile (www.linkedin.com/in/annaheijker).
