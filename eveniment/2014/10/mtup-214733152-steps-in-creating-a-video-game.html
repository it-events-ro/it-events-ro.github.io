Steps in creating a video game

marți, 28 octombrie 2014

GameDEV Iasi Community, let's gather!


Next Tuesday we’re going Live! We will have the First GameDEV Iasi Meetup @ Ramada!

GameDEV Iasi Meetup is meant for game developers to get together and talk about design, programming, art and production of great games. Come along and share your own ideas and game projects, or find people to work on a project with you.

You'll fit right in if you are a professional game developer, indie, hobbyist, student or a professional from other related fields, who wants to find out more about making games.


Here's what we've prepared for this first meeting:


18.30 – 18.45 The Gathering! GameDEV Iasi Community

18.45 – 19.00 Opening Words - Andrei Istrate , Co-Founder @ RGDA (Romanian Game Developers Association)

19.00 – 19.40 Panel discussion Steps in creating a video game

Guests:

• Catalin Marcu, Producer @ Mobility Games

• Nicu Berbece, Developer - "Move or Die" | Founder "Those Awesome Guys“

19.40 – 19.55 Q&A

After the presentation most of us will continue the talk over a beer (or two).

GameDEV Iasi Meetup, 28th of October, 2014, a very important event for the Community, here in Iasi, is organized by RGDA, with the support of Mobility Games. The attendance is free of charge, if you book your seat on Meetup.com.


If you want to contribute to the development of this community, please distribute the event to other professionals or enthusiasts of game development!

Link: http://www.meetup.com/GameDEV-Iasi-Meetup/events/214733152/
