Google Tech Talk & Info Session at FMI

miercuri, 22 octombrie 2014

Google will give a tech talk (abstract below) that will also include information about internships and job opportunities. FMI students intending to attend are asked to fill out this form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/19mVpiaOXKc5jSRKAHPWkbxhpEgTT1llnJwDe3Accim0/viewform. Date: 22nd October Time: 19:00 Location: Amf. Spiru Haret Title: Google Cloud navigator: App Engine, Big Query, App Script & more Abstract: The talk is a short introduction of what is cloud computing and why it makes sense to use it with examples from Google cloud. The presentation will include several interesting cloud services that can make life a lot easier for a developer. Bio: Mihai Pora is a software engineer from Krakow currently building a data warehouse using Google’s tools. He joined Google in 2011 working on Google TV and later switching to Resource Management. In his 20% time, he's added a feature to Docs Scanner and built a robot with Project Tango. Previous to Google he worked in Bucharest on a wide variety of projects that included enterprise architecture, business process management, data warehousing, IPTV, image processing, computer vision and online commerce. google.com/+mihaipora www.linkedin.com/in/mihaipora If you have any questions, email romania-tech-students@google.com.
