How to Start a Startup by Sam Altman, President @ YCombinator - Lectures 1-3

joi, 2 octombrie 2014

Sam Altman, President of Y Combinator, the #1 startup accelerator in the world; and Stanford are teaming up to teach a class called How to Start a Startup. How to Start A Startup or CS183B features some of the best speakers from the past 9 years of Y Combinator dinners. The class will cover how to come up with ideas and evaluate them, how to get users and grow, how to do sales and marketing, how to hire, how to raise money, company culture, operations and management, business strategy, and more. Over 130 cities and universities have signed up to follow along and stream the class together, as well as peer evaluate the projects. ___________________________ ClujHUB brings this opportunity to Cluj-Napoca.
