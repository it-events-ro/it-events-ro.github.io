Pitch Fire Training - Startup Weekend Cluj

miercuri, 19 februarie 2014

Want to blow everyone's socks off at the next Startup Weekend Cluj pitch fire session? Then REGISTER, right NOW for this training, for free: http://bit.ly/1b1SOMy Startup Weekend Cluj has partnered with Wayra CEE to provide you the opportunity to learn the secrets of the perfect pitch from awesome coaches, to learn from speaker’s experience in the startups world and to find more about the opportunity offered by Wayra CEE, the youngest academies in the accelerator’s global network.
