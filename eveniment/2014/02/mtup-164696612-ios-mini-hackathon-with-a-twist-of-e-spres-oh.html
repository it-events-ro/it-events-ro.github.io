iOS Mini-Hackathon With a Twist Of [e-spres-oh]

duminică, 9 februarie 2014

The Sunday between the Introduction to iOS Development and the Advanced iOS Development training events cannot be an inactive one.

That's why TiMo and [e-spres-oh] are inviting iOS development aficionados to spend the day coding in a hackathon mode.

Participants are invited to work individually or in small teams on one of their current iOS projects or to start new ones, in an open approach, sharing, asking and offering feedback from peer participants and from Paul Ardeleanu , the London-based developer and trainer delivering the above mentioned training events.

The best results of the day will be awarded with programming books.

[e-spres-oh] is one of the most remarkable digital development company in Timisoara and Romania, focused on continuously creating success stories, a lot of them based on the usage of mobile technologies. The amazing [e-spres-oh] team will offer to hackathon participants an amazingly inspiring location, a well assorted fridge, the proverbial coders' pizza and, of course, the best espresso coffee.

If you are programming for iOS you should not miss this!


IMPORTANT NOTICE

We are looking for participants that have at least some previous experience/knowledge of iOS development.
