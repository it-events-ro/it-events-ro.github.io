Why should you come? Because you may win a Misfits Shine.

joi, 22 mai 2014

Well, we have wrote a few reasons why should you come. But know, we will offer you a bigger one: you may win a Misfits Shine wearable. Something with apps n stuff. But what's the catch? First, you have to come to Cluj Hub, in 22nd of May. Second, you'll have to write your name, e-mail and cell phone number, down on a piece of paper and put it in a bowl (old school, I know) and we will extract a name, at the end of the evening. The lucky one HAS to be there or else the prize will go to someone else. Good luck! ___________________ You may (or may not) have heard about the biggest tech event from Transylvania, called Techsylvania. And even if you did, you're not sure if you should spend those money to come to the conference. You're not sure if it's worth it, actually, even though Google Glass and speakers from Twitter and Yahoo sound pretty great. Therefore, we organized this meet-up so you could come and pour your soul out and ask us about every single detail, including I don't know...the weather, maybe? Oh, and we will bring some cute tech stuff, like Sphero, Withings Pulse, Pebble, Leap Motion and Little Printer. Don't forget your smartphone home! Schedule: 18:30 - Welcome 18:50 - Conference overview - Vlad Ciurca 19:10 - Hackathon overview - Cristin Iosif 19:30 - Misfit Wearables raffle prize 19:50 - Networking & testing gadgets Aaaaand we will offer you some special discounts! Come to the tech side,we have cookies & gadgets. Looking forward to meeting you on Thursday! Yours sincerely The Techsylvania Team
