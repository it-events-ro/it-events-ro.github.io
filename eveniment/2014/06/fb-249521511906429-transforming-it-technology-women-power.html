Transforming IT: technology & women power

luni, 16 iunie 2014

This workshop brings together local changemakers who are already taking part in a global movement to create a supportive network for women empowerment through technology. The idea is to offer an overall picture on the topic of women in IT and see which are the local perspectives about the gender issues in IT and the worldwide dominance of men in technical fields. Our guests will exchange ideas and present some initiatives that are meant to fill in the gaps between women and men in IT in Romania. We will discuss about the role and the importance of having women in a tech-environment and find out new ways of encouraging girls to become leaders and the best professionals in the technology field. We will explore the ideas that can open up the IT industry, attract young people and especially women passionate about technology in the fields of engineering and computing. Moderator: Roxana Rugina, CEO & co-founder Simplon Romania and Our guests: Andrei Roth, General Manager Recognos Romania Ioana Zileriu, Operations Manager at Imprezzio Global,Member of the Board of Directors la CLUJ IT Cluster Claudia Suhov, co-founder & Creative Director Kidster More details: http://arcadianetwork.org/
