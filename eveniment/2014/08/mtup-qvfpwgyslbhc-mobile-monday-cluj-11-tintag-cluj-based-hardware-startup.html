Mobile Monday Cluj #11 - TINTAG - Cluj-based Hardware Startup

luni, 25 august 2014

Hello everybody!


What do you love most when it comes to coding? Would you rather code for a client, or would you rather code for your own product? I'd just go crazy if I knew I have a product to launch and that I have to write a few more lines to get it done for the big moment!

Well, about this and much more on how it's like to code for your own product, we'll talk with the guys from TINTAG ! Who and what is TINTAG? They are the newest startup on the Cluj scene, that have developed an idea in the hardware sector.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tvpY7oIs4Ow

Track your items on the go. TINTAG helps you locate at all times any object you've lost, big or small, near or far. The app on your phone allows you to easily determine the whereabouts of any items that have a TINTAG attached. It alerts you and keeps an eye out for your dog, your kid or a favorite item that leaves your sight and the comfort distance you previously defined within the app.

Andrei will take the lead and he will guide us through the process of what TINTAG is today, with main emphasis on:


• the solution that TINTAG represents


• the presentation of the TINTAG prototype


• technical solutions
