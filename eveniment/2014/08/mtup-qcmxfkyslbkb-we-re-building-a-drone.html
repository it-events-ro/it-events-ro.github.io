We're building a drone!

joi, 7 august 2014

Dear Makers and Designers,

We’re announcing the series of next events that are going to take place within the community. In the following two months we are going to learn how to build a drone and moreover, we are actually going to build one! You know, just to make things more fun and, of course, as we like it, practical.


The plan is to have two meetings per month and have the drone finished by the end of September.


o  August

-> Meetup 1


Kick-off meeting - more details to come

-> Meetup 2


·  Make connectors for motors and rotor controllers

·  Attach battery to the drone body

·  Attach components to drone body


o  September

->  Meetup 3

·  Calibrate flight controller

·  Connect radio controller components

-> Meetup 4

·  Final motor test

·  Liftoff!!!one!

This series of meetups are for people who are interested in learning about drones and how they are built . We are not interested in your background, whether it be technical or not. Basically, if you are interested in knowing how these things work and if you generally have a keen interest in assembling, disassembling and understand how things are made, this is the right place for you!


Next Thursday, we'll be kicking it off with a meeting where we're going to discuss about what a drone is, what it's components are, we're going to have people who have already build one and we're going to plan the next meetups and form the groups for each sessions.


If you have already build a drone previously and you want to share your experience with the groups and be one of the mentors, please email us at [masked].


Have a great week!

Tech Makers and Designers Team
