#13 PM Meetup - Risk Management

joi, 21 august 2014

Hi Everybody, Next we're going to discuss Project Risk Management. This is quite an important factor within a project, as it will allow you, the project manager, to win and not lose money when dealing with uncertain events, simply by having a proactive attitude. By having an effective risk management, your team members will have an even work pace, not having to jump in fire mode, just to make up for the lost time or of for the lost resources. According to PMBOK edition no.5, project risk management includes five process groups: • plan risk management • identify risks • perform qualitative risk analysis • perform quantitative risk analysis • plan risk reponses • control risk Our discussions' leader will be Cristian Baciu. You can register here: http://www.meetup.com/PMI-Romania-Cluj-Napoca-Project-Management-Meetup-Group/events/194808502/. Your contribution is 30 ron or 20 ron if you are a PMI Romania Chapter Member. We look forward to meet you!
