Startup Summer Party

vineri, 29 august 2014

StartupTransilvania.ro managed by Transilvania Entrepreneurship Center together with ClujHUB and their partners are organizing the first large community event dedicated to the startup founders from Cluj. As a constantly growing community we would like to be together as many of us and to celebrate all of the entrepreneurs' actions and dedication in developing businesses and creating Cluj the Romanian center of entrepreneurship. The motivation of this event is: - to make more of us aware of how many we already are - to meet a series of people interested about the startup community in Cluj - to have fun together and to bound deeper connections in order to show mutual support - to meet several stakeholders from the region and from abroad Friday, 29th of August, starting with 17:00 we are waiting the startup founders, tech enthusiasts and product developers to connect over a nice barbecue, drinks and surprise entertainment. Among our special guests, we have some good friends that will share their thoughts and experience on the matter of entrepreneurship: -Fabio Carati, Msc. Strategic Business Developer European Business Accelerator - EIT ICT Labs -Daniel Homorodean, Member of the Cluj IT Cluster Board -Cristian Dascalu - founder of Startup Transilvania -Lorand R. Minyo - Technology and science advisor, in love with disruptive innovation -STEP UP Launch with Cristian Dascalu and Lorand Minyo For startup founders the drinks are free so please RSVP here. BBQ for startups - 15 ron | Drinks & BBQ for non-startups - 30 ron The Beat will bring their contribution to the event and will create a lovely atmosphere with their enthusiastic, young and fresh musical presence. More details to come ... so stay tuned ;)
