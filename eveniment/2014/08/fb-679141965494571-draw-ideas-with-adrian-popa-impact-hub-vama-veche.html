Draw Ideas with Adrian Popa @ Impact Hub Vama Veche

joi, 7 august 2014

Draw Ideas with Adrian Popa @ Impact Hub Vama Veche Adrian is a graphic facilitator passionate about the big picture perspective, who uses live hand drawn images to record meetings and presentations, to guide people through processes and to help them explain ideas to their public in a simpler way. For the last 3 years, he has worked with groups of people engaged in presentations, training sessions and internal processes, providing them with a clear bird’s eye view of their time spent together, creating shared memories and increasing engagement and retention. What will happen We are going to get our hands dirty while finding the best way to visually portray our ideas, literally seeing them through our own eyes and the eyes of others. If you firmly believe that you CAN’T DRAW, I’m pretty sure this experience will provide you with a different perspective!
