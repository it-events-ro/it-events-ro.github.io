Banat Summer Hackathon

vineri, 1 august 2014

Timisoara Startup Hub , Timisoara Mobile Development Group and HackaTimis are calling all the hackers that are still close to their keyboards and not on the beach to join the coolest tech event of this summer.

Building on the existing traditions of Tim Teen IT Summer School , ECAHack , Timisoara Summer Hacks , we have set up a format focused on developing apps and games for the latest technologies in mobile and wearables field.


Participants will have the opportunity to hack and build for a wide set of devices, including mobile and wearable devices (Google Tango & Mozilla Phone prototypes, Google Glass, Sony Watch 2, Estimote beacons), AR drones (Parrot with AR SDK) and robotic toys (Sphero).

The event will benefit of the involvement of well reputed tech experts from Timisoara and beyond, that will act as mentors, jury members and speakers. Also, during the weekend we’ll launch at least two initiatives aiming to support and to offer field of activity to the local hacking community.

Teams developing the best projects will be awarded by Alcatel OneTouch with some of their top of the art devices!

Participants are supposed to contribute 50 RON for covering the costs of two lunches, one dinner, beverages and snacks to be ordered over the weekend. This amount will be requested upon registration.

A more detailed registration form we ask you to fill in can be found at http://bit.ly/BSHackRegForm !

Get in touch with us if you have ANY questions!
