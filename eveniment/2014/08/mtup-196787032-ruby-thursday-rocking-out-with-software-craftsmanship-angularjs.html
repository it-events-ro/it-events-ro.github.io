Ruby Thursday: Rocking out with software craftsmanship + angularJS

joi, 7 august 2014

On 7th August, you are invited to join the Timișoara Ruby Development Community and rock out to the tune of software craftsmanship and angularJS. On the menu are two knowledgeable guests with challenging presentation topics, interesting discussions and the opportunity to catch up with the latest news & developments in the Timișoara Ruby community. Oh, and did we mention ice cream & lemonade? We will also have plenty of those to keep the summer heat at bay.
