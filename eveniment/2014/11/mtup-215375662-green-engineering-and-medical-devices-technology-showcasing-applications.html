Green Engineering and Medical Devices: Technology showcasing & applications

luni, 17 noiembrie 2014

Dear Makers and Designers,

Our next meetup will be focused on discussing hardware and software solutions, taking into consideration actual market trends at a local, national and international level.

At Tech Makers Meetup we encourage sharing and disseminating information. Our purpose is to bring to our community interesting projects and know-how, such that the members will be enabled to further develop new technology on their own.

The session will bring fresh info about projects developed in the sectors of Medical Devices and Green Energy .

Vlad Zileriu from National Instruments will bring topics of immediate importance and interest in what the two sectors are concerned. As part of a company and of teams directly involved in the development and implementation of projects in the two domains, he will discuss about valuable trends on the two markets and about the technology NI has incorporated and can incorporate in such projects.

This meeting is dedicated to those of you who are interested in technological advancements (technical and non-technical persons alike), developers , engineers , medical engineers , environmental engineers , people interested in creating products and services (hardware or software based), founders/co-founders of startups , makers who want to understand market trends , visionaries and futurists . The aim of the event is to generate dialogue, debates and ideas on the basis of the technology that is presented.

In this presentation, the following technologies and projects will be presented:

- technologies for fast prototyping, software and hardware implementation concepts and platforms

- NI product showcasing

- green engineering and medical devices: discussions about applications where NI technologies have been used, going through case studies and debating ideas that could be implemented in these fields

Through this exposure of their products and projects they've been part in, NI is aiming at creating awareness around their products and technologies. Also, NI is presenting their openness towards supporting existing startups and future startups.

Come to this meetup to talk with Vlad and understand how you could benefit from their support : either logistical, either know-how.
