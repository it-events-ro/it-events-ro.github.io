#15 PM Meetup - Monitoring & Controlling Process

joi, 20 noiembrie 2014

Hello all,

Continuing the monthly workshops, we are coming back with a session lead by Dorin Popovici, PMI-ACP, PMP on monitoring and control. He has an extensive experience and many of you already know him.

The Monitoring and Controlling process oversees all the tasks and metrics necessary to ensure that the approved and authorized project is within scope, on time, and on budget so that the project proceeds with minimal risk. This process involves comparing actual performance with planned performance and taking corrective action to yield the desired outcome when significant differences exist. Monitoring and Controlling process is continuously performed throughout the life of the project.

As you well know, this community is meeting from 2013, sharing experiences and best pratices between local PMs. These sessions are independently organized and everybody is welcome, no matter the professional affiliation to a specific company.

Your contribution is 30 ron or 20 ron if you are a PMI Romania Chapter Member.

We look forward to meet again !
