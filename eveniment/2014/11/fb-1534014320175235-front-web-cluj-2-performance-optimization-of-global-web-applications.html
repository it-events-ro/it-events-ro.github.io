Front Web Cluj #2: Performance Optimization of Global Web Applications

marți, 18 noiembrie 2014

Hi guys! Next UP, we're going to meet on the 18th of November and we're gonna have the guys from Trust You coming over and talking about their experience, challenges and developement skills. Going Global - Performance Optimization of a Complex Web Application for a Global Customer Base TrustYou's main product is a social media monitoring platform used by over 10,000 hotels worldwide. Behind the scenes, their application is made up of dozens of different server components, and tens of thousands of lines of JavaScript. Our speaker, Cristian Constantinescu has worked in the TrustYou web application team for 2 years. He is based in our Cluj office. Cristian has a Bachelors Degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and a Masters Degree in Computer System Security. In the past, Cristian has worked at several big IT companies, but currently enjoys the challenging startup environment at TrustYou. His talk will briefly describe the challenges they faced and solved in making the application fast for all their customers - starting from blocking of Western websites in China, general performance problems of serving a "big data" application around the globe, to industry-specific challenges (*cough* Internet Explorer 8 *cough*). ---------------------------------------------------------- Register for this meetup here: http://www.meetup.com/Front-WEB-Cluj/events/218614600/. ---------------------------------------------------------- See you on Tuesday!
