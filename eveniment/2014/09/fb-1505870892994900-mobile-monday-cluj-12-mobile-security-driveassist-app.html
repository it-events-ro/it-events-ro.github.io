Mobile Monday Cluj #12 - Mobile Security & DriveAssist App

luni, 29 septembrie 2014

Hello everybody! This new meetup of mobile developers brings about an interesting subject, that we've been long expecting. We're going to have onboard Sorin Moldovan, who is going to develop on the subject of Mobile Security and how to enhance security on our devices as users and how to enhance security for our apps, as developers, having in mind the safety of our clients. Next up we'll have Razvan Itu, a proud startuper, fond of image analysis and recognition. Razvan is currently involved in developing advanced driver assistance systems using mobile technologies and computer vision. The main project he is working on is a new startup called Drive Assist that represents an augmented Android application meant to assist drivers on public roads and highways. Razvan discovered his passion for software development during high school. He began developing simple web sites for personal use. Later on he found a new interest, mobile development. Nowadays, Razvan has a bachelors degree in computer science, and is studying for a masters degree in artificial intelligence and computer vision. ________________________________ You can register here: http://www.meetup.com/Cluj-Mobile-Developers/events/177046932/. See you on Monday! ________________________________ The event will take place at ClujHUB and your contribution is of 25 lei, representing the entrance fee, the coffee break and snacks. ________________________________ Mobile Monday is a global community - cross platform, that unites many interested parties: mobile developers, people of vision and influencers. The goal is for dialogue to be stimulated, ideas to be exercised and cooperation to be implemented. The event aims at creating an environment appropiate for sharing best practices, and interaction opportunities based on global market trends. Thank you to our sponsors for supporting the local mobile developers' community: IQuest and 3Pillar Global Romania.
