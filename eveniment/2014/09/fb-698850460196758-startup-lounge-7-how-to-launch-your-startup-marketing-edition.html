Startup Lounge #7: How to LAUNCH your startup - marketing edition

marți, 16 septembrie 2014

In this edition, we will discuss about the launch of a startup and how marketing and branding should be approached when talking about this matter. Startups need marketing! No exceptions. Although they assume these developments would be necessary only after a high turnover point is hit, that is not true. Marketing should be part of their business from the very beginning. Starting it is thrilling! But the "build it and they will come" approach isn't much of a reality, as behind the overnight success lies a huge workload and years of commitment and failures.
