The Very First TEDxTarguMuresSalon

joi, 4 septembrie 2014

Our event is called “TEDxTarguMuresSalon”, where the x stands for an independently organized TED event. At “TEDxTarguMuresSalon” we will mix recorded speeches from TEDx conferences, with socializing moments with the purpose of creating deep discussions among the participants and their close ones. The theme we chose for this event, Reinvent yourself by mastering your mind, combines Emotional Intelligence with Body Language - two important concepts with a great interest in discovering more about these aspects nowadays, which can influence our lives in a positive way. We trust that it will have a great impact on the local audience for actively participating in this event, due to the relevant and useful information they can learn and debate with the other participants at the event. Your benefits: • You will have the chance to meet new and interesting people with whom you will socialize and discuss upon ideas on the same interests • You will be a different person after this event • You will have a contribution in bringing an added value to our city and spread the TED spirit in our community • You will spend quality time while you will inform yourself with knowledge that you can use it in your advantage from now on • You get to share your ideas based on the subject • You participate to one of the most important events for people who are interested in self development VERY IMPORTANT: Subscriptions are needed for attending the event! There will be a FREE Entrance and the event will be held in English. ONLY the first 80 people who submit will have a place at the event! We will confirm your place by e-mail!
