Let's build something together! :)

miercuri, 24 septembrie 2014

Hi,

I've been thinking that we should spice things up a bit, as there hasn't been any activity in a while.

There have been some past meetups regarding the lean start-up ideas and about meeting each other. How about having a special meetup where each one is focused on how he can help the other people?

There are multiple ways of doing this. One can share his experience, his contacts, his support and so on and one can benefit in return from doing this by gaining respect in the community, by making friendship bonds even stronger and by having fun in the process of working with potential business partners.

I think this group is a great "safe environment" for all sorts of experiments that can be proven to have success in the future.

What do you think?

The proposal is for Wednesday, the 24th of September 2014, and the location may vary depending upon the number of participants.
