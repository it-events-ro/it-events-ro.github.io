Creative Coffee Cluj #5 - Experimentalist projects: "Transforma Clujul"

vineri, 26 septembrie 2014

How would you transform your city? What would you do to improve it? What do you need so you can make it happen? In this edition of Creative Coffee Cluj we will focus on seven project proposals from the Experimentalist challenge, from June 2014: "Transforma Clujul" (Transform the City of Cluj). The people who worked on these seven proposals, and who will be our speakers, are: • Silviu Medesan - Aproape de Somes is a project of social and cultural activation of the Somes river, which, even though it is the backbone of Cluj, it is completely ignored. Starting with 2015 they will activate the riverbanks through events, exhibits and workshops with the help of a raft that will start from Grigorescu neighborhood, will pass the city center and it will get to Oszer. This project, proposed by Diana Galos, Lala Panait and Silviu Medesan, was the winner of the Experimentalist challenge. • Radu Salcudean - Pavilion expozitional multifunctional. The proposal is to create a multifunctional space that can be used as an unconventional art gallery, or as a projection room, or even as a workshops and debates room. • Diana Buluga - Traseul Artelor project wants to personalize seven tram stops from Cluj-Napoca. The time passed for waiting the tram can be used for reading/watching some thematic and attractive materials, which can be both educational and entertaining. • Ruxandra Barbat - Imprumuta o carte de la banca. The objective is to have mini-libraries all over the city's parks from where people can borrow books. • Alin Grigore - Modernizarea sistemului de depozitare/colectare a gunoiului menajer rezidențial. Alin's project proposal has as its goal the removal of several problems caused by the present waste management system. • Ania Ciotlaus - Camera obscura. The number of ways to present an image is infinite. Her proposal is the construction of a large dimensions dark room with the purpose to transform this space in a photography, drawing and painting experimental space. • Marian Arhip - Responsabilizare sociala. Cluj needs to be aware of how harmful is the individual transport, alone in a metal can.
