Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 15 septembrie 2014

This "back to school" edition of Timisoara Mobile Development Group will take place exactly on September 15th.

The program will include three presentations of three mobile apps:

Alex Tirziu , a high-school kid volunteering for Red Cross/Crucea Rosie will present his First Aid app , acting as a virtual assistant and instructor for persons that have to intervene in emergency health situation.

https://lh5.ggpht.com/So1O0cekA8UvnYjVzeW7B_qQsbTFf5-lqhpURNwvPpCPlBLLCQwp-CVLVvXq9DcGoQ=h310-rw

Ionut "Johnny" Antiu will share the technology and business advances his Hi-Fi music from cloud app, Style Jukebox , records after getting investment from Launchub, the Sofia/Bulgaria VC Fund/accelerator.

Lucian Todorovici , will present his app Med101, the winning project at the recently held Startup Weekend Oradea,  a mobile platform enabling pharmacies to predict and stay informed about medicine requisites or shortages.

In the end of the event the organizers of a major hackathon planned for October will share some info about their project.

More details to follow!
