UI/UX Cluj Meetup#9 - What happens if we make UI/UX changes?

marți, 9 septembrie 2014

Talking with you guys, we understood that you need and you want to see some case studies. So, this is what we bring forward. Alex Ursa, from Netlogiq, will discuss on a particular project, what UI/UX changes he and his team members have made and where those changes lead to. If you are interested in metrics before and after UI/UX changes, than this is the meetup to be present at. As a professional background, Alex has more that 5 years' experience in Marketing and eCommerce. Part of the team of Netlogiq since 2012, he is involved in more than 50 projects, most of them being related to eCommerce. Register here: http://www.meetup.com/UXUICluj/events/177041562/. See you on Tuesday, same place, same hour, The Team ---- In Cluj-Napoca there is a lot of potential for growth in the software sector in more strategic (and better paid areas as well), such as User Experience and User Interaction Design. From this perspective, we are forwarding you this invitation of joining us and collaborating within the UI/UX community. This invitation is addressed to individuals and companies as well. Due to the fact that each company has one, two ot three UI/UX specialists we invite you to connect and thus create a growth environment for the professionals in this area. In this way we are contributing to the overall development and augmentation of specialised talent in the Cluj-Napoca area. We can't wait to meet you and learn from one another!
