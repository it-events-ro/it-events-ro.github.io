Steering Group Theme Presenters Format (for example if we let everyone join, only first 30, ...) Time and Place Little Helping Hands PR We need a steering group to decide all the stuff above. After we have that at place, SG can pick a theme, which will result on getting presenters etc.

Because the next meeting will be already at the beginning of May, we need to take actions soon. I put a date, time and place for this first meeting, but those are just to have something written down. Please, propose something else if you want!

If you have any questions or comments, don't hesitate to let me know.
