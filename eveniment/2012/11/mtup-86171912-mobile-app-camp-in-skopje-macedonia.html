Mobile App Camp in Skopje, Macedonia

vineri, 30 noiembrie 2012

http://photos4.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/9/6/0/c/event_167498412.jpegECAbit , an Eastern Europe and Central Asia Network of Business Incubators and Technology Parks supports the Skopje, Macedonia based YES Foundation member incubator to organize an outstanding international Mobile App Camp , to take place this November 30th-December 2.

Timisoara young mobile developers are highly encouraged to:

- participate,

- meet and learn from world-class speakers and mentors but also from their peers,

- work for developing their applications

- compete for pre-qualification for the March 2013 Selection Days of the Sofia, Bulgaria based Eleven Startup Accelerator

Registration is opened to both individuals or teams.

Organizers can cover costs related to travel, board and lodging for up to 8 Romanian participants, up to 150 EUR/participant.

Please get in contact with me over group's message board in order to get more details and to express your interest to attend.

Link: http://www.meetup.com/TiMoDev/events/86171912/
