Design Patterns Walkthrough & Distributed Scrum Teams

marți, 6 noiembrie 2012

In this meetup we will discuss two topics.

1) Design Patterns Walkthrough
Csaba Patkos will be introducing us to design patterns and how his team is applying them to their work. Here's what he had to say about the session:

I invite you to gather and talk about each design pattern pattern a few minutes. I will prepare a UML schema for each pattern and I will present in a few words the concepts behind each pattern than you can share your opinion about the pattern for another few minutes.

I imagine this would be a good introduction for those who are not familiar with design pattern but also an opportunity to share experiences for those who are.

2) Distributed Scrum Teams

In the second part of our meetup we'll discuss the challenges teams distributed across multiple locations face when trying to work according to agile values and maintain a high level of communication, transparency and trust. Bring your success and your horror stories and let's see what we can generate as a group.
