TiMoDev Academy: The Gil Eluard's iOS Programming Mini-Class

luni, 26 noiembrie 2012

Couple of months ago we've started to include in the TiMo events' agenda short classes conducted by Gil Eluard , iOS tech lead at Three Pillar Global's Timisoara office. They went really well, with 20-30 people following pretty late in the evening (sometimes after 10 PM) Gil's explanations and examples.

We have decided to test a spin off of the mini-trainings from the main TiMo program but keeping them immediately before it, in order to offer to those interested the whole TiMo experience in a single day, rather than splitting it.

So, once in each month, the iOS training will open the TiMo Monday at 6 PM for a packed 45 minutes, to be followed by the main event.

The precise topics to be approached by Gil on November 26th will be announced during the next week.

Just to avoid Android fans from feeling any kind of frustration, we are announcing that they should prepare for a two days weekend Android crush course during January 2013, currently under preparation by Madalin Gageanu .
