Pioneers Festival Vienna presentation

luni, 3 septembrie 2012

On Monday, September 3rd, at 19:00, at J'ai Bistrot, Marius Mocian will talk about the Pioneers Festival conference ( http://pioneersfestival.com/ ), the biggest event for startups in Central Europe, which takes place in October in Vienna. As an ambassador of the conference, he will be able to answer any questions regarding the event. He will focus especially on the Startup Challenge, a startup pitching competition where you can win 25000 EUR, but more importantly, you can get visibility and feedback. Everybody who wants to participate to the challenge, or just attend the conference, is welcome!

Link: http://www.meetup.com/The-Startup-Lab/events/80217502/
