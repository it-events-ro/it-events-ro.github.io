Founders Secrets - Lucian Todea about Soft32

joi, 22 martie 2012

Thursday, we will find the secret for the succes of Soft32 a pioneer of download and reviews websites. With visitors from North America, Europe and Asia, Soft32 delivers simple, powerful and up-to-date reviews and downloads for more than 5 million users worldwide.

Soft32Updater is developed as an extension of Soft32 that complements it very well by first of all solving a major problem most PC and Windows users are having: chasing, searching, finding and installing the latest software versions.

The meeting is part of a series of events designed to inspire us to open our business minds as to be prepared for a global economy where technology actually changes the present much faster than we expect.

About Lucian Todea Lucian

Todea is the Founder and CEO of ITNT, an online publisher and provider of digital content. Actively involved with various other technology & internet projects.
He was named one of the "The 1000 most important businessmen" in “Who’s Who in Business Romania – The 1000 most important businessmen" by ZF (2008, 2009 and 2010),  "Top 100 young managers" by Business Magazine (2007).
ITNT was named in 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011 as one of "The Biggest players in the Economy"  in: “Anuarul de business al Romaniei – Cei mai mari jucatori din economie” (2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011)
When he is not online,  Lucian loves to read books, play games, drink wine, and explore the world.
