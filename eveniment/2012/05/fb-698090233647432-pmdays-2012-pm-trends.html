PMdays 2012 - PM Trends

vineri, 25 mai 2012

PMdayS invites you to train your imagination. With a new format and renowned speakers, this event will not grant you any break for the mind. Throughout the two days of this event, we will go from Agile to Risk Management, Business Creativity and Innovation. In charge with instigating the audience to debate will be Fredrik Härén, Alexandre Cuva, Michel Thiry, Roland Gareis, Peter Taylor and many others. If we add as a special ingredient the 10th anniversary of PMI Romania Chapter, we have all the premises for a “Week” (of just 2 days) dedicated to Project Management in Romania. Are we too demanding, maybe? Will PMdays 2012 become the reference we all need for the future? Definitely, we all wish for such a thing to happen. If you allow me an analogy, I wish for PMdays 2012 to become the hot couture event for the Project Management in Romania. Are we going to be successful in creating such an event? You are all invited to find out together with us! For more information about this event please visit www.pmdays.ro
