Hackers dating. Work in a startup, a very unusual one!

vineri, 20 iulie 2012

You'll always hear amazing serial entrepreneurs saying that working in a startup is the first step towards becoming a founder yourself. We believe the reasons are obvious: testing if it fits you, learning a bunch about how to run a startup, knowledge you'll rarely access in a corporation.

And even if you are not willing to set up a startup yourself right away, here's the challenge we are throwing down to you.

Backyard Brains , a very unusual tech startup founded by Greg Gage and Tim Marzullo, making neuroscience available to everyone, meaning for the first time ever, school children and amateur scientists will have access to similar tools used by neuroscientists worldwide to study Electrophysiology : the electrical activity of neurons . By following a few simple steps, everyone can experience how the brain is able to communicate our senses, memories, hopes, and desires!

So these cool dudes are looking for a couple of hardcore coders to join the team. They would like to make an extension to their Backyard Brains iOS application to allow them to do "Spike Sorting".

And we also thought, a cool event should happen in a cool space. So, here we are co-organizing it with The HUB Bucharest in their freshly open location in the Old Town.

See you there and Greg, and Tim over Skype!
