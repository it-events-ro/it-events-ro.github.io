"A Semantic People Recommendation Engine exploring Trusted Connections"

marți, 31 ianuarie 2012

Our guest is Mr. Zoltán Kisgyörgy, Master in Information Technology, www.reea.net

"The usage of the social platforms (e.g. Facebook, Twitter) has largely increased in recent years. Millions of micro-documents (i.e posts, up-dates, tweets, comments, etc.) are published by users each day. These contents build up a huge knowledge base which offers potential possibility to understand the user's real-time interests better. However these contents have lots of limitations such as short size, ambiguity, unstructured syntax and lack of contextual information. That is the reason why they have not been exploited significantly yet. On the other hand, to deal with this huge amount of data, a semantic transformation process into machine-readable data is also necessary. As the result of these considerations, we propose to exploit the technologies of Semantic Web to address this problem."
