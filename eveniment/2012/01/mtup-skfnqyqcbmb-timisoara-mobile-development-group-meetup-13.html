Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup #13

luni, 16 ianuarie 2012

We had to postpone the first TiMo event for 2012, from the second to the third Monday of the month. The winter holidays and the skying vacations induced some difficulties in shaping the program.

At this time, for January 16th we can announce that you will be able to attend an edition centered on three presentations:

1. Oana & Arthur Csertus and Valentin Keleti , the current team of CobaltSign , will talk about 1tap2send , their first launched Windows Phone application, already finalist in the Core77 design competition. The app permits you to generate predefined messages and to send them extremely rapid, if needed.

http://photos1.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/6/9/e/8/event_84867112.jpeg2 . Adrian Mirea and his colleagues from Haufe Lexware will  showcase Lexware Zeitmanagement a time management application developed here in Timisoara, available for various mobile platforms (Android, iOS, Windows Phone) already downloaded by several tens of thousand of users, mostly from DACH countries.

http://photos1.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/6/b/0/event_85261712.jpeg3 . Mihai Gheza of Bandello , a Copenhagen-based indie gaming agency, will talk with us over Skype about the indie games industry, his experience with founding an indie studio and working for an indie agency. Topics include: self-publishing or dealing with publishers, Scandinavian indie hits (Minecraft, Angry Birds, Limbo), risks and challenges to face before cashing, promotion, funding opportunities and some gossip :).

http://photos3.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/7/0/f/0/event_84868912.jpeg4 . Petre Popescu will share with us the current version of Matter Wars, an iPad strategy gaming  application, currently under development. Petre is looking forward for your feedback and beta testing commitments.

We are looking forward to meet you!
