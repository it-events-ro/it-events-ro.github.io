The Social Web

luni, 23 ianuarie 2012

What is the Social Web and the Social Media? How do Big Brands create and manage their presence on the Social Web? How are the communities created, monitored, exploited and how is their feedback measured? These are only a few of the questions we intend to address during this meetup.


Invited speakers:
Alexandru Tabalae - Mechanical Engineer at ARRK Research and Development former software journalist - cupluta.ro; injineru.ro
Radu Meza -  Assistant Professor at the Journalism Department teaching courses and seminars on Journalism and Popular Culture, Media Analysis, Online Journalism, Narrative Techniques and Media Studies - mezaradu.ro
