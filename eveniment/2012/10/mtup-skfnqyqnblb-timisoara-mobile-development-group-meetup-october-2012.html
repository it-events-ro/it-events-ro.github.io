Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup October 2012

luni, 15 octombrie 2012

Somehow against the TiMo habits, we know one month in advance the whole program of this mid-October event:

Lucian Pacurar will present Stb CRM Mobile, a mobile extension for the CRM app developed by his company. We'll hear about his experience and the roadblocks faced in the process of developing a cross platform native mobile app, for iOS and Android, using Appcelerator's Titanium Mobile Development Platform, connected to the Stb CRM back office using RESTful web services.

Stb Software Development is a Dutch company supporting customer relations and business processes for 20 years on the European market, and lately on the North American market, too.

The second presentation will refer to the kick-start Ionut "Johnny" Antiu and Radu Prescura of Digital Geek had with the September launched, cloud-based, cross platform, mobile-in-mind designed music app Style Jukebox . With about 5000 activation in the first 2 weeks and a seamless deployment, the success of the app proves that the massive development period of more than a year was worth living by the two geeks and their team.

http://photos4.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/4/1/8/e/event_159316782.jpegWithin the third presentation, the Arad-based Claudiu Cristea (managing partner of Webikon and president of Drupal Romania Association )  will showcase how Drupal served as a mobile app development platform for several projects like one delivering real-time, rich data and great user experience for clients of one of the Europe's crowded airports.

Of course, we'll continue in the end of the meetup with TiMo iOS development mini-trainings with Gil Eluard .

If you feel inspired and want to share something related to your activity or interest in the mobile tech or business, please registered for the 3 lightning speeches slots (3 mins each) we want to include between the 4 main presentations.

As in the previous editions, our hosts will charge participants an amount of 15 RON, offering them the possibility to serve 3 items (beer, non-alcoholic beverages, snacks or sweets).
