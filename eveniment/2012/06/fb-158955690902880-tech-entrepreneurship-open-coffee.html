Tech Entrepreneurship Open Coffee

duminică, 10 iunie 2012

As if this period wasn't enough complicated for you, Timisoara tech (&) entrepreneurship community members, I am challenging you with a new, short-noticed but still enjoyable, hopefully, event. So, beside the Timisoara GeekMeet #19 (this Saturday) and the Prezi Camp Timisoara (coming Monday) and the local elections turmoil, my offer to you is to: join a set of highly relevant people for tech entrepreneurship, coming from Berlin, Cluj and Bucharest for a relaxed, Sunday morning Open Coffee. The special guest for this event are: Daniela Neumann, Senior Product Manager at Rocket Internet GmbH, Berlín (http://uk.linkedin.com/in/joindaniela) Robert Muresan, Game designer and owner of exosyphen studios, Cluj Napoca (http://ro.linkedin.com/in/mrobert) Bogdan Pencea, Project Manager la DasCloud.ro, Bucharest (http://www.linkedin.com/profile/view?authType=name&locale=en_US&id=9755401&authToken=7N3v) We are inviting up to 20-25 Timisoara people interested in tech entrepreneurship to join us for a relaxed discussion on their current and future projects and on the way Timisoara could be better connected into a national, continental and global network of startup activities that could shortcut our paths to success. Participants are kindly asked to be prepared to pay an amount of 15 RON to cover the catering cost.
