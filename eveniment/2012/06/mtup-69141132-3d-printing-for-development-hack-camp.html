3D Printing for Development Hack Camp

vineri, 22 iunie 2012

The rise of 3D printing has been compared to the beginning of the industrial era in how deeply it might impact our society.

TechSoup, EESTEC and TechforTrade will host Dr. Phil Reeves and the folks from http://www.3d4dchallenge.org for a 3D printing hack day in which you can learn about, talk about, brainstorm about and play with a 3D printer!

This is all part of the 3D4D (3D for Development) Challenge through which socially entrepreneurial ideas can win up to $100,000 to kick start their 3D for society idea.

We are limited to about 35 seats so please sign up early for this exciting event. See you there!
