Junior Hackers - Gathering #3

marți, 28 iulie 2015

This Tuesday, the 3rd gathering of Junior Hackers - Web Development Group will take place. If you would like to learn Web like a boss, or to find out what Jr. Hackers is all about, join in and let's get to know each other. When: Tuesday, July the 28th, 19:00 - 22:00 Where: Hackerspace Iasi - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Powered by Silicon Hills & Hackerspace Iasi - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - <> This time we'll be spicing things a bit, especially for those on level 1 & 2: ** Those on level 1 (grinding tutorials on codecademy): We will be starting on the basics of building an actual website (the one presented for those in level 2), on which you shall improve, for Gathering #4. ** Those on level 2 (grinding features for the actual website): First we will be going through your mindmap (the logic you used when creating the website) - After which the mentors will present the "appropriate" process flow for future projects. The second part will be focused on establishing together certain features that will be added to your websites, and working on implementing them. ** Those on level 3 (working on your own projects): Same formula, focusing on individual discussions with the mentors, and working on your specific issues. <> There will be two new features for the next gathering: 1. All participants will be split in groups of 2-3 and will be give 3 challenges to complete throughout the gathering. 2. All participants will be given a partner and a collaborative task to work on until Gathering #4. - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - << Important >> This gathering is solely focused on Web Development, and is targeted to beginners. For other technologies/programming languages there will be future meetings, once those specific Study Groups will be launched. - - - Those who are interested in joining the program, must register on the website, and complete the application form: https://siliconhills.wordpress.com/projects/junior-hackers-after-dark/ The program costs 40Ron / Month and has no deadline, depending solely on the participant's determination, rhythm of learning and the goals he/she has set. The participants must also bring their own laptop.
