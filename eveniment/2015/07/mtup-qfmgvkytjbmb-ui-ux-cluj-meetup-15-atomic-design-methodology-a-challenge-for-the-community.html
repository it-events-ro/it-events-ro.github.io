UI/UX Cluj Meetup #15 - Atomic Design methodology+ a challenge for the community

marți, 21 iulie 2015

In this edition of the UI/UX meetup, we will have a presentation and a challenge for the community. First of all, Sebastian Big (UI/UX designer at Betfair) will present us the Atomic Design methodology and then we will work on two case studies for the startups GoMountain and Crypto Teller Machines. Then we'll enjoy the summer evening in the beautiful Cluj HUB garden.

Agenda:

6.15 - 6.30 - Come, get your nametag, and grab a coffee while we wait for the community to gather

6.30 - 7.15 - Sebastian Big (Betfair) - Atomic design? Design atomic!

Atomic design is a methodology for creating design systems. Let’s see if we can apply this methodology to the actual design workflow.

7.15 - 7.35 - Analyzing the UI/UX interface of the GoMountain application.


GoMountain is a mobile friendly web application that provides its users with objective, relevant, real-time information on the activities you can perform in mountain resorts. The application is based on a community that will provide information via reviews. In addition, the application will allow users to book accommodation, rent winter sports equipment, access the services of an instructor / guide and more.


7.35 - 7.50 Analyzing the UI prototype of the Crypto Teller Machines startup bitcoin self-serviced kiosks.


Crypto Teller Machines is a hardware manufacturer, focused on Bitcoin Exchange Kiosks, serving an emerging community of more than 3M users. Its main goal is building affordable self-service kiosks while maintaining a high service level.


At this meetup we will ask the UI/UX communuity to give us a helping hand in developing the look & feel of the machine, as well as crafting an outstanding user experience when interacting with the machine.


7.50 - 8.10 Analyzing the web application of SportMix.


Sportmix is a startup that proposes a web application that lets you discover nearby people you can play sports with. You can create a Game and invite people or you can join an existing one. Sportmix also enables you to connect with people that play the same sports as you and discover new cool stuff to try out!


8.10 onwards - networking time :)
