Junior Hackers - Gathering #1

marți, 21 iulie 2015

This Tuesday, the first gathering of Junior Hackers - Web Development Group will take place. If you would like to learn Web like a boss, or to find out what Jr. Hackers is all about, join in and let's get to know each other. When: Tuesday, July the 21st, 19:00 - 22:00 Where: Hackerspace Iasi - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Powered by Silicon Hills & HackerSpace Iasi - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - << Important >> This gathering is solely focused on Web Development, and is targeted to beginners. For other technologies/programming languages there will be future meetings, once those specific Study Groups will be launched. The first part, of 30-60 minutes (from 19:00 - 20:00) is open access to all, after which the gathering will continue for those that have subscribed to the program. - - - Those who are interested in joining the program, must register on the website, and complete the application form: https://siliconhills.wordpress.com/projects/junior-hackers-after-dark/ The program costs 40Ron / Month and has no deadline, depending solely on the participant's determination, rhythm of learning and the goals he/she has set. The participants must also bring their own laptop - we provide the wifi and cofee :) - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Schedule: 19:00 - 19:30: General presentation - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - We will talk about what the study groups are really all about, How will the way of working be, What is the meetings schedule & What the participants' expectations are. 19:30 - 20:00: General discussion with the mentors - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - There will be an open panel of discussion regarding the general interests of the participants and their current level of knowledge. 20:00 - 20:30: Individual discussions with the mentors - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Each participant will have the chance to have a quick talk with the mentors, so that the mentors can make a personalized plan of action for each participant. 20:30 - 22:00: Start working - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - After each participant has talked with the mentors, he will find a spot to settle down, take out his laptop and start working or following the recommendations given to them.
