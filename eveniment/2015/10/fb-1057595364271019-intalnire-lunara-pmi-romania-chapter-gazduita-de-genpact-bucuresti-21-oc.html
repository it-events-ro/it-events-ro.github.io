Intalnire Lunara PMI Romania Chapter gazduita de Genpact, Bucuresti, 21 Octombrie 2015

miercuri, 21 octombrie 2015

PMI Romania Chapter are placerea sa va invite la o noua editie a Intalnirilor Lunare organizate in Bucuresti, gazduita de Genpact in data de 21 Octombrie 2015. Genpact (NYSE: G) is the architect of the Lean DigitalSM enterprise, generating impact for hundreds of clients including more than one-fourth of the Fortune Global 500 Locatia: Sediul Genpact, Bld. Dimitrie Pompeiu, 9-9A, Iride Business Park, Cladirea 10, Etaj 3, Sector 2, Bucuresti Agenda Evenimentului 18:00 – 18:30 – Inregistrare & Cafea 18:30 – 19:00 – Introducere Genpact – Project & Risk Management Culture 19:00 – 19:45 – Risk Management @ Genpact 19:45 – 20:00 – Pauza / Socializare 20:00 – 20:45 – Discutie tip Panel: Risk Management 20:45 – 21:00 – Socializare Prezentatori / Invitati Luc Mauvarin – Europe Latin America Africa Practices Solutions Transformations Services Leader Luc has over 25 years of varied experience, mostly with GE where he previously held several Global and European roles inside the GE Healthcare Services and Engineering teams, in France, India and USA. He is a certified Six Sigma Black Belt and holds an Engineer degree in Mechanical from Université de Technologie de Compiegne, France (UTC). Ana Maria Bejenariu – EU & LATAM Transition Lead, 10+ years Project Management experience and Shared Service set up Consulting. She is PMP certified since 2010 and holds a degree in Foreign Languages from Bucharest University. Kapil Agarwal – Transition Lead Global Accounts, 10+ years Project Management experience, 8+ years leading Transitions in EU. He is Six Sigma Green Belt certified and holds a MBA in Banking and Finance from NMIMS, premier Management Institute in India, Post Graduate in Insurance Management, Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from Delhi University Pentru aceasta intalnire sunt disponibile 40 de locuri. Daca sunteti certificat ca PMP, PMI-SP, PMI-RMP si PMI-ACP puteti revendica 2 PDU-uri prin participarea la acest eveniment, in urma primirii codului evenimentului de la PMI Romania. Conditii de participare daca nu sunteti membru PMI Romania Chapter: Daca nu ati mai participat si la alte intalniri lunare ale PMI Romania in anul curent. Dorim astfel sa acordam acces persoanelor interesate sa devina membri ai organizatiei noastre si care doresc sa cunoasca mai intai beneficiile provenite din aceasta afiliere. Va recomandam sa solicitati si alte detalii despre organizatia noastra adresandu-va membrilor board-ului PMI Romania prezenti la aceasta intalnire. Daca sunteti invitatul unui membru PMI Romania. Este necesar ca membrul PMI Romania Chapter sa transmita un mesaj pe adresa comunicare@pmi.ro continand numele, adresa de email si nr. de telefon ale acestuia.
