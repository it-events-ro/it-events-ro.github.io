ISTC Workshop - 31 October, Hotel Ramada

sâmbătă, 31 octombrie 2015

In the first part of the event we will take our first steps in Jenkins. What it is? What it is use for and who uses it? You will learn how to create a job and configure it, how to create reports and send emails and some tips & tricks. In the second part of the event we will hear "A story about attitude and ownership" narrated by Florin Asavoaie. The event will take place on October 31, at Hotel Ramada. The activities will begin at 09:00 so make sure to be there in time :) Registration Due to the limited number of seats available, if you wish to participate, please access this form and fill in the required data: http://goo.gl/forms/laOl0Bq8n1 Following your online subscription, you will receive a confirmation message and in the shortest time more details about the selection process and other event details. The registration period is until October 29. The selection of participants will take place on October 30, 2015 when we will also contact all selected participants for a firm attendance confirmation. Please note that if you do not confirm participation, we will forward the available opening to the next qualifying candidate. P.S. Bring a laptop or find a partner to share one with, you will be using it to get hands-on experience with Jenkins Thank you for your interest in the Iasi Testing Community events!
