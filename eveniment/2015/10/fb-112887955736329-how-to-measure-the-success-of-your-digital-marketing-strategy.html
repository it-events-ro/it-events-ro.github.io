How to measure the success of your digital marketing strategy

vineri, 23 octombrie 2015

This edition is all about the metrics. This DM meetup is open not only to marketers but also to startup owners, who would like to to their marketing themselves but don't know for sure if they are headed in the right direction. We will have an awesome guest speaker that will also present at TeCOMM. Agenda: 18.00 - 18.15 Guests arrive 18.15 - 19.00 Dragos Smeu, senior performance manager at Kubis Interactive - Performance campaign for your startup You are used to start from scratch. You made up your mind about technology, providers, production, distribution, costs and all things about product management but what about media promotion? What’s the best media channel, what strategy suits best for your needs, what’s your main audience and where you can find it and what should be your benchmarks and the KPIs that you need to achieve? Dragos will try to answer all those questions on 23rd of October at Cluj Hub. 19.00 - 19.30 Raluca Ciocian-Ardeleanu, Communication strategist @Cluj HUB - 5 Google Analytics tips & tricks you should be using Google Analytics is the preferred analytics tool that dominates the market. With its easily accessible and fairly easy to implement tracking, you can set it up in no time. But are you doing Google Analytics right? Let's find out. After the event, you are invited to join us to know each other better, share some industry tips and tricks together and enjoy the friendly HUB environment. See you there! __________________ About the speakers: Dragos Smeu He is Performance Manager @Kubis Interactive, with more than 5 years of experience in online marketing and pay per click marketing. Until now he has invested over 1 million EURO in performance campaigns for more than 150 clients from more than 100 different areas of activity: from e-commerce shops for books, fashion or agriculture to B2B or online platforms from brewing industry, beauty or FMCG. You can find him on www.smeu.ro & www.kubisinteractive.com. Raluca Ciocian Raluca is our very own communication strategist, and she has been working with website optimization and monitoring for more than 4 years. Apart from her work here, she freelances on upwork and she has some side projects where she volunteers. She loves to educate herself about analytics and how to better websites. _____________________________ The event fee is 25 lei, payable at the door, and it includes the snacks and drinks offered at the coffee break. Looking forward to gather the community again!
