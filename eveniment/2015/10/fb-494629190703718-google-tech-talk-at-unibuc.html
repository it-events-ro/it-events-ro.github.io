Google Tech Talk at UNIBUC

marți, 27 octombrie 2015

Mihai Damaschin (Google Software Engineer and UNIBUC alumni) will be giving a talk on contextual/conversational Search. We hope to see you there! When: 27th October @ 18:00 Where: amf. Spiru Haret Abstract: Our phones are turning from simple devices into the equivalent of a human assistant. For this to take place your phone has to be able to understand what you're saying, be able to reply, and understand the things in your environment and your life. In this talk we'll go through what Google is doing to turn this into reality. We'll be looking at conversational search, location aware search and Now-On-Tap.
