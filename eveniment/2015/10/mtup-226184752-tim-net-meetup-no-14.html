Tim.Net Meetup No 14

miercuri, 28 octombrie 2015

“Talk is cheap. Show me the code.” ― Linus Torvalds

We’re showing you all the code at our next #TimNet meetup!

Adrian Cuzman is a software engineer that loves the IT technology, starting from the smallest AT tiny microcontrollers to the multi-tier applications, but from all of them, making 2D/3D games is considered by him the most fun part of programming.

The topic of his presentation will be ‘Unity introduction using C#’.

Flavius-Radu Demian has a passion for software architecture and experiments on .Net, Android on iOS. His interests lie in OPP principals and patterns, cross-platform approaches and Xamarin. He is also the General Manager of his newly founded software company, @Deventure. He is the main organizer of the Tim.Net Meetup.

Flavius-Radu Demian will put the loupe on another hot topic, 'C# everywhere - Building Cross-Platform Apps with Xamarin and MvvmCross’.
