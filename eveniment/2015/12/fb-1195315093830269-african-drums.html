African Drums

duminică, 20 decembrie 2015

Ce ? The worskhop aims to connect us with African-style hand drum as an aid to relaxation and meditation. We will work together as a team, to relax and also play the African Drum called Djembe, communicate through the rhythm and thus create an atmosphere appropriate for expressing creative emotions and positive energies. A spiritual experience and a very accessible activity, therefore, if you are a beginner you will learn to play together as an orchestra in just a few minutes, leading to a sense of joy and self - satisfaction. Cu cine? Mark Oostendorp together with PeeJay Vincent and Lolo Noukpetor formed the band called Ikhaya Drums. Ikhaya means house or home in Nguni language, also in the sense of meeting place for sharing ideas, visions and experiences. Ikhaya includes creative minds, visionary and enthusiastic users and anybody who participates. The individual is not ikhaya. Când? Duminică@incubator107 , 20 Decembrie de la 19:00 la 21:00 Unde?
