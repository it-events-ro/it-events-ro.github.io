How to make African braids?

joi, 14 mai 2015

Ce ? The braids tradition came from Africa, where they were more than simple hair ornaments. The style of making the braids had more significations: it could show the social status of a person or even the interest of a person for getting married Nowadays the braid is a classical hairstyle symbol for us. Being an afro braid, crown or more complicated, they can be found everywhere, from the catwalk to the streets, and even on the heads of celebrities. I will teach you three types of braids: fishtail braid, root braid and embedded braid. Cu cine? Maria, EVS volunteer in Cluj Napoca, original from Cape Vert, but living in Portugal.
