TEDxTârguMureșSalon - "Happiness is an inside job"

joi, 7 mai 2015

Dear TEDxers, Here we come with the 3rd edition of TEDxSalon! VERY IMPORTANT: Subscriptions are needed for attending the event! Register here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1_0cQxpH-fOlpq5XqYOOomCW4ZGJ-SAM4nKaYUg-YlXU/viewform Entrance tickets: 15 RON The 3rd edition for TEDxTârguMureșSalon will be hosted with the theme based on Happiness - "Happiness it's an inside job". We have chosen this theme based on the suggestions given by all of you whom completed the subscription form for the second Salon. With this theme, Happiness, we want to recognize which are those "triggers" that make us people happy: actions, things, feelings, other people etc. therefore realize how we can explore this in everyday's life. We believe that we can change our state of mind by feeling happy everyday while we notice all the small delights which surround us and be grateful for having them. We are more than happy to have with us as speaker and moderator Simona Ciff psychologist, trainer and author. She was one of the speakers for our 1st TEDxTârguMureșSalon. More information about Simona Ciff: https://about.me/simona.ciff Our event is called “TEDxTarguMuresSalon”, where the x stands for an independently organized TED event. At “TEDxTarguMuresSalon” we will mix recorded speeches from TEDx conferences, with socializing moments with the purpose of creating deep discussions among the participants and their close ones.
