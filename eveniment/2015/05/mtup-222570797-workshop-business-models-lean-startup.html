Workshop Business Models & Lean Startup

marți, 19 mai 2015

Ever wondered how other people make money? Ever wondered what's their plan? Join us to learn how to structure your business, how to approach startup development and basically how to make a business plan that works for you and your idea.

Tips & Tricks will be shared by our 2 experienced guests: Mircea Vandan and Dumitru Radoiu.

Mircea Vadan will talk us trough Business Model Canvas which is a shorter and more productive version of the classical business plan and also he will share his knowledge on Lean Startup Methodology or how to scientifically approach creating and managing startups and get a desired product to customers' hands faster.

Moving on to the practical part of the evening Dumitru Radoiu you will give you the chance to actually create a business model canvas and receive feedback on it on the spot!

More about our coaches:

MIrcea is keen on customer development and user aquisition. Previous to Seed For Tech, co-founded 2 startups with which he participated in 2 accelerators and raised investment. He is driven to help startups and is active in the tech startups community.

Dumitru Radoiu is Founder and Financier at Startup Lab, Co-Founder and President of the Board at Sysgenic. He believes in the future, in setting free peoples' creativity. The person who says it cannot be done should not interrupt the person doing it. Business incubation is not about putting people into buildings but about putting "buildings" into people. Bring together the right people in one place, set free their creativity and you'll be amazed what they come up with.
