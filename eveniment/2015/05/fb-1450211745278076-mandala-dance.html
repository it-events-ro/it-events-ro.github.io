Mandala Dance

vineri, 22 mai 2015

Ce ? Atelier spontan în Parcul Central cu meșterii noștri din Ucraina. Cute and lovely girls, I am inviting you to come in contact with real You, open your feminine nature, because everything is integrated within you. Everything is easy and simple and has already been given to us by Mother Nature, we should just trust life and let it go to dance. We will practice the temple Mandala dance, connect with the elements of nature and awaken your sensuality. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iHraPRl9Rgw Cu cine? My name is Alyona I'm from Ukraine, I am a practicing psychotherapist. In my work, I paid special attention to female practices, use physical therapy, art therapy and dance. Cu ce? Please, wear long dresses and pick up litter. Când? Vineri, 22 Mai de la 18:00 la 20:00 Unde? Ne vedem în faţa cazinoului din Parcul Central şi alegem împreună locaţia. (See you in front of the Casino in the Central Park and we will choose together a place) You have to register on our website: http://cluj.incubator107.com/workshops/1274 We suggest a donation for every workshop/presentation . It helps the project Incubator107 Cluj grow and also encourage the trainer. The donation suggested is 10 RON for every workshop. If you can't afford, no worries, you can help us with sharing our project or being a volunteer or even more, a trainer. We are waiting for you!
