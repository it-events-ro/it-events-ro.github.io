Pioneers Bus - Pitching on wheels

miercuri, 27 mai 2015

Let's travel together to Vienna for the awesome Pioneers Festival! The Pioneers Bus gives you the possibility to: - get discounted tickets to the festival - participate in a pitching contest that gives you the possibility to participate in an exclusive pitch competition the evening before Pioneers Festival, where startups can get exposure and possibly even publicity for their products - win a wild card to a speed dating session with selected international media In order to find out more about the opportunities you have, visit this link: http://pioneers.io/festival2015/travel-pitch/ In order to get a discounted ticket for the festival, write us an email on startup@clujhub.ro, and we will also keep a seat for you on the bus! Route: Cluj-Napoca - Oradea - Budapest - Vienna Seats: 15 Departure: 27th of may, in the morning Come meet the startup community in the region, practice your pitch around other teams & mentors and have lots of fun in the meantime!
