JavaScript Cluj Meetup #2 – AngularJS Internals - Digest

marți, 26 mai 2015

Are you a JS dev interested in the latest trends? Then join us at the 2nd meetup of JS Cluj Community – Angular JS Internals - Digest JS Cluj Community meetup its all about connecting developers and facilitating the coming together of like minded people to share their knowlege as well as keeping you informed on best practices and future directions. The community is entirely focused on technical topics with participation of local specialists. // Agenda: 6:15 – Doors open 6:30 – Event starts with welcome from the organizers 6:40 – Key Note Presentation: Cornel Stefanache – AngularJS Internals - Digest AngularJS is a JavaScript framework that improves performance, but can’t solve all your problems. No matter how quick your framework, you can always write code that reduces performance. Understanding key concepts in Angular is an important step in developing fast applications. The $digest cycle is one of the features that brought Angular the popularity it has today. It is the foundation between $scope, directive and controller and is responsible of keeping the model and DOM consistent. The presentation will demo the way the $digest cycle works and reacts internally. 7:20 – Networking // About the speaker: • Cornel Stefanache, Technical Lead at 3Pillar Global See you there and spread the word with fellow JS developers! --------------------------------------------------------------- Please confirm your participation by registering here: http://www.meetup.com/JavaScript-Cluj/events/222048591/ as places are limited. --------------------------------------------------------------- Registration on Meetup is mandatory!
