Creative Mingle #1

miercuri, 27 mai 2015

Creative Mingle #1 - Graphic Design Edition • ABOUT Let people know what you do! Creative Mingle aims to bring together people from a range of design fields, including UI/UX, web design, print and illustration. Held in a friendly and cozy location, it gives you the opportunity to meet and greet other creative minds in Cluj-Napoca, for a full evening of networking, sharing experiences and know-how. See creative professionals share their work and get valuable insights, present your own projects to open up for collaborations and job offers. Creative Mingle is all about sharing and socializing, getting to know people with the same passion as yours. TALK. EXCHANGE. ENGAGE • SCHEDULE 18:00 - Warm-up 19:00 - Presentations 20:30 - The Mingle • SPEAKERS Didi Kasa - www.didikasa.com Péter 'Dutzu' Árpád - https://www.behance.net/dutzu Zöld Levente - https://www.behance.net/leventezold Stefan Asafti - https://www.behance.net/stefanasafti Iulian Iancu - https://www.behance.net/IulianIancu • PRESENTATION Introduce yourself to the community by showcasing your creative skills and let everyone see your work, with a short presentation. Feel free to drop us a message or give us a call to discuss the details. Don't be shy! You could get the chance to find people to collaborate with. We aim to create a united community and help eachother out • PLACES & INFO We have 40 open seats for the event, so hurry up and confirm yours and get a table by sending us a message or giving us a call. Whether you're looking forward to present your work or you're coming just for the mingle, we welcome you. • ORGANIZERS AND CONTACT The Creative Cluj team is here to answer any of your questions, get your confirmation and details: Burciu Razvan - 0740550391 Csongor Horvath - 0747057724 Razvan Calin - 0743426567
