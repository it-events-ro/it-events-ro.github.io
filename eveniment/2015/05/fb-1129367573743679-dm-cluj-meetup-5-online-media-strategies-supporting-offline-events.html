DM Cluj Meetup #5 - Online media strategies supporting offline events

marți, 5 mai 2015

In this edition of the Digital Marketing Cluj Meetup, we will talk about the way in which we can choose the right communication channels and strategies for creating engagement around offline events. Nowadays, most of us rely strongly on the internet for reading news, finding out about events, organizing meetings and so on. This is why the digital strategies play an important role in the success of an offline event, be it a conference, a concert or a launch. As marketers, it is important to know how to make people enthusiastic about attending an event and how to keep the sense of community and engagement during and after the event, via social media and other digital channels. Let's learn from two speakers that can share with us from their experience.
