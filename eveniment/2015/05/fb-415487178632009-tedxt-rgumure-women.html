TEDxTârguMureșWomen

vineri, 29 mai 2015

Va asteptam in data de 29 mai, 2015, la Sala Flora din incinta Cinema Arta, Targu-Mures, pentru a fi prezenti la TEDxTârguMureșWomen 2015, o transmisie live a unei sesiuni din cadrul conferintei TEDWomen 2015 Momentum (Avant), organizata in 27-29mai 2015 in Monterrey, CA. Participarea este gratuita si se va face pe baza inscrierii, prin completarea urmatorului formular: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/10ILFB7BB6K4jrYdEcPG_zkD0jnqyOylyVVO2G_disP8/viewform In urma inscrierii, veti fi contactat/a de unul dintre membrii echipei de organizare pentru a confirma prezenta. Avantul. Forta de miscare. Cresterea vitezei. Crearea tractiunii. La TEDxTârguMureșWomen 2015 vom explora idei indraznete care vor da avant modului in care gandim, traim si lucram. Pentru a descoperi scanteia care inspira inovatia. Pentru a aduce la suprafata provocarile care vin impreuna cu schimbarea sociala, descoperirile si explorarea. Pentru a seduce cu strategii, solutii si noi moduri de a observa si crede. Pentru a intelege cum liderii globali isi desfasoara activitatea pentru a crea impact de durata. Pentru a schimba punctul de vedere, dezbaterea, competitia. Pentru a impartasi povesti care ne unesc si intaresc comunitati. Suntem incantati sa ne implicam in crearea acestui avant! The TEDxTârguMureșWomen 2015 event will take place on Mai 29th, at the Flora Hall from Cinema Arta, Targu-Mures. This event represents a live streaming of a session from the TEDWomen 2015 Momentum conference, organized in Monterrey, Ca between 27-29 May 2015. Attendance is free and will be based on registering by completing the following form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/10ILFB7BB6K4jrYdEcPG_zkD0jnqyOylyVVO2G_disP8/viewform Shortly after registration, you will be contacted by our team to confirm your presence. Momentum. Moving forward. Gaining speed. Building traction. At TEDxTârguMureșWomen 2015, we’ll explore the bold ideas that create momentum in how we think, live and work. To reveal the spark that inspires innovation. To surface the challenges that come with social change, discovery and exploration. To seduce with strategies, solutions, and new ways of seeing and believing. To understand how global leaders sustain their work for lasting impact. To shift the view, the debate, the competition. To share the stories that connect us and strengthen communities. We're excited to kick off the forward movement.
