May BucharestJS Meetup

miercuri, 20 mai 2015

Our next BucharestJS Meetup is scheduled for 20th of May and you don't want to miss this one! Thanks to our sponsors we're going to give out 6 free invitations for Dev Talks Bucharest and 2 free tickets for <a>JSCamp</a>.

• 18:45 - Participant's arrival

• 19:15 - Intro notes

• 19:30 - JavaScript ALL THE THINGS! by Valentin Gosu, Software Engineer at Mozilla

How could we have an Internet of Things without the web? Firefox OS apps, JavaScript, WebAPIs, WebIDE and short hacky solutions to complicated problems.

• 20:15 - Choosing 4 lucky winners from the audience: 3 x Dev Talks Bucharest tickets and 1 x JSCamp ticket

• 20:20 - Building a cross-platform desktop app with GitHub's Electron by Cristian Pascu, Founder FlairBuilder.com

While the web is moving forward, with mobile apps being the hot thing, the desktop is still very much relevant today. And building cross platform desktop application has never been easier. FlairBuilder, a prototyping tool initially built in Adobe Flex and AIR, is now close to be released in version 4. I will share my experience in building a complex design application using only web technologies.

• 21:05 - Choosing 4 lucky winners from the audience: 3 x Dev Talks Bucharest tickets and 1 x JSCamp ticket

• 21:10 - Closing notes & beer

Feel free to invite 1-2 friends that might be interested in joining the meetup and make sure to find us on Facebook ( https://www.facebook.com/groups/BucharestJS ) and Twitter ( http://www.twitter.com/bucharestjs ).
