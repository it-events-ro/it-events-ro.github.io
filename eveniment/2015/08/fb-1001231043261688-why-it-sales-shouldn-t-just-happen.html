Why IT sales shouldn't just happen?

joi, 6 august 2015

Timisoara Startup Hub & Banat IT invites local entrepreneurs, marketing and sales specialists from IT companies IT, as well as other interested people to join an informal gathering where participants will have the opportunity to discuss about various aspects connected to sales, an activity that proves critical for better valorization of the creativity, know-how, effectiveness and work capacity of the IT companies’ staff. While Romanian IT companies are recognized mostly for their technical capabilities, a majority of tech entrepreneurs do not have the needed knowledge, time, motivation, courage to invest for solving the stingy problem of developing a significant in-house sales capacity. That’s why, in the case of most of IT services and even products providers in their initial or intermediate development stage, previous customer referals remain the main promotion channel, and sales are most often... just happen. The discussions within our event will be initiated through a short intervention by Maria Malina Constantinescu, Sales Manager at ENEA Software AB, a global supplier of Linux and real-time operating system solutions, including middleware, tools, databases, and world class services, head-quartered in Stockholm, Sweden. Malina will showcase several of her experiences faced as sales manager and business development director in Romania and Sweden, covering various markets in Europa and especially in the Nordic Countries. Afterwards, the audience will be encouraged to get involved in discussions focused on issues their companies are facing on topics like Direct Sales, Consultative Software Sales, Account Management, Sales and Marketing Planning, Negotiation, subjects that are best covered by Malina’s expertise. Provided that the event will raise real and immediate interest on these topics Malina will be ready to follow-up in the next period.
