International Coworking Day Celebration @ Cluj HUB

luni, 10 august 2015

On the 9th of August the world is celebrating the International Coworking Day. As active coworking promoters, we are bringing our contribution to this way of working, interacting and being by making it known to as many people as possible. This is because we believe in building stuff that matters in a pleasant environment, surrounded by creative people which have a positive impact on our everyday mood & productivity. So, on Monday 10th starting 5.30 PM we gather at Cluj HUB to share with you the secrets behind the concept of coworking, what it feels like to work at ClujHub and what we’ve experienced after 3 years of existence. We will close the info session by engaging in a special coworking quiz contest - the best answers will be consistently rewarded! We open our door to all those who share our values not just this time, but as often as we can. Therefore, all participants will receive a free 3 day voucher for working at Cluj HUB! Come join us for a great time in an unconventional workspace! Entrance is free of charge. You’re also welcome to bring your friends along:)
