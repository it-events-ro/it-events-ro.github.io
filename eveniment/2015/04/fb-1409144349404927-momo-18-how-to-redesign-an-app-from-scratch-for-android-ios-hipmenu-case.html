MoMo #18 - How to redesign an app from scratch for Android & iOS - hipMenu case study

luni, 27 aprilie 2015

Dear mobile developers, Ciprian Costa and Daniel Divricean, our speakers for next Monday's evening, will present us the new hipMenu application that got a redesign a few months ago. The app had to be updated to the latest technologies and thus the two software engineers decided to re-write it almost from scratch. They will present both the way in which they organized the redesign as well as the implementatation stage (for Android and iOS). You will benefit a lot from this discussion especially if you are involved in a medium or large mobile application project.
