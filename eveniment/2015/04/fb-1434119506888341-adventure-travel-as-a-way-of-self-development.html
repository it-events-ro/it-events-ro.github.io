Adventure & Travel as a way of self development

vineri, 24 aprilie 2015

Ce ? It’s nice and cozy in your comfort zone but there is something that is not letting you feel complete. Is that the wanderlust that tells you to take your rucksack and step on the road without knowing your next destination? That is not escapism, it’s a invite from the universe to re-create yourself. Self development books or standard education is not the only way of creating yourself. Traveling and adventuring are essential and powerful tools of growing and developing. Come to hear more about this and the personal stories of travelers who are what they are because of traveling! Come to hear more about ways and resources for free or cheap travel! Cu cine? The host of this event is Goce from Macedonia who is right now EVS volunteer in “Support for Youth Development” association in Cluj. He calls himself adventurer by profession and passion and loves experiencing the world in the true taste without filter. Besides Goce there will be other adventurers included in the event, like Bogdan who is a man of many passions and hobbies. Some of them are traveling by hitchhiking, exploring the world and writing about it. He is quite known by his project WorldWideHitchHiker. Cu ce? Bring your curiosity, enthusiasm and your open heart for the wild and adventurous way of life. Maybe a pan and a notebook wouldn’t be bad idea to record the resources we want to share with all of you. CUM? You have to register on our website: http://cluj.incubator107.com/workshops/1219 We suggest a donation for every workshop/presentation . It helps the project Incubator107 Cluj grow and also encourage the trainer. The donation suggested is 10 RON for every workshop. If you can't afford, no worries, you can help us with sharing our project or being a volunteer or even more, a trainer. We are waiting for you!
