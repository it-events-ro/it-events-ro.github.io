PMI Monthly Meeting Cluj Napoca gazduit de Evozon

joi, 9 aprilie 2015

PMI Romania Chapter are placerea sa va invite in Cluj Napoca la Monthly Meeting-ul gazduit de Evozon. Va asteptam la Hotel City Plaza, Strada Sindicatelor Nr. 9-13, Cluj-Napoca, pe 9 Aprilie 2015, incepand cu ora 18.15. Tema evenimentului "How to setup a project for fail/success in software outsourcing". Agenda evenimentului: 18:15 – 18:45 Inregistrare & Welcome Coffee 18:45 – 19:00 Prezentarea PMI Romania Chapter Cluj Napoca Branch Dorin-Viorel Popovici, PMP, ACP - 12 years in software industry, currently Senior Engineering Manager with 3Pillar Global. Areas of expertise: Project/ Product/ Program/ Portfolio/ Line Management. 19:00 – 19:10 Prezentarea companiei Evozon. Gabi Cretu, CEO and Co-Founder Evozon. 19:10 – 19:55 "How to setup a project for fail/success in software outsourcing” Claudiu Cretu, Head of .Net and Mobile departments @Evozon 14+ years in software industry, specialized in managing software development teams and departments. 19:55 – 20:05 Pauza 20:05 – 21:05 Discutie tip panel Claudiu Cretu, Head of .Net and Mobile departments @Evozon 14+ years in software industry, specialized in managing software development teams and departments Vlad Panut, PMP, 8 years in the software industry, currently Product Manager @ AROBS Transilvania Software, managing the leading fleet management and tracking solution in Romania (TrackGPS). Areas of expertise: Project/ Product Management, Business Analysis. Andreea Bejenaru, relatively new in the software industry - 2 years, PM with Evozon, Areas of expertise & interest: Project Management, UX Process, Business Analysis. Ovidiu Rus - Project Manager at Evozon. With a focus on Agile methodologies I enjoy making teams and projects succeed Dorin-Viorel Popovici, PMP, ACP - 12 years in software industry, currently Senior Engineering Manager with 3Pillar Global. Areas of expertise: Project/ Product/ Program/ Portfolio/ Line Management. 21:05 – ..... Networking In urma participarii la eveniment, 2 PDU-uri pot fi revendicate de persoanele certificate PMP. Un numar de 50 de locuri sunt disponibile pentru acest eveniment. Fiecare membru poate veni insotit de un invitat, non-membru PMI Romania. Este necesar doar sa transmiteti un mesaj pe adresa comunicare@pmi.ro continand numele si prenumele invitatului. In cazul in care v-ati inregistrat la eveniment si nu mai puteti participa, va rugam sa ne informati cat mai repede posibil despre acest lucru trimitandu-ne un e-mail pe adresa comunicare@pmi.ro Link pentru inscrieri: http://pmi.ro/articole/235/pmi-monthly-meeting-cluj-napoca-gazduit-de-evozon/
