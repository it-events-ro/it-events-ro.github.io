.NET Cluj Community Launch

joi, 30 aprilie 2015

Are you a .NET dev interested in the latest trends? Then join us at the .NET Cluj Community Launching Meetup on the very last day of April. .NET Cluj Community Meetup its all about connecting developers and facilitating the coming together of like minded people to share their knowlege as well as keeping you informed on best practices and future directions. The community is entirely focused on technical topics with participation of local specialists. The event will be opened to interested participants from Cluj and Transylvania. --> Agenda: 6:00 – Doors open 6:30 - Event starts with welcome from the organizers 6:40 – Key Note 1. Andrei Olar – MVVM ... is that all? About 10 years ago (2006) WPF or Avalon as it was known back then, was a relatively new concept that proposed an architecture based on a slight variation of the MVC design pattern. Let's see where that might shine. 7:10 – 10 minutes break 7:20 – Key Note 2. Radu Vunvulea – Azure Web Apps - a cloud services for web and mobile Discover how you can create, deploy and manage websites and web application in a simple and scalable way. Using Web Apps infrastructure we can deliver the same functionality both web and mobile apps with the same back-end code. Each steps can be 100% automated and integrate with business processes. Websites, Mobile Services and BizTalk Services are no Azure Web Apps 7:50 – Networking See you there !
