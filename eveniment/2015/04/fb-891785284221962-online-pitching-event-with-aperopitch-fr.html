Online Pitching Event with Aperopitch.fr

joi, 23 aprilie 2015

Ready for a pitching event in a fresh, innovative format? What awaits you is a live pitching session via Skype in collaboration with Co-Work in Grenoble, France. Three local startups will have 3 minute pitches in front of the french mentors and participants and 3 French startups will have pitches for our mentors here in Cluj. Each pitch will be followed by a 4 minute Q&A session, and of course, valuable international feedback that you can't otherwise get! From our side, we'll have Lucian Cosoi and Andrei Barabas as mentors for the event. The language of the event will be English and the participation is provided for free by Startup Transilvania. Developed in a partnership with Aperopitch.fr and Connectom (http://connectomebeta.appspot.com/), the event will be the first of its kind, organised by Startup Transilvania. The local pitching startups will be: • SenseWave • Proto • ChallengeXP The startups from Aperopitch.fr will be: • Visioglobe • Fieldcloud • HAP2U • Claire Robba (Holimeet) The aim of the event is to connect entrepreneurs, investors and other like-minded people with innovative ideas in order to ignite international dialogue and possible collaborations. _________________________________________________ Please confirm your presence here: http://www.meetup.com/CoFoundersLab-Cluj/events/221856978/ _________________________________________________ Looking forward to seeing you there!
