The Automation Test Engineer's Journey to Quality Assurance


In the last years the software testing has evolved very much, having an important role in software development and continuous delivery.

There are companies which understood the value brought by this department, they have invested to keep up with technology evolution and project development methodologies, and have created a framework for testers to constantly improve their skills.

The market demand for testers increased, but many of the candidates don’t know in what this job consists, which are the necessary abilities to fulfill their tasks and in what context they can apply their theoretical notions.

How the tester role will evolve depends on departments approach, but also depends on each tester approach to work much faster, much pleasant and much easier.
