Game Lab Cluj #3 - Monetization in Freemium Games

joi, 22 ianuarie 2015

Hey guys and girls! We’re back with fresh content and enthusiastic members, over 100 now! Razvan Calin is a game developer at Gameloft Romania for 4 years now. He is a designer who finished law school and a game designer with 3 major projects: The Dark Knight Rises, Total Conquest, Order&Chaos: Duels. He enjoyed the last panel we held on freemium/premium games and now he wants to tap further into the tips and tricks of building a successful freemium game. This one is going to be big! We'll start things off with some stats related to the global Gaming Industry, touching on subjects like platform, revenues, social, game genre and continuing with a breakdown of Key Performance Indicators -what they mean and how important each one is. We'll follow this idea with an 'Ads vs. IAP' discussion, based on a study regarding how to use coercive monetization to increase your revenue stream. We'll end it with a short-and-sweet presentation on new types of ads.
