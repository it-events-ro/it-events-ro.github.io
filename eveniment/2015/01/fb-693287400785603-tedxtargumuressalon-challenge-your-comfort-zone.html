TEDxTarguMuresSalon - Challenge your comfort zone

joi, 29 ianuarie 2015

We are more than eager to release the second event after the first one which was sealed with our participants excitement and appreciation! We welcome you to the second edition of TEDxTarguMuresSalon! We welcome those who are interested in challenging themselves and are eager to pass through their comfort zone. This Salon's topic will be entrepreneurship where we will present TED videos about entrepreneures who didn't settle and strove for evolving and passing from something usual to something new and ambitious. Our special guest and moderator is Augusta Romanta. Augusta has 16 years of management experience and over 12 years of experience in training programs as partner of CODECS. She graduated the MBA program (Master of Business Administration) of the Open University Business School UK and is a certified trainer of Zig Ziglar Romania. Augusta is over 10 years, an accredited tutor for the Open University Business School programs: People and Organizations, Finance and Marketing, Integrated Vision, Management Performance and Change. More about Augusta Romanta: https://www.linkedin.com/pub/augusta-romanta/13/856/575?domainCountryName=&csrfToken=ajax%3A6730389548203295309 Our event is called “TEDxTarguMuresSalon”, where the x stands for an independently organized TED event. At “TEDxTarguMuresSalon” we will mix recorded speeches from TEDx conferences, with socializing moments with the purpose of creating deep discussions among the participants and their close ones.
