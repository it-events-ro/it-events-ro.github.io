Alternative travel - How to travel without money?

joi, 29 ianuarie 2015

Ce? We invite you to a friendly meeting, where we'll try to find out how to travel without money. Bogdan will share his experience about this kind of travelling after he has traveled since March 2013 and has crossed 10 European countries. He wants to share with us that for travelling and discovering the "big world" you don't need so much money, or money at all. Cu cine? I’m Bogdan, 24 years old, and still counting. I am a man of many passions and hobbies. Lots of them are still out there waiting for me to discover them. Some of them are traveling by hitchhiking, exploring the world and writing about it. I did quite a lot of stuff during my time here on Earth and one of the biggest one is this project, WorldWideHitchHiker. I started it in March 2013 and I don’t plan on stopping anytime soon. The idea is simple: I hitchhike around the world and volunteer in exchange for food and accommodation. Cu ce? Maybe you'll need a notebook and a pen to write down some tips and tricks. Cand?
