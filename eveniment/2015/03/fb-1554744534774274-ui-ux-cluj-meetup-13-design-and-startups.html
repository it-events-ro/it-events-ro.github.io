UI/UX Cluj Meetup #13 - Design and startups

marți, 10 martie 2015

Hi all! This week we'll enjoy the presentations of the following speakers: • Adonis Raul Raduca - As a Startup, am I asking the right UX questions? 1. Am I solving the right problem?Is it a real problem?Do I really understand the problem?Have I framed the problem in a correct manner? 2. Am I designing the product experience beyond the surface level?How well have I understood my users' Mental Model?Am I exposing the right Conceptual Model?How is my Information and Interaction architected?Have I got a Behaviour Design layer? 3. Is my product smart enough?Am I considering Automation?What about the Algorithms Design?Have I employed an AI? 4. Am I really in the experience business?Am I considering the entire experience with my company?Is the UX my key value proposition?Is it part of my Brand strategy? • Roxana Oroian - How to design for a Startup _____________________________________ Please RSVP here: http://www.meetup.com/UXUICluj/events/220430259/. _____________________________________ In Cluj-Napoca there is a big potential for growth in the software sector in more strategic (and better paid) areas such as User Experience and User Interaction Design. From this perspective, we are forwarding you this invitation of joining us and collaborating within the UI/UX community. This invitation is addressed to individuals and companies as well. Due to the fact that each company has one or more UI/UX specialists, we invite you to connect and thus create a growth environment for the professionals in this area. In this way we are contributing to the overall development and augmentation of specialised talent in the Cluj-Napoca area. We can't wait to meet you and learn from one another! CALL FOR SPEAKERS FOR FUTURE MEETUPS We are looking for speakers who have something unique and innovative they would like to share with the rest of the UI/UX Cluj Community at one of our next meetups. We are interested in just about everything to do with User Experience or User Interaction, from usability best practices to future trends, or even just a project in which you take pride. Additionally we are looking for people who can lead a workshop on an UX/ UI related subject. Is this you? If so, please contact us. Thank you!
