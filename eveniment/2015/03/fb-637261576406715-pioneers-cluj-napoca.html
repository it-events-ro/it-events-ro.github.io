Pioneers Cluj-Napoca

joi, 19 martie 2015

Global Pioneers is the place where entrepreneurs find meaningful connections that lead to partnerships in order to grow projects in a faster and more sustainable way. People helping people to support business growth. Happening twice a year in your region, Global Pioneers embraces the local entrepreneurial ecosystem and transforms it into a global community by connecting all the regional ecosystems. Pioneers Cluj Napoca is an evening full of tech talks and networking with entrepreneurs, coders, builders, and other troublemakers :). In the first part of the evening, the mentors will form a panel to discuss the generation of angel investments in the Transilvania Innovative Ecosystem. In the second part of the event, we will have a pitching contest. While the jury deliberates, you will have the opportunity to network with fellow pioneers.
