Open Connect Cluj #18 sezonul 3

joi, 5 martie 2015

Save the date: Thursday, 5th March, when a new edition of Open Connect Cluj is going to happen. Do you wonder what Open Connect is? Open Connect is a meeting of networking, pitching and mentoring for entrepreneurs, media people, programmers, designers but also other people who are curious about these areas. This edition, given that it happens in the begining of March, it will be dedicated to women only pitchers and mentor. Following the model of the Open Connect meetings, initiated in Bucharest, the schedule for our meeting will be the next one: (the only difference will be that the pitches and mentoring session will be different each time) 09:00 - 09:15 - Arrival 09:15 - 09:25 - Ice-breaking (presentation of each person: who he/she is, what he/she is doing, what he/she is offering, what he/she needs) 09:25 - 09:45 – Pitching session 09:45 - 10:15 – Mentoring session 10:15 - 10:45 – Discussions, coffee and networking 10:45 - 16:00 – You can stay in the HUB to work or socialize, based upon available places ---- The pitchers and the mentor for this edition are: - Letitia Nadasan, Founder @ Uber Project - Diana Tcaciuc, Founder @ Nanette - Agentie de bone - Ioana Mihet, antrenor dansul mirilor si wedding planner @ Bridal Sense - Silvia Nasaudean, Designer & PR Assistant @ Edge Academy Mentor: Alexandra Craciun, Founder @ Exquisite _________________________________________________ Please take a moment to RSVP on Meetup here: http://www.meetup.com/CoFoundersLab-Cluj/events/220621656/]. _________________________________________________ We are looking forward to seeing you! The event is free of charge, but for refreshments and drinks corresponding to a nice morning, we count on a donation of 10 ron. If you have a project, a start-up or an event that you would like to present, please contact us. Also, for proposal of subjects and people for mentoring, send us an e-mail at contact@clujhub.ro. The Open Connect Cluj event is organised montly by ClujHUB on the first Thursday of the month.
