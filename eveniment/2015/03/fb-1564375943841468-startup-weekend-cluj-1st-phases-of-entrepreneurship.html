Startup Weekend Cluj - 1st Phases of Entrepreneurship

joi, 26 martie 2015

Startup Weekend Cluj 2015 is fast approaching and we are going to have a kickoff meetup to set the event off on the right foot. We would like to invite all of you to our first meetup, no matter if you are a student, developer, designer or business guy We'll have two special guests who will discuss about entrepreneurial experience and will share some useful tips for startups: Oana Calugar and Robbert Visser. 6:00 - 6:20 Joining, networking 6:20 - 6:40 Robbert Visser an urban nomad & serial entrepreneur with a passion for startups & founder of Cluj Cowork will share insights about Cluj Ecosystem 6:40 - 7:00 Oana Calugar with a vast experience as marketer, communicator, head of customer support, mentor, adviser & entrepreneur will talk about how to get startup team flow. “Entrepreneurship is about the nonstop navigation of uncertainty and being "in flow" is a critical aspect of success. Flow states allow an entrepreneur to stay open and alert to possibilities, which could exist in any partnership, product insight, or customer interaction. Inspired by my experience at Startup Weekend events as a participant and from Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi, "Flow", I would love to share some ideas with the SW followers” 7:00 - 7:15 SW Cluj team will spread the news about the 4th edition And most important we will NETWORK. Food, drinks and entertainment is on us. See you there!
