Banat IT Early Autumn Barbeque

miercuri, 23 septembrie 2015

An informal networking event for members of the Timisoara tech community. After the 2014 Summer edition this is the second event thought to spark belonging feelings to the Banat IT community. A lot of live music with @Posta Mare, A.L.L. Friends Band, and Virgula, plenty of fun and several surprise games are awaiting to all the participants. The venue chefs will grill and cook specially designed food: the Banat IT burgers and the Banat IT gulas. The event participation is free of charge but you are supposed to pay for your own food and drinks orders. :) This year there will be a charitable component to our event. We are organizing a raffle and an auction in order to raise money for our fellow colleague, Florina Gogiuman, who struggles with a long and costly recovery process after a less successful surgical procedure. The list of raffle prizes includes: - a Coding Serbia Conference participation ticket* (8-9 Oct, Novi Sad, 150 EUR value) - a set of loudspeakers offered by DAB IT - a life coaching package with Anca Petre - 5 massage sessions by Florin Chindea Participants will be able to bid in the auction for: - an exquisite Tokaj wine bottle and a hand made cork pinboard (offered by Salonul ROVINHUD and Zoli Szoverdfy Szep) - 2 Agile/Scrum consultancy hours offered by Flavius Stef & Mozaic Works - several art works by young Timisoara artists, We are looking forward to meet you at La Capite to create and to enjoy the Banat IT vibe! Thanks to ARIES-TM for the help in organizing the event! * you will only be eligible to win the Coding Serbia Conference ticket if you are registering using the 123contactform.
