Let's watch together live the Apple Keynote Address

miercuri, 9 septembrie 2015

Apple’s September 9th keynote address is set to be one of the company’s largest events in history, as is reflected by the expansive size of its San Francisco venue.

What will be announced?
Besides a pair of new iPhones, the substantially revamped Apple TV set top box, and new bands for the Apple Watch, Apple is currently planning to debut a pair of new iPads at next week’s event: the long-rumored iPad Pro, and a refreshed version of the iPad mini, according to trusted sources.

The Bill Graham auditorium, where the keynote will take place, is a massive 7000-seat building, and sources suggest that it was deliberately chosen to reflect the event’s scope.

We are giving you the possibility to watch it live with us and be the first to know what Apple has in store for us.
