eWine HackTM 2015

vineri, 13 noiembrie 2015

Somehow ad-hoc, but nevertheless attractive enough, at the confluence of a river of fine wines and a stream of data, a novel and quite unique kind of hackathon emerges. From the 13th to the 15th of November, Banat IT and RoVinHud, join forces (and probably clink glasses) in order to bring together teams of wine loving, tech savvy hackers, and provide them with some technology and mentorship if needed, in order for them to tackle some of the issues facing the wine makers and quince the thirst for technology of the wine artists and craftmen. The hackathon will take place as a satellite event of Salonul RoVinHud, one of the highest quality wine fairs in Eastern Europe, in which over 25 of important wine producers in Romania and Hungary will present their products. The RoVinHud Salon aims at promoting fine wines and at educating for responsible consumption and social responsibility. Equally, RoVinHud is passionate about providing a high quality context to both the exhibitors and the visitors. And because wine brings people together and melts down barriers, the entire profit of this year’s edition will be directed to support the young people with disabilities from the "Ceva de Spus" ("Something to Say") Association, as they continue their work towards a society that is more open, accessible and inclusive for people with mental or physical disabilities. The program of the event will include: Friday, 6 PM - 8 PM - meeting the wine-makers and brainstorming for idea generation. Friday, 8 PM - till the last droplet of energy, just hacking Saturday, 11 AM - till the last droplet of energy, pure hacking Saturday, 6 PM - reality check with the wine-makers Sunday, 11 AM - 5 PM - hacking finalizing Sunday, 6 PM - projects demo and awards If you are interested to attend, please apply by fillig in the form you may find following the "Ticket URL" above, no later than Friday, November 13th, 13.00. As this is a pilot event where we'll only have a limited seating for 20 participants, we will have to select out of all the registrants the best mixture of design, programming, business/marketing specialists. We'll let all the applicants know about the positive/negative admission decision on Friday, November 13th, 14.00.
