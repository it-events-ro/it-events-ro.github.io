How to make a Hit game!

marți, 17 noiembrie 2015

100% Romanian Video Games!


Dan Dimitrescu , co-founder of the studio Killhouse Games is our special guests for this edition of GameDEVIs.

Dan, lead designer and producer, will share with us some interesting and useful stories about developing his hit title Door Kickers, one of the most successful video game created by a Romanian indie studio.

For the second part of the meeting , we want to invite indie game developers from Iasi to present their games and talk about their development process.

Agenda

18.30 – 19.00: Dan Dimitrescu, Killhouse games

19.00 – 19.45: Be a GameDev in Iasi! Local game developers talk about their projects

20.00+ : Networking!*

* we can neither confirm nor deny that this may or may not entail heavy, self-sponsored (but hopefully responsible) drinking in a pub. It may be wise to negotiate hangover leave with your employer before attending.

GameDEV Iasi Meetup is organized by RGDA and Mobility Games. The attendance is free of charge, if you book your seat on Meetup.com .

See you there!
