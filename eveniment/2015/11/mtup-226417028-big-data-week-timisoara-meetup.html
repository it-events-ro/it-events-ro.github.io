Big Data week: Timisoara Meetup

joi, 26 noiembrie 2015

Hello Big Data lovers!

This year, Timisoara will be part of Big Data Week, organising a meetup in order to talk about big data, nosql, algorithms and many more.

The speakers who will entertain the audience are:

1. Andrei Lihu - Hacking the DNA: An Insight into the Discovery of Biological Patterns

2. Mihai Udrescu - From Computer Engineering and Network Science to Big Data and Medical Research

3. Gabriel Iuhasz - Monitoring in Big Data Frameworks

4. Silviu Vert - Surviving as a tourist in Timisoara - Open Linked Data to the rescue!

5. Cosmin Ursachi - Working with Titan

Food and drinks will be provided in order to keep the energy level up!

Interested? Then we are looking forward to having you @bdw15 Timisoara!
