Heartbeat. Music for Capoeira

sâmbătă, 28 noiembrie 2015

Heartbeat. Music for Capoeira Ce ? 7 November: We make ourselves familiar with the instruments, talk a little bit about the origins of the instruments and we are going to play one of the Berimbau rhythms ( Sao Bento Grande de Angola) and one Atabaque rhythm (Ijexa). We also get to know the other instruments and play them together. 14 November: Short explanation about the energy of Capoeira music, how can we feel that energy and how to work with the rhythm (Sao Bento Grand de Angola). We are going to learn about the Bateria - the place where the instruments are in a Roda and also about how many rhythms are in Capoeira (different style and different “bateria”). 28 November: We are going work on some lyrics in Portuguese, make a Roda simulation with the rhythms learned in the previous days. This is the part where we’ll have a lot of fun and good energy. Cu cine? My name is Luis Miguel, Pirata in Capoeira, I’m a Graduado in Capoeira and I started to practice Capoeira in 2008, in Portugal. I am currently teaching Capoeira within the Alemar Capoeira group in Bucharest, Tuesdays and Fridays at Popa Nan 82. More info here. Când?
