Startup Grind Business Talk & Networking with Bogdan Herea, CEO PITECH+PLUS

joi, 19 noiembrie 2015

FOUNDER of PITECH+PLUS group of businesses, Bogdan Herea was born in Bucharest, Romania. His results in school brought him a scholarship at INSA LYON (L’Institut National des Sciences Appliquées de Lyon) where he studied Computer Engineering. His entrepreneurial projects started at the end of 2005, when he founded his own IT services company in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, after 13 years of living and working in France. In 2005 he founded PITECH+PLUS. After 10 years, the company acquired clients in Europe, US, Middle East, Africa and Asia. In 2014, Bogdan expanded his entrepreneurial efforts again by changing the group operational strategy, from services to services and products. As a founding member of CLUJ IT Cluster, PITECH+PLUS is developing an innovative solution for managing and sharing the parking spots in the city. 2012 // Bogdan Herea was awarded by GADIF with the distinction “Entrepreneur Francophone de l’Annee” and also became President of the Francophone Business Club in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. 2013 // Umanlife.com was awarded by Startup Academy in France and by the Community PharmaSuccess with the distinction “Best Pharma Start-up”. Bogdan Herea is co-founder. 2014 // ACADEMY+PLUS was awarded in 2014 by the French Chamber of Commerce and Industry in Romania with the Prize for Innovation. Join us for an awesome talk with a local successful entrepreneur! Startup Grind & Startup Transilvania Team.
