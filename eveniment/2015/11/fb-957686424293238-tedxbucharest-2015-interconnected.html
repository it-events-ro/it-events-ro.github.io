TEDxBucharest 2015 | Interconnected

sâmbătă, 7 noiembrie 2015

For over 7 years TEDxBucharest has been a focal point of ideas, actions and people changing our world. We have created bridges between our community and brilliant ideas, we have focused attention on burning issues and have built relationships. We look at the people who touch and impact communities, at ideas which unexpectedly change an entire industry. We promote and provoke ideas which interconnect fields of activity, industries and perceptions. This year, we build upon the power of an Interconnected world.
