Declare Independence and Finance your Early Stage Startup

marți, 17 februarie 2015

Timisoara Startup Hub and Vitas IFN Romania are joining forces organizing an event dedicated to young Timisoara Area entrepreneurs and entrepreneur-wannabes focusing on finance sources for startups in very early stages of their evolution. During the 2 hours event, participants will be exposed to actionable information on financing a startup through accessing business angel or venture capital investment, through enrolling in EU financing programs with a grant component or through accessing loans from non-bank financial institutions. Additionally, during the event, the Vitas IFN Romania will present their business plan competition developed within the Declaratia de Independenta/ Independence Declaration project, endowed with a 20.000 RON prize, opened to startups younger than 6 months or to teams just aiming to register a new company. All participants offering feedback during the event will participate in a raffle with a surprise prize (stay connected, we’ll probably disclose the secret prior to the event). The program of the evening will include: 18.30 - Participants’ registration 19.00 - Event opening Romania Startup - Grants for hundreds of Romania startups during 2015 - Radu Ticiu Romanian and European Landscape of Business Angels & Business Accelerators - Adrian Pica Vitas Romania - financing institution supporting early stage startups - Adrian Garlonta 19.45 Break & Networking 20.15 Declaratia de independenta Business Plan Competition - Adrian Garlonta Q&A 20.45 Networking 21.00 Raffle winner announcement 21.15 Conclusion of the event The participation to the event is free, but in order to secure your place you have to register here: http://bit.ly/vitasreg.
