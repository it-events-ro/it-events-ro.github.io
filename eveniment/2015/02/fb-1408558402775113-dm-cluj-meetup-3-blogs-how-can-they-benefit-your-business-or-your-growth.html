DM Cluj Meetup #3 - Blogs: How can they benefit your business or your growth?

marți, 3 februarie 2015

Dear Digital Marketers, Now that a new year has started, we plan on conducting these meetings on a monthly basis, with the same recurrence: the meetups will be held on the first Tuesday of each month. Having this in mind, please schedule/book the dates in your calendar. :) This Tuesday we will have a handful of digital marketers who will share their knowledge with us. This meeting's topics are focused on blogs and the art of blogging: why, who, how, for what purpose. What are blogs used for? Are they just a freelancer's endeavor? Who would best benefit from having a blog? A SME, a multinational, is it something more related to the field of activity? Is it best suited for programmers, for copyrighters? Whould a blog be useful for artist or for sportsmen? Would a blog be useful for an awareness campaign, or a fund raising campaign? What do you think? Well, if you're curious about all these things and a lot more other, you're especially invited. Also, please leave us in the comments below, requests for special topics that you would like to approach and also mention if your question is intended for any of the speakers specifically by mentioning their name. Shortly, the agenda is as follows: • Florin Rosoga - Convert your visitors to buyers Florin is a blog coach, mentor and a writer. Owner of the most popular personal development website in Romania florinrosoga.ro, with over 150.000 unique visitors/ month. Founder and co-founder of projects like: profiblogger.ro, sertarulcujucarii.ro, florinrosoga.com, wealthacademy.ro, blog2money.ro. • Chinezu - Cristian Birta China (online presence from Bucharest/ live meeting) Cristian is a well known Romanian blogger and entrepreneur, he lives in Bucharest and is part of a few interesting projects like: Kooperativa, Blogal Initiative and Chinezu TV. • Dan Ciulea and Gabriel Aldea Dan Ciulea - blogger, entrepreneur and builder. He is Cristian Gog's manager, co-founder of Fantasmagia and stakeholder at Class Fiscal Consulting. Among other projects he develop you cand find Nepoti de Craciun, a successful social campaign from Cluj. Gabriel Aldea - blogger, entrepreneur and founder of Cluj Life network, which includes clujlife.com, partytude.ro, nappoca.ro and festivaldecluj.ro. Passionate about traveling, photography, people and events, loves staying connected to the local environment. He has a rich activity as a PR, SM and content developer. Associated with names such as Transilvania Media Group, TIFF, How to Web Cluj.
