PyTim #7 - Introducing RoPython Timisoara

miercuri, 25 februarie 2015

Welcome to PyTim #7. This meetup marks our affiliation to the RoPython national initiative which has similar events in Cluj, Iasi and Bucharest (more on this and a potential RoPython national conference at the meetup). We will have 3 talks covering Python, open source and cloud automation. Here is the Agenda: 18:30 - 19:10 Adelina Tuvenie (Cloudbase Solutions) - Automation as a Service with StackStorm 19:00 - 19:10 Coffee Break 19:10 - 19:40 Radu Ciorba (3Pillar Global Romania) - Growing Pains and Performance Woes with Learning Media 19:40 - 19:50 Coffee Break 19:50 - 20:20 Vlad Temian (PressLabs) - GitFS The new venue is Timisoara Startup Hub and the date is February 25! See you there. #Python #opensource #cloud #automation #meetup #Timisoara #PyTim #RoPython
