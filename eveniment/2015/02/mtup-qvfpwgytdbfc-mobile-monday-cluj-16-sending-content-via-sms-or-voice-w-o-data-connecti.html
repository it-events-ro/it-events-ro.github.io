Mobile Monday Cluj #16 - Sending content via SMS or Voice, w/o data connection

luni, 23 februarie 2015

We are PANGEA , a San Francisco-based start-up, founded by a Romanian IT experts. At PANGEA, we develop technology that powers smartphone applications via SMS/Voice . No DATA or WI-FI access needed. Our solution allows web content to be delivered through SMS/MMS & data over voice, to (and from) mobile phone users lacking access to an IP connection. We connect people outside data coverage or unable to afford a data plan.  We are happy to be here at MoMo and share with you our ideas on developing a successful start-up and highlight the technical challenges we faced along the road to developing our innovative technology.


Topics covered:

- SMS/MMS/VOICE

- Http Proxy

- Android limitations

- Digital and analog modulation

- Tools and frameworks.


About the speaker:

Sebastian Presecan is the CTO of Pangea. He has been involved in developing/managing mobile applications for more than 6 years, before starting this project. Sebastian has also coordinated the team that developed the Trip Journal application, one of the winners of the Android Development Challenge organized by Google in 2009.
