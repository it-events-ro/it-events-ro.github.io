Startup Lounge #9: Growth Hacking – demythisation, or what it really is about?

joi, 19 februarie 2015

This edition will be a must for all startups, because the theme we’ll aproach on is growth hacking, what it is, what it does, how it does it, what should startups expect from it, why is it so important for startups to do it right and what could startups benefit from it if they do it the right way. We’ll talk about why it isn’t just a matter of „We take that, we do this, and BAM users will flood us…” or „It’s easy, look at how those guys at Dropbox did it”. We’ll look into what the implications are for applying certain growth hacking techniques, examples of how others applied them and what kind of results did they obtain during a period of time.
