Creating 100% Romanian successful games

joi, 26 februarie 2015

The story of a Romanian studio and their successful games made 100% in Romania.

Marius Badita and Ionut Georgescu , co-founders of the Studio 88mm Games are our special guests for this 3rd edition of GameDEVIs.

Marius, lead programmer and Ionut, game designer and 3D artist, will share with us some interesting and useful stories about developing their projects for mobile and PC, from scratch to success.

A very brief history of ...

December, 2013. 2 passionate game developers started their journey and founded 88mm Games.

April, 2014. They published their first game Frontline: Road to Moscow , a turn-based strategy placed in the Second World War, on iOS.

Mai, 2014. Frontline: Road to Moscow on Steam

October, 2014. Marks the release of a second game on this series, Frontline: The Longest Day on iOS, PC & Android.

Present. 9 passionate game developers working on 3 new projects.
