Speed Dating!

miercuri, 17 iunie 2015

We are tired of job announcements, CVs, interviews, tests, tricky questions, boring answers, shirts and ties, staying in a circle and saying our names. Job interviews are so last century. Let’s bring music, drinks, friends, cookies and games instead. Who’s invited? Virtually, everybody can join our party. We won’t keep you out if you are cool and creative but our focus is to find our new Marketing / Communication / PR girl. How does it work? We won’t tell you everything now, let’s keep some mystery around this. But we will give you some clues. You will get to speed-date with each High Contrast rock star and you will have to impress us all. We will take you through marketing, PR, design, philosophy, art, technology and you will make us want you. You will have to stand out and make yourself noticed. What do you have to do? - join our party Wednesday at 6 p.m. (it can last till midnight) - bring something like your personalized CV / portfolio / sketchbook - bring your lucky pen and your sense of humour. Be there: the 17th of June, Horia 6, Brașov, România.
