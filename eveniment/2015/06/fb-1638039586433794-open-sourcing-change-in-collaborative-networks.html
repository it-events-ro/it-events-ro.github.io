Open Sourcing Change in Collaborative Networks

marți, 30 iunie 2015

Let’s talk about the impact of networks. In the age of communities, networks are vital infrastructures. As we are beginning to understand their potential, learning to build and harness connectivity is a powerful skill. So how does collaboration in networks actually work? What are main dynamics of collaboration, how are resources distributed across the organizations, and which governance models work? When do networks fail? Finally, how can making knowledge open be conducive to more effective change? We will explore these questions in a two ­hour event starting with four national and global communities, each with a distinct model of networked collaboration.
