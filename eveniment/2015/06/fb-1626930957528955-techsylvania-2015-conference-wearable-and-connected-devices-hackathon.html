Techsylvania 2015: Conference & Wearable and connected devices hackathon

sâmbătă, 6 iunie 2015

The Hackathon (June 6th and 7th) will be an event focused on wearable and connected technologies, where teams of talented people come together to hack a solution on some of the most interesting devices out there. More information about tickets, the cool devices that will be available (Google Glass, Leap Motion, Pebble, etc.) and the prizes can be found here: http://www.techsylvania.co/hackathon/ The top 3 teams will have free passes to the conference and also the chance to demo their projects in front of the entire audience at the Techsylvania conference. The Conference (June 8th and 9th) will be the main event consisting of presentations and discussions with key influencers and exceptional international people from around the world. The top 3 teams that come out of the Hackathon will also present on stage what they have accomplished. More information about the confirmed speakers can be found here: http://www.techsylvania.co/speakers/ This is your chance to get that extra edge and connect with people that can really make a difference!
