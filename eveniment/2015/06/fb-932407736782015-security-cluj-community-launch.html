Security Cluj Community Launch

marți, 23 iunie 2015

Security Cluj Community inaugural event will be marked by a community meetup focused on security practices with participation of local specialists. 6:15 - Doors open 6:30 – Event starts with welcome from the organizers 6:40 – Key Note Presentation 1. Lucian Suta - What is XSS? Let’s talk about on of the most common vulnerabilities! Let’s recap what is Cross Site Scripting (XSS), what are the damages it can do and how do we protect against it. Also what specific prevention mechanisms we have in some more common web application frameworks. 7:10 – 10 minutes break 7:20 – Key Note Presentation 2. Cristian Serban - Have you been hacked or would you even know that? Let’s share some experiences of previous incidents, how we found out, how they were exploited, and what we did to fix them. Also what mechanisms we have in place to quickly detect that an attack is taking place. 7:50 - Networking About the speakers: Lucian Suta is a Development Security Advisor in the Security group at Betfair where he advises teams on how to build secure software. In previous roles, he has worked for wide range of software companies in the United States, from small start-ups such as Enosys Software to large Fortune 500 such as Qualcomm and NCR, in different fields such as enterprise information integration, semi-structured databases, telecommunications. In Romania, he co-founded a start-up where he was responsible for building the software solution: translating business requirements to technical ones, software architecture, managing a team of developers, etc. It was during this time that he developed an interest in software security which materialised into the current role at Betfair. Lucian studied Computer Science at University of California San Diego where he received a Bachelor of Science and a Master of Science degree. Cristian Serban works at Betfair as a Security Architect dedicated for the Gaming area. He started his career 13 years ago as a developer and for the last 8 years he has focused on the security of software development, helping teams build better products. Cristian has numerous security certifications (including OSCP, OSWP, CEH, ISO 27001 Internal Auditor) and sometimes provides trainings and gives talks at public conferences. Starting 2014, Cristian and Lucian are SABSA chartered architects (SCF), the first ones certified in Romania by the SABSA Institute. ----------------------------------------------------------------- This event is free of charge but registration on Meetup is mandatory!
