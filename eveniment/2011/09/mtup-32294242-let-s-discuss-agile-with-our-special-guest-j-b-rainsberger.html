Let's discuss agile with our special guest: J.B. Rainsberger

marți, 13 septembrie 2011

Summer is almost gone, autumn is knocking at the door, so that means only one thing: The holidays are over and it's time to get to work. In the last years the adoption of agile practices steadily increased in Timisoara and everyone has had their share of success and roadblocks. Come share them with us in another meetup of the local Agile community.

To help us in our agile journeys, we have enlisted the help of a veteran agile practitioner, coach and trainer: J.B. Rainsberger , who has been kind enough to participate via Skype in our next meetup, and share with us a few tips and tricks.

Receiver of the first Gordon Pask award , author of a book on JUnit ( JUnit recipes ) and regular guest at agile conferences, J.B. spends his time coaching teams and training on various topics related to agile software development and project management, lean, kanban, effective team and organization management or product design. To find more about him, visit his personal blog , technical blog or follow his twitter account. You can also watch a podcast with him on productivity and happiness , or enjoy his stance on writing integration tests .

So on Tuesday, Sep. 13, at 19:00 , we'll meet at our usual location (the Timisoara Business Incubator) and discuss Scrum, XP, self-organization, TDD, test automation and more . Be sure to confirm your participation, as seating is limited.

If you have some special topics/questions that would like to be addressed, feel free to post them in this meetup's thread.

Participation is free of charge.
