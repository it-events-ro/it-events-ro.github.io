Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup #12

luni, 12 decembrie 2011

The 12th monthly event of Timisoara Mobile Development Group and the last one of 2011 will have a varied program, featuring new mobile applications developed by local teams, presentation on mobile business and development methodologies, and a short report on the presence of Outclan to international events.

Program

1. Raul Andrisan & Stefan Filip, NextRoot (Timisoara) - Song Mix: Music Now, After Pics Earlier

2. Alexandru Bolboaca-Diaconu, AgileWorks (Bucuresti) - Agile Mobile or Mobile Agile?

3. Cosmin Tiru, Microsoft Student Partner Program (Timisoara) - Excite Competition & Resulting Windows Mobile Apps

4. Felix Gravila and Outclan (Timisoara) - Outclan in Chisinau and at How to Web, by TiMo Crowd-Funding

In more detail, the 4 (up 20 minutes each presentations) will be as follow:

1. Raul Andrisan & Stefan Filip, NextRoot (Timisoara) - Song Mix: Music Now, After Pics Earlier

Few months after the release of Instamap, their successful Instagram client, Raul and Stefan stroke again with an application based on  usage of audio information (song samples from the iTunes) within a music trivia game that can be played in several modes on iPhone, iPad or iPod devices.

Raul and Stefan will refer to the experience of trying to capitalize on a previous notoriety when launching a new app and some interesting cross selling effects for their application.

2. Alexandru Bolboaca-Diaconu, AgileWorks (Bucuresti) - Agile Mobile or Mobile Agile?

http://photos1.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/3/6/a/8/event_76453992.jpegRepresenting AgileWorks, the structured core of the Romanian Agile movement and community, Alex is a well known programming expert, consultant and team coach, both abroad and nationally, including here in Timisoara. With his presentation, Alex will bring in participants' discussion the ways using Agile approaches could power up the typical small teams involved in mobile development, resulting in constantly reaching the "Small Teams, Big Results" level.

3. Cosmin Tiru, Microsoft Student Partner Program (Timisoara) - Excite Competition & Resulting Windows Mobile Apps

Cosmin, quite recently (August 3rd) registered as 100th TiMo group member will present the Windows Phone 7 component of the Excite 2011 project, currently in progress, that brought about 40 students to a series of theoretical and hands-on trainings followed by an app development competition that resulted in the releasing of about 6 application (based of the availability of an extended set of publishing vouchers).

4. Felix Gravila, Outclan (Timisoara) - Outclan in Chisinau and at How to Web, by TiMo Crowd-Funding

Following the good impression generated with their presentation within October's TiMo # 10, the high-school students from the  Outclan received an impressive financial support from the group's members to travel to and attend the Advancing Innovation in Central and Eastern Europe and Central Asia event in Chisinau, Moldova (6-8 November) and How To Web Conference (9-10 November) in Bucharest. The spectacular and very well perceived internationally crowd-funding exercise and some of the benefits obtained by the Outclan team will be presented.
