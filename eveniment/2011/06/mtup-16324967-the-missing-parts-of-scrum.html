The missing parts of Scrum

joi, 9 iunie 2011

" Scrum is deliberately incomplete " is a quote from an interesting article by Uncle Bob with contribution from Mike Cohn available at http://www.scrumalliance.org/articles/300

The discussion theme proposed for this meeting is:

Did you know that Scrum is incomplete?

If you did , what did you do to fill in the missing parts?

If you didn't , what do you think you need to fill in the missing parts?

Subtopics and possible discussion points:

Test Driven Development (TDD) Continuous Integration / Deployment
Pair Programming Collective Ownership Refactoring / Clean Code Other topics... maybe even non-engineering, bring your own

Remember to bring:

yourself - ;) your input / insights - we want to hear about your ideas, teams, success stories your questions / problems - maybe you'll find some answers
