RDF triple stores tutorial

miercuri, 23 februarie 2011

For the next meetup Dia will prepare a short tutorial on the use of RDF triple stores in real world applications. She will concentrate on AllegroGraph ( http://www.franz.com/agraph/allegrograph/ ).

Those who are familiar with other triple store technologies are welcomed to prepare additional tutorials/presentations. It will be very interesting to see the similarities and the differences between knowledge representation, reasoning and query using several RDF technologies.

Link: http://www.meetup.com/Cluj-Semantic-WEB/events/16091083/
