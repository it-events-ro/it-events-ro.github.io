Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 21 februarie 2011

Final event TiMo#2 configuration


The event gets more in significance for the participants as it will connect the local community to one extremely recent major industry event (this week's Mobile World Congress in Barcelona, presented by mobilissimo.ro's special envoy, Zsolt Tamassy), by attracting the participation of Robert Muresan (@exorobert, from Cluj), one of the most experienced Romanian mobile developers, by the active role that will be played by experts from small, medium and very big companies (Cristian Sitov, Arthur Csertus and Mircea Romanitan) and also by the presence of a very young and promissing developer, Vlad Temian that is still studying in Timisoara "Grigore Moisil" High School.

The program of the 2nd edition of the #TiMo Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup got its final details:

Mobilissimo.ro's Zsolt Tamassy will present his new views on the mobile field, sharing his MWC experiences!
Robert Muresan @exorobert will talk about his Personal 10 years history in mobile development Cristian Sitov's will showcase JS frameworks for web interfaces on touch display smartphones Vlad Temian will present his TroleyRail application Timisoara Alcatel-Lucent Android development team members will bring insights on their Home Media Sharing apps Arthur Csertus will explain how ICMed Mobile (by Syonic) will change the way e-health data could be instantly accessed.

Our daily registered participants count shows that at least 45+ will join us next Monday!

Follow us on twitter ( @raduticiu and @softwareincub or the #TiMobile hashtag) for news and further details on the event.
