Timisoara How To Web Community Event

miercuri, 12 octombrie 2011

Timişoara Software Business Incubator proudly continues its participation in the evolution of the How To Web project. The remarcable Bucharest 2010 How To Web conference atmosphere will be recreated in this November 9th-10th in an augmented way, including 24 presentations & panels, 30+ international speakers and 12 workshops to attend. All this for an expected crowd of about 750 participants, creators of the brave, web-based, world we are living in 24/7. Together with Bogdan Iordache, HTW co-founder, Timișoara Software Business Incubator invites members of the local and regional startup and web business community to join Tuesday, October 11th, starting at 7:00 PM in the lobby area of the C Building of City Business Centre, a biz & tech event designed to introduce us in the HTW atmosphere by discussing themes connected to startup business creation, grow-up and failure, and major current technological challenges and trends. The program will feature presentations delivered by: Florin Cornianu & Tudor Bastea of 123contactform (Timisoara) Mihai Rotaru of Squeeqly (Bucharest) Ciprian Borodescu of Webcrumbz (Bucharest) Bogdan Iordache of HTW (Bucharest). Following what we are expecting to be intense discussion we will gladly invite you to continue personal and professional interaction over a cup of coffee or a bottle of beer in 700 Coffee & Lounge.
