Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

marți, 12 aprilie 2011

#TiMo #4, the 4th edition of the Timisoara Mobile Development Group meeting comes to you with some novelties: - we'll relocate our meetup place together with the 700 Coffee and Lounge in the C building of the Timisoara Business Center - we are pretty sure you'll manage finding it ;) - we'll have the first mobile product launch as part of a #TiMo event: RedEye by Net Interaction - there will be a presentation of two different apps (Ekoroad mobile and CTSMob) parts of a car-sharing platform developed by AMVTEK, one of the first Timisoara companies devolping in the mobile field - we'll reserve some time for informal discussions regarding the participants'/group members' support needs and future group activities ideas We are looking forward to meet you again!
