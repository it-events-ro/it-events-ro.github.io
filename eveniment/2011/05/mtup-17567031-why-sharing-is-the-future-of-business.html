Why sharing is the future of business?

miercuri, 25 mai 2011

A videoconference right here at Bucharest Hubb with Lisa Gansky , the author of The Mesh , TED speaker, ex Impact Junkie at Mozilla, Co-Founder of Ofoto (Kodak Digital Services), Investor and Advisor in several online businesses.

Content & Schedule of the event:

1. 18.30 - 18.45 - Welcoming of the attendees/Check-in

2. 18.45 - 19.00 - Presentation of the book, The Mesh , translated and published in Romania at Publica, by Publica representatives, Bogdan Ungureanu and Catalin Muraru.

3. 19.00 -19.30 - Videoconference and Q&A with Lisa Gansky about why sharing is the future of business.
