Startup Lessons Learn Timisoara Simulcast 2011

luni, 23 mai 2011

Timisoara Software Business Incubator and Timisoara Agile Software Meetup Group are inviting you (and of couse, your friends interested) to join together to watch a simulcast of the Startup Lesson Learned Conference at Timisoara City Business Center, 10th C. Brediceanu Str, Building C, Ground Floor starting at 7.00 PM.

Startup Lessons Learned 2011 is the sequel to last year's inaugural event also simulcasted locally in Timisoara Software Business Incubator, which brought together in San Francisco nearly 400 entrepreneurs and executives interested in building and supporting lean startups.

The goal for this event is to give practitioners and students of the lean startup methodology the opportunity to hear insights from leaders in embracing and deploying the core principles of the lean startup methodology. The day-long event will feature a mix of panels and talks focused on the key challenges and issues that technical and market-facing people at startups need to understand in order to succeed in building successful lean startups.
