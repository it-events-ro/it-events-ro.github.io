Lessons learned doing Agile

vineri, 4 martie 2011

PLEASE READ THE WHOLE DESCRIPTION IN ORDER TO FIND INFO ON EVENT REGISTRATION! Timisoara Agile Software Meetup Group is a gathering of Agile practitioners (and practitioners-to-be) established jointly by Timisoara Software Business Incubator and Agile Works Romania, less then 18 months ago aiming to promote Agile attitudes, practices and business approaches. In the meantime the group grew spectacularly to more than 150 registered members, organized 13 events of various kinds. We may say that there is a history of agile adoption and practice behind most of the group's members so this week is the time to reveal to each other what this adventure brought to us. This meetup is all about the lessons that we've learned by applying the Agile state of mind each day of our (professional) lives. Flavius Stef and Lucian Daniliuc will start the meetup by sharing valuable experiences aquired during the past years, but everyone is encouraged to share their experiences. As usual, the Timisoara Software Business Incubator will host this meetup, so participation is free of charge. Just come, have a coffee or tea and share. However, donations to cover the beverage costs are welcomed. Please be aware that the number of available places is limited to 26, in order to ensure a good sharing and learning experience. Be quick and please register on the group's page http://www.meetup.com/The-Timisoara-Agile-Software-Meetup-Group/. THE ONLY VALID REGISTRATION WILL BE THOSE DONE ON meetup.com.
