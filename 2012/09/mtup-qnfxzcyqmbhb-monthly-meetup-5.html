Monthly Meetup #5

miercuri, 5 septembrie 2012

Hi all,

UPDATE : Last week, Oana, Eniko, Jari and me met to talk about what type of exercises we could do. We had a very short and productive meeting, so I'm happy to say that we came up with a nice plan for the next TDT meetup :).

We'll split in small groups (3-4 people) and each team will have to do the exercise, and then present their results to the others. This way people will be able to question your ideas, and you'll be able to defend them as part of the final debriefing. For me, this is a very important part of the learning process!

So, hope to see lots of you on Wednesday!

Thanks,
