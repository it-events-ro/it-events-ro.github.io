Timisoara Mobile Development Group Meetup

luni, 10 septembrie 2012

We are inviting you for a new season opening for TiMo, after a period that definitely was not a calm holiday for the group (just remember we convened in August in the numbers of 60-70 for talking node.js

and mobile development in cloud).

What we already know is that on September 10th we'll have in the program a presentation by Catalin Gheorghiu on Windows Phone 8.

http://photos2.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/e/6/d/4/event_154259092.jpegAlso at the September event, we'll launch and present what will be TiMo's series of (micro-) trainings on various topics under the wider iOS development theme. The training series is prepared and will be delivered by <strong style="text-align : justify"> Gil Eluard , Technical Lead at the Timisoara office of <strong style="text-align : justify"> Three Pillar Global, Inc .

The last presentation of the day will remind participants about holidays: Oana and Arthur Csertus and Valentin Keleti of CobaltSign recently launched Ready Set Holiday! , "a simple yet beautiful app that tells you how many weeks and days you have left until that long awaited summer vacation starts", as app's website explains.

Keep an eye on group's page while we'll most probably add more content in the meantime. Also, if you feel that you have something worth presenting just let us know. We'll be delighted to include you on the list of speakers.
