First steps towards getting your WebDriver's license

Ever dreamed of jumping into Test Automation, but weren't sure of what was going to happen when you opened Pandora's little (automated) box?

We, the guys at Cloud Troopers Intl., would like to be your Test Automation “MythBusters” and, also, plant that much needed curiosity seed for this amazing aspect of SQA!

How?

Well, we're not going to host some fancy workshop labeled “how it's done”, but more like “how we do it” and we'd be delighted if you'd follow up on that by showing us how you do it in your cooky, geeky automation crew.

What's the agenda?

Main topics we'll be storming through in 2-2.5 hrs:

Why do we need Test Automation? Short introduction to Selenium IDE and WebDriver Identifying objects in Selenium IDE scripts on a real SUT Converting IDE to WebDriver WebDriver scripts on a real SUT (using Maven, TestNG, Java and Eclipse) Script versioning in Git Continuous integration in Jenkins Conclusions, Tips & Tricks we learnt the hard way. Q&A Who should attend?

New to automation? This is just the place to catch a glimpse at a possible workflow for automating your test cases. And, of course, all you seasoned automators out there, we look forward to meeting and sharing knowledge. Don't be shy!
