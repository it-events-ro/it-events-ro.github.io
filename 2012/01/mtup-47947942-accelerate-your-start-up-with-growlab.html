ACCELERATE YOUR START-UP with GROWLAB

joi, 26 ianuarie 2012

Startupers out there, if you're in Bucharest on January 26th, this is a must-attend event!

One of North America’s top angel investors, Boris Wertz is hosting an event in the effort to get to know the Bucharest start-up community and meet its many talented entrepreneurs and engineers.

Time has been reserved for 20 entrepreneurs to give a 1-minute pitch and receive invaluable feedback. If you aren’t among the selected 20 start-ups, no worries! There will be plenty of networking time to meet Boris afterwards.

Boris Wertz </a>is the Founder & CEO at <a href="http://www.wmediaventures.com/">Wmedia ventures and venture partner at Munich based Acton </a><a href="http://www.actoncapital.de/">Capital </a><a href="http://www.actoncapital.de/">Partners . His portfolio encompasses over 25 early-stage consumer internet and mobile companies. Most recently he co-founded </a><a href="http://www.growlab.ca/">GrowLab .

Grow Lab is one of the top start-up accelerators that supports the world's best entrepreneurs through seed funding, office space, as well as intensive mentorship and programming in Vancouver and Silicon Valley. Romanian start-up, Sunnytrail, just recently graduated from GrowLab’s 2011 Fall Program.

Event outline
- Brief presentation about GrowLab
- Start-up pitches
- Drinks and networking
