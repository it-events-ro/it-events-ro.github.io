What exactly is a Business Model and how do I create a great (new) one?

miercuri, 17 octombrie 2012

TechCrunch quote: "Everyone in the tech world talks about business models....The reality is that a business model is like the old saying about teenage sex: everyone talks about it all the time; everyone boasts about how well he or she is doing it; everyone thinks everyone else is doing it; almost no one really is..."
Let's talk about what a business model is and how to create a great (new) business model....in case we are quizzed by the investors...

Our special guest is Mr. Szasz Orlando, owner of Renania .

Renania is the largest Romanian company operating in work security and health.

Our host tonight is Mr. Remus Toma, Product Manager at SociaLook .

SociaLook is a start-up company, developing a social contact discovery tool that enables entrepreneurs and investors to find relevant persons in a company, or a location.
