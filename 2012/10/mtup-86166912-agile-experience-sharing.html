Agile Experience sharing

miercuri, 17 octombrie 2012

Dear Agile enthusiasts!

Due to the success of the first Agile Meetup it is time to organize another exciting Agile meeting.
This meeting, like the first one, is meant for those business stakeholders, management and development teams, managers, programmers, testers who want to learn how to improve their work and their organizations. The objective of this meeting is to share our experience and knowledge. It’s a community driven event, which encourages the participants to share, to learn from others and to ask. You’ll find in this event tools and techniques for continuous improvement in the Agile environment.

This meeting's agenda:
• Meaning of Story Points in the creation of the Release Plan by Barabás László.
• Experience sharing in Transition from „Ad-hoc” development to Agile.
• Questions and Discussion.
• Setting up the topic for the next meeting.

The Agile meeting will take place on 17th October, at Zanza Café & Lounge, Piața Trandafirilor nr. 52 ( http://www.facebook.com/zanzacafelounge ), from 17:30.

Please come in and take part on it and if you like share with us your Agile experiences. Agile local communities are based on your voluntary participation.

Please accept our invitation and indicate your participation at the following communication platform: http://www.meetup.com/The-Targu-Mures-Agile-Software-Meetup-Group/ .

We are waiting for you!

Best regards,
Barabás László

Notes:
The community meetups are free of charge.
