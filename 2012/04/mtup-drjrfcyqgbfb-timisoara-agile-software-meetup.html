Timisoara Agile Software Meetup

marți, 10 aprilie 2012

The first part of the April meetup will be dedicated to the topic of Early Agile Education .

We'll talk about the lessons that could be learned following two recent projects that exposed Timisoara high-school and university students to Agile principles and practices.

5 of the group's members (Dan Popescu, Flavius Stef, Lucian Daniliuc, Radu Ticiu, Victor Tomescu) were involved in Spring IT , a " Scoala Altfel " project initiated by Timisoara Software Business Incubator and Liceul de Informatica "Grigore Moisil" Timisoara, and in the AIESEC University's IT School .

This two projects brought the Agile concepts closer to about 100 young future IT specialists aged 15-21. What was in all this as a basis for a systematic approach that could equip youth with a powerful tool usable both in one's profession but also in one's personal life. Does it make sense to invest by supporting customized Agile trainings for this future employees? Are we ready to involve ourselves and/or companies in such an educational project?

The rest of the meetup will be treated free style, as we are in kind of an Easter Holiday status, with ad-hoc, flash-talk and Q&A.
