Lean Startup Conference 2012 Timisoara Simulcast

luni, 3 decembrie 2012

On December 3rd, the biggest names in the lean startup global movement and a bunch of well known entrepreneurs will gather for The Lean Startup Conference in San Francisco.

For the third year in a row, Timisoara is listed as an official simulcast location. We will stream the entire single-track event in the tech & entrepreneurship ecosystem-friendly spaces of City Business Centre .

Join us to watch, learn and discuss how to be more innovative, achieve faster real success and, generally, be more effective.

The event starts Monday evening at 19:00 and ends at about 03:15, early Tuesday.

Whether you’re new to Lean Startup methods or have been putting these practices to use in your organization, this event promises to educate and inform. The Lean Startup Conference brings together innovators from both startups and Fortune 500 companies, government agencies and non-profits, all keen to share lessons on building effective lean organizations. More than 30 entrepreneurs and experts will speak about their first-hand experiences, with keynotes including:


Marc Andreessen, co-founder and general partner at Andreessen Horowitz

Steve Blank, serial entrepreneur, author, lecturer at Stanford

Beth Comstock, chief marketing officer at GE

Eric Ries, author of The Lean Startup

Drew Houston - CEO of Dropbox

Ash Maurya - author of Running Lean: How to Iterate from Plan A to a plan that works

While the knowledge about this new approach in the field of entrepreneurship has gone global, startup ecosystems are always local. Join us at the Mezzanine of the City Business Centre's D Building on Monday December 3rd at 17:00 to meet other Timisoara entrepreneurs interested in creating and managing successful startups.

The organizers will arrange for a friendly atmosphere, with the best assortment of snacks and cookies, cold and hot beverages, so you will be able to refresh during the streaming breaks.

To learn more about the conference including the detailed program and the speakers line-up, visit The Lean Startup Conference at http://leanstartup.co/ .

For fully register your participation and to buy tickets, please visit the simulcast's page on Eventbrite .

Read about the Lean Startup movement and principles at http://theleanstartup.com/ .
