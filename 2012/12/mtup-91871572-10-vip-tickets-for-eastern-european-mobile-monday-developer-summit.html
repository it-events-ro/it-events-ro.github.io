10 VIP Tickets for Eastern European Mobile Monday Developer Summit

luni, 10 decembrie 2012

This year's edition of Eastern European Mobile Monday Developer Summit will take place Dec 10th, in Budapest, offering a great opportunity to those interested to meet professionals from public and private sectors,  and young and talented developers and entrepreneurs in the field of Mobile Communication from the CEE region. The event's agenda includes a competition of promising apps developed in the region.

Dan Romescu , our fellow group member, involved in the organization of the

event, was kind enough to secure 10 VIP tickets for Timisoara Mobile Development Group members.

Please let us know via a comment bellow if you are interested to be part of the Timisoara delegation to attend the event and we'll send you the proper promotion code to be used on event's registration webpage .

The only rule that applies is "First came, First served."

We'll bring more details related to the logistics of the participation via e-mail messages addressed to those registered.

IMPORTANT NOTE: we may be able to obtain more free VIP tickets, so those interested should get in contact with us via a group message.
