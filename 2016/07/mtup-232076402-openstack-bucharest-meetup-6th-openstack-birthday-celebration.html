OpenStack Bucharest Meetup, 6th OpenStack Birthday Celebration

joi, 14 iulie 2016

As every year we celebrate the OpenStack Birthday, 6th anniversary together with our second Meetup OpenStack Bucharest Group meeting.  And we are proud to receive the OpenStack Foundation support for this event.

Since we`d like to have a regular OpenStack meetups in Bucharest and we hope to build a strong community around OpenStack, so please help us spread the word and extend this invitation to any and all OpenStack enthusiasts out there, developers and users alike.

We have 2 interesting topics in store for you, ranging from OpenStack and not to forget the Birthday Celebration party from the end of event.

Full Agenda:

18:30 - 18:40 Welcoming the guests. Short networking session before the event starts.

18:40 - 19:00 OpenStack 6th Celebration review - Daniel Marin OpenStack Bucharest Group.

19:00 - 19:40 "What’s new in Mitaka and beyond? OpenStack security in Windows Server 2016.” by Iulia Toader - Cloud Engineer & OpenStack contributor from Cloudbase Solutions.

19:40 - 20:20 Live Demo - AIC + Gemini OpenCloud Solution (Taiwan) - how powerful is a cloud based on OpenStack by Eduard Bulai DevOps Skylab Startup Development.

20:20 - 20:30 Special prize from OpenStack Foundation : Free Exam Code for Certified OpenStack Administrator http://www.openstack.org/coa

Winner will be decided by lucky draw of all attendees business cards (please bring one don`t forget!)

20:30 - 21:30

6th Birthday Celebration of OpenStack party - with pizza and soft drinks.

See you all at the event!
