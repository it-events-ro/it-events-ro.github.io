TM-42-TDT - How it was at the European Testing Conference

joi, 31 martie 2016

This time we will talk about our experience while attending the European Testing Conference.

An overview of the topics will be made and we will share with you information regarding the presentations we liked.

Other people that attended the conference are also invited to talk about their experience with us.

There will be also a discussion about conferences, meetups, trainings and any other testing events that you know or you have attended and you want to share information about them.
