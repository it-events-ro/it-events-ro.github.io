marți, 1 martie 2016

Angular.js past or future?

Viorel Smeu, will share his expertise with using it for building live applications and what his plans are for the local community of angular.js developers.

We will discuss some of AngularJS key points, what sets it apart from other frameworks and why it's fun to work with it. Besides that, Viorel will share some tutorials and links that helped him along the way and if there are angular developers also some tips and tricks from his personal experience.

He will also share his motivation for wanting a local angular.js community in Brasov and why it's important to have such communities.
