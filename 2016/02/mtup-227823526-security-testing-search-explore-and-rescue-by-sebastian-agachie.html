Security Testing: Search, Explore and Rescue by Sebastian Agachie

miercuri, 24 februarie 2016

Sebastian believes that curiosity, motivation and the right attitude are the key ingredients of achieving everything you want. Although in his career Sebastian covered almost every position in IT, from networking to development, he always had an incredible passion for security.


His courage and motivation were the key fundamental aspects of the new security department that was created in Centric Romania, where he currently works as an Ethical Hacker and Security Discipline Coordinator. Aside from coordinating and keeping the security department up to date, he is driving through security operations such as: pen tests, security advises and awareness for developers/testers/managers throw a various series processes.
