PM Hangout: PMI Cluj Kick-off for 2016

joi, 11 februarie 2016

After the winter break, let's get together to share our plans and expectations for PMI Cluj in 2016.

Everyone is welcome! Please come to share ideas and expectations even if you feel you will not be involved in organizing PMI activities in 2016. As long as you think you will attend at least one PMI Meeting, Meetup or Hangout then your presence is valuable. :)

Topics I thought to cover in our open discussion:
- Getting to know each other
- See would like to get involved in 2016, maybe assign areas
- Consolidating the PM/PMI community in Cluj with PMI events and networking
- Sharing PMI Bucharest actions and see if and where we want to align and / or contribute - I will speak with Teodor Darabaneanu, PMI Romania, before our meeting and ask him to share their plans from 2016 onwards.
- Next events in Cluj: anyone who wants to be a host and / or propose topics of interest

See you on Thursday, February 11th!
