Microarchitectures for Small Android Applications & Post-MWC Round Table

luni, 29 februarie 2016

Madalin Gageanu , a frequent presenter at Timisoara Mobile Development Group strikes again! This time he will come with a presentation of the approach recommended for building a microarchitecture suitable for small to medium sized Android applications with emphasis on API consumption and the clear separation between UI and business logic.

In the second part of the evening you will have the opportunity to witness a round-table discussion exposing really fresh opinions on the 2016 edition of the Mobile World Congress that, due to the kind people of ARIES-TM will be significantly more visited by Timisoara professionals in the mobile field than the previous editions.

We'll let you know shortly while in Barcelona who will be the participants to the round table! For sure, we'll try to bring Daniel Giurea, president of ARIES-TM and coordinator of Romanian national pavilion at MWC!

Looking forward to meet you in City Business Centre!
