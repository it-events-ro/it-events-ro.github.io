UX Tactics in Corporate Environment & Building crowd-enabled growt in products

miercuri, 24 februarie 2016

UX Tactics in Corporate Environment


The banking industry is one of the most complex lines of business in which you can operate as UX designer. Hundreds of stakeholders, multiple projects at the same time and different levels of understanding about what UX is. I invite you to a dialogue about:


- User Experience as part of Customer Experience

- UX Designer as a Leader, UX as a Mindset

- Communicating the UX Vision within the Organization

About the speaker:


Andrei Mihai has more than 9 years in the digital industry for companies like Yahoo!, Raiffeisen Bank, National Geographic, several interactive agencies and start-ups. Also, Andrei is the founder and trainer for UXProject, first UX Academy in Romania.

Challenges in building crowd-enabled growth in products


Informal talks on how to create user engagement. A product presentation about how it works and ask opinions, feedback, share xde experience.
