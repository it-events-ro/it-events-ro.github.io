Let's talk about Automated Infrastructure!

miercuri, 18 mai 2016

VeLabs, the technology arm of Ve interactive, will be hosting on Wednesday, 18th of May, The Journey to Automated Infrastructure Meetup. The participation is free.

Over the past 9 months - Ve Interactive has been on a journey to automate our systems - we're now doing multiple releases per day of both our software and infrastructure.

See how by making our deployments simpler - we've been able to move from deploying once every 6 weeks - to deploying multiple times a day. Also see how by adopting Puppet, Vagrant and Azure - we've been able to do the same for our infrastructure.


Speakers:

Tom Harvey, Infrastructure Automation Engineer: "Tom maintains infrastructure and monitoring at Ve Interactive. When he's not travelling the world - he's probably working with Swift - building shiny things for iOS. Has been known to Automate Infrastructure from time to time".

Gabriel Nepomuceno, Head of Infrastructure Automation. "Gabriel is the guy that you run to when nothing is working. Historically a .Net developer has been dragged to the ops world by the desire to automate everything. A really laud Brazilian guy passionate about development and empower change trough code".

The event will happen in our office, Cluj-Business Center, Henri Barbusse 44-46, 4th floor, Cluj-Napoca.

Please register your participation.
