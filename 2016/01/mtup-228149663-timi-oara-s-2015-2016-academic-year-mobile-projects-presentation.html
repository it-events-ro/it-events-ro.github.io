Timișoara's 2015-2016 academic year mobile projects presentation

luni, 25 ianuarie 2016

Update 1 - the list of the 17 projects to be pitched is here

Update 2 - the winning team of each of the three app categories (Mobile Games, Utility Apps, Lifestyle Apps) will be awarded with visitor tickets to Mobile World Congress 2016 , courtesy of ARIES-TM

Update 3 - an international jury to evaluate competing projects

Computers Department of the Automatics and Computers Faculty of Politehnica University of Timișoara , Timișoara Mobile Development Group , Timișoara Startup Hub and Banat IT Association invite all interested professionals and entrepreneurs in the mobile field (but definitely not limited to them) to join us in the City Business Centre venues, Monday, January 24th, starting 6:00 PM in a first edition of what we are aiming to set as a tradition in Timișoara's industry-academia relationship:

Timișoara Academic Mobile Projects
Presentation to Industry

The event will be a public showcase of the practical projects developed by the students BSc. cycle final year, in Information Technology, Faculty of Automation and Computers at Politehnica University of Timisoara, attending the Mobile Systems and Applications course lectured by our friend, Marius Marcu , Associate Professor with the involvement of his collaborators Alexandru Topîrceanu and <a>Cristian Babău</a> .

Out of ca. 40 projects developed during the first semester of[masked] academic year, the best ca. 20 have been selected to be presented publicly by the student developers. They are split in three categories: Mobile Games, Utility Apps, Lifestyle Apps. The apps will be organized in order to be presented on two different tracks (of course in two different rooms). All projects will be presented in 8-10 minutes timeslots. Specialist juries will evaluate the apps from the conceptual, technical and business/market potential points of view, generating the biggest part of the final grade the student will get.

The presentations will be followed by a networking event, facilitating the contact of the presenting students with the people in the audience.

As an attendance member, you will be able to understand the overall professional level of the generation graduating this year, to assist to a dynamic program including, for sure, interesting projects to associate with in an adviser, promoter, business partner, or, why not, investor capacity. And yes, we are not hiding the possibility that this event can get your company some recruitment leads. :)

Please let us know via a comment on this event announcement if you would be interested to candidate to participate as a member in one of the three juries.

Just to give you an idea about the relevance of the projects presented, check the winning ones in 2014 ( 50-100k downloads ) and 2015 (5-10k downloads).


Looking forward to meet you at this promising event!
