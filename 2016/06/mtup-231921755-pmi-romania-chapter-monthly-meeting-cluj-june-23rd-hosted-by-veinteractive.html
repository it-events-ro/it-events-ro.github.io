PMI Romania Chapter Monthly Meeting - Cluj June 23rd - hosted by VeInteractive

joi, 23 iunie 2016

PMI Romania Chapter has the pleasure of inviting you to a new Monthly Meeting in Cluj, on June 23rd 2016, hosted by VeInteractive.
Meeting Topic: Roadmaps and Stakeholders Management
Agenda
18:15 – 18:45 Registration, Coffee & Drinks
18:45 – 19:00 PMI Presentation – Tips and Tricks for PMP Certification – Cristina Has
19:00 –19:10 Host company presentation: VeInteractive – Cristian Cazan
19:10 – 19:25 Presentation Liaising Projects & Products – Alex Ivan
Roadmaps can create a continuity of purpose, and help both project and product managers in their day-to-day work. On one hand roadmaps can represent a high-level, strategic plan while on the other, they can be a tactical, detail-oriented tool. In this session we will cover the different types of roadmaps, their benefits, and how to align the different types of stakeholders.
19:25 – 19:40 Presentation Stakeholders Management – Delora Balomirean
What matters the most when dealing with all parties involved into a project, is it possible to keep everyone happy?
19:40 – 20:00 Break / Snacks / Networking
20:00 – 20:45 Panel Discussion Roadmaps and Stakeholders Management
20:45 – Networking

Panel Members:
- Cristina Has, PMP – Moderator – Cristina is a Product Manager and has over 5 years experience managing projects, programs and portfolios
- Alex Ivan – Alex is a digital geek and Computer Science graduate turned Product Manager. He enjoys being at the forefront of building products that bring value to humans and businesses using them and the company creating them alike. He has concepted, built, released and maintained products and web integrations in startup and corporate environments.
– Delora Balomirean – Delora is a Product Manager at VeInteractive. She has worked in the role of Business Analyst and Product Owner for over 7 years, on different IT projects for a number of companies coming from different business areas. Her domain experience covers Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Enterprise Resource Management (ERP). She is a certified PSPO and had worked both in an Agile and Waterfall environment.
– Vlad Botos – Vlad is a Product Manager at Wolters Kluwer GRC. With over 8 years of experience in roles like Business Analyst, Project Manager and Product Manager he enjoys building new products that deliver outstanding value to users in financial institutions.
– Ciprian Ciplea – Ciprian is Product Manager at AROBS Transilvania Software. Ciprian is an IT professional with 15 years background covering software/system/product development from different perspectives: initially as a developer, later as a project manager and currently as a product manager. Ciprian's focus, as a product manager for a car fleet management system, is to understand his stakeholders and market needs, define the product's added value for the final customers and transpose all this together with the technical teams into the real world fast.
