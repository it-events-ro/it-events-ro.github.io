marți, 7 iunie 2016

Designing the User Experience for a Local Startup - PARKING+PLUS (RO/EN, all levels)


This presentation is about the full journey as an User Experience Designer for PARKING+PLUS, a local startup that builds an innovative ecosystem of web and mobile applications, aiming to change the way we create and consume parking services.

The presentation is built around design case studies for multiple websites , mobile applications and marketing materials.

About the speakers:


Ovidiu Berdila - UX Designer @ Qiagen. UX Designer and a seasoned Software Developer, motivated to bring more joy to the world by helping people understand and use technology with ease.

Cristian Ureche - Managing Director @ PARKING+PLUS | Smart Access Box. Deeply involved in PARKING+PLUS. Accountable for Vision, Strategy & Execution. Interested in smart cities initiatives.
