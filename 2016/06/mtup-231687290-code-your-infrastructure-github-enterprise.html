Code your Infrastructure / GitHub Enterprise

miercuri, 29 iunie 2016

Join us at our first meetup to meet other DevOps practitioners, enthusiasts and learners.

Agenda :

18:30 - 19:00 Welcome coffee and networking

19:00 - 19:50 "Code your Infrastructure" by Liviu Damian - Gone are the days when we manually configure our servers or different infrastructure components. Today's tools, combined with the plethora of Cloud Infrastructure providers allows us to achieve an amazing level flexibility and scalability. In this presentation we will show how Ansible, Packer and Terraform can be used to implement Infrastructure as Code and Immutable Infrastructure paradigms. This presentation will feature a demo session.

19:50 - 20:10 Coffee break and networking

20:10 - 21:00 "GitHub Enterprise" by Alexandru Sabau - Everyone knows and loves (or hates) Github. Their base offering fits the requirements of almost every startup or company. But what can you do when your company has to meet tighter security policies and regulations, such as PCI-DSS compliance? We will show you the benefits of GitHub Enterprise and how to implement it in your company.

21:00 - 21:30 Beer and Networking

All backgrounds and levels of experience are welcome :-)
